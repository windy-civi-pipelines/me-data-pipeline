9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 458, L.D. 1072, “An Act to Amend the
10 Laws Governing the Land for Maine's Future Program and to Authorize the Use of Options
11 to Purchase at Agricultural Value”
12 Amend the bill in Part A in section 11 in §6209 in subsection 6 in the 2nd line (page 4,

14 State'
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment clarifies that fee interests in land acquired under the Land for Maine's
1199 Future Program in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, chapter 353 and held by the State
2200 may not be sold or used for purposes other than those stated in chapter 353, unless approved
2211 by a 2/3 majority of the Legislature.
