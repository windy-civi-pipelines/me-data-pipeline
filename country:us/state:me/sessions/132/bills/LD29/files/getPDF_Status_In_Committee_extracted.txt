4 STATE OF MAINE
5 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
6 132ND LEGISLATURE
7 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
8 HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to S.P. 46, L.D.
9 29, “Resolve, to Require the Department of Transportation to Implement the
10 Recommendations of the Lower Road Rail Use Advisory Council”
11 Amend the amendment by inserting after section 2 the following:
12 'Sec. 3. Limitation on removal of tracks. Resolved: That the Department of
13 Transportation may not remove any portion of the railroad tracks described in section 1 or
14 2 for which there is a pending request for proposals application from a train company or
15 any other commercial enterprise proposing to use that portion of the railroad tracks.
16 Sec. 4. Use of state funding or state bonds prohibited. Resolved: That the
17 Department of Transportation may not use funding provided by the State or funding
18 obtained through the sale of bonds issued by the State for the purposes of removing railroad
19 tracks pursuant to section 1 or 2.'
20 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
21 section number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A." The amendment prohibits the
2244 Department of Transportation from removing any portion of railroad track for which there
2255 is a pending request for proposals application from a train company or any other
2266 commercial enterprise proposing to use that portion of the railroad tracks. The amendment
2277 also prohibits the department from using any funding provided by the State or funding
2288 obtained through the sale of bonds issued by the State for the purposes of removing the
2299 railroad tracks that the department is directed to remove under the resolve and committee
3300 amendment.
