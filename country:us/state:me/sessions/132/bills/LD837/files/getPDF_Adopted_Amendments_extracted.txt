5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 370, L.D. 837, “An Act to Require the
10 Office of the Public Advocate to Implement the State's Climate Action Plan”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Require Positions Taken by the Public Advocate to Be Consistent with the
13 Cost-effective Implementation of the State's Greenhouse Gas Emissions Reductions
14 Obligations'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 35-A MRSA §1702, sub-§10, as enacted by PL 2011, c. 79, §2, is amended
18 to read:
19 10. Independent representation of the interests of the using and consuming
20 public. The Public Advocate, when taking a position on any matter in any proceeding or
21 forum pursuant to the Public Advocate's authority under this chapter, shall exercise
22 independent judgment to ensure the position:
23 A. Is consistent with the priorities established in section 1702‑A and the requirements
24 of this chapter; and
25 B. Promotes stabilization and lowering of prices paid by those members of the using
26 and consuming public whose interests the Public Advocate is representing.; and
27 C. Is consistent with the cost-effective implementation for ratepayers of the
28 greenhouse gas emissions reductions obligations established in Title 38, section
29 576-A.'
30 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
31 number to read consecutively.
