132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1134
S.P. 461 In Senate, March 18, 2025
Using Female Facilities
Reference to the Committee on Judiciary suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BERNARD of Aroostook.
Cosponsored by Representative SMITH of Palermo and
Senators: GUERIN of Penobscot, HAGGAN of Penobscot, Representatives: BAGSHAW of
Windham, LYMAN of Livermore Falls.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §14 is enacted to read:
3 §14. Biological males' participation in competitive female athletic programs and use
4 of facilities
5 A school administrative unit or an elementary school, secondary school or
6 postsecondary educational institution in the State that receives any state funding may not
7 allow a person whose biological sex assigned at birth is male to participate in a competitive
8 athletic program or activity that is designated for females or to use a facility that has been
9 designated for use solely by females.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill prohibits a school administrative unit or an elementary school, secondary
1122 school or postsecondary educational institution in the State that receives any state funding
1133 from allowing a person whose biological sex assigned at birth is male to participate in a
1144 competitive athletic program or activity that is designated for females or to use a facility
1155 that has been designated for use solely by females.
