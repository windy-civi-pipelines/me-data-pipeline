14 State or Local Financial Assistance and to Ensure Municipal Compliance with
15 Federal Immigration Laws'

17 out the following: "United States citizenship" and inserting the following: 'lawful
18 presence'
19 Amend the bill in section 1 in §60 in the first indented paragraph in the 2nd line (page
20 1, line 5 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "a citizen of" and inserting the following:
21 'lawfully present in'
22 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2.
23 Amend the bill in section 4 in §2008 in the headnote (page 1, line 29 in L.D.) by striking
24 out the following: "United States citizenship" and inserting the following: 'lawful
25 presence'
26 Amend the bill in section 4 in §2008 in the first indented paragraph in the 2nd line
27 (page 1, line 31 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "a citizen of" and inserting the
28 following: 'lawfully present in'
29 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
30 number to read consecutively.
