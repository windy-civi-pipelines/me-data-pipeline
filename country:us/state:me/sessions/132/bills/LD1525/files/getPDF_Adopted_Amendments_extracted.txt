6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1010, L.D. 1525, “An Act to Promote
10 Firewood Banks in Maine”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §1849, sub-§1, ¶B is enacted to read:
14 B. Upon written request from a firewood bank, the bureau may donate timber for use
15 as firewood to the firewood bank if the following conditions are met:
16 (1) The director determines that sufficient funds are available in the Public
17 Reserved Lands Management Fund in subsection 2;
18 (2) Any harvest and trucking fees are paid for or reimbursed by the firewood bank;
19 and
20 (3) All contractual requirements of the harvest operation from which timber is
21 donated are met.
22 For purposes of this paragraph, "firewood bank" means an organization that purchases
23 or accepts donations of firewood and makes available firewood free of charge to
24 individuals and families in need of heating assistance.
25 Sec. 2. Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of
26 Forestry to promote firewood banks. The Department of Agriculture, Conservation
27 and Forestry, Bureau of Forestry shall promote the development and public awareness of
28 firewood banks in this State. For purposes of this section, "firewood bank" has the same
29 meaning as in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 1849, subsection 1, paragraph
30 B. The bureau shall develop and dispense informational resources to promote the
31 successful operation of firewood banks in this State. In fulfilling its obligations under this
32 section, the bureau may collaborate with the University of Maine System. The bureau
33 shall:

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1010, L.D. 1525
1 1. Provide information to the public for the purpose of increasing awareness of the
2 resources available at firewood banks and the charitable efforts needed to maintain
3 firewood banks in this State; and
4 2. Develop and maintain on the bureau's publicly accessible website a list of all
5 firewood banks operating in the State.'
6 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
7 number to read consecutively.
8 SUMMARY
99 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment authorizes the Department of
1100 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands to donate timber for
1111 use as firewood to a firewood bank if certain conditions are met.
12 The amendment also directs the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry,
13 Bureau of Forestry to promote the development and public awareness of firewood banks in
14 this State. In addition, the amendment directs the bureau to develop and dispense
15 informational resources to promote the successful operation of firewood banks in this State.
16 In fulfilling its obligations, the bureau may collaborate with the University of Maine
17 System.
