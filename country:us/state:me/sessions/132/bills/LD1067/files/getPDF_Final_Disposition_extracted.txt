16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, prohibits a mobile
1188 home owners' association from contacting a mobile home owner in the mobile home park
1199 more than once about the association purchasing the mobile home park after being told by
2200 the owner not to make contact again.
