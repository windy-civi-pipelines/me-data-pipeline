2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
4 Reproduced and distributed under the direction of the Clerk of the House.
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 884, L.D. 1361, “An Act to Require
10 Insurance Coverage for Covered Dental Services Provided by Licensed Dental Hygienists
11 and to Authorize Licensed Dental Hygienists to Bill Commercial Dental Insurance”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Require Insurance Coverage for Covered Dental Services Provided by
14 Licensed Dental Hygienists'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 24 MRSA §2317-B, sub-§12-E, as enacted by PL 2009, c. 307, §1 and
18 affected by §6, is amended to read:
19 12-E. Title 24-A, sections 2765 and, 2847-Q and 4257. Coverage for services
20 provided by independent practice dental hygienists or dental hygienists, Title 24‑A,
21 sections 2765 and, 2847‑Q and 4257;
22 Sec. 2. 24-A MRSA §2765, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §11, is further amended
23 by amending the section headnote to read:
24 §2765. Coverage for services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or
25 dental hygienist
26 Sec. 3. 24-A MRSA §2765, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §11, is further
27 amended to read:
28 1. Services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.
29 An insurer that issues individual dental insurance or health insurance that includes coverage
30 for dental services shall provide coverage for dental services performed by an independent
31 practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist licensed under Title 32, chapter 143 when those
32 services are covered services under the contract and when they are within the lawful scope
33 of practice of the independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.

1 Sec. 4. 24-A MRSA §2847-Q, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §13, is further
2 amended by amending the section headnote to read:
3 §2847-Q. Coverage for services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or
4 dental hygienist
5 Sec. 5. 24-A MRSA §2847-Q, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §13, is
6 further amended to read:
7 1. Services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.
8 An insurer that issues group dental insurance or health insurance that includes coverage for
9 dental services shall provide coverage for dental services performed by an independent
10 practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist licensed under Title 32, chapter 143 when those
11 services are covered services under the contract and when they are within the lawful scope
12 of practice of the independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.
13 Sec. 6. 24-A MRSA §4257, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §15, is further amended
14 by amending the section headnote to read:
15 §4257. Coverage for services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or
16 dental hygienist
17 Sec. 7. 24-A MRSA §4257, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2015, c. 429, §15, is further
18 amended to read:
19 1. Services provided by independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.
20 All individual and group health maintenance organization contracts that include coverage
21 for dental services shall provide coverage for dental services performed by an independent
22 practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist licensed under Title 32, chapter 143 when those
23 services are covered services under the contract and when they are within the lawful scope
24 of practice of the independent practice dental hygienist or dental hygienist.'
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
26 number to read consecutively.
27 SUMMARY
2288 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires health
2299 insurance carriers to reimburse dental hygienists for covered services that are within their
3300 scope of practice if those same services would be reimbursed if provided by a dentist.
