132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1409
H.P. 931 House of Representatives, April 1, 2025
Reference to the Committee on Health and Human Services suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative GRIFFIN of Levant.
Cosponsored by Representatives: BAGSHAW of Windham, CIMINO of Bridgton, COLLINS
of Sidney, JAVNER of Chester.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §4005-G, sub-§1, as enacted by PL 2017, c. 411, §11, is amended
3 to read:
4 1. Kinship preference. Except as provided in subsections 3, 5 and, 6 and 7, in the
5 residential placement of a child, the department shall give preference to an adult relative
6 over a nonrelated caregiver when determining placement for a child, as long as the adult
7 relative meets all relevant state child protection standards.
8 Sec. 2. 22 MRSA §4005-G, sub-§7 is enacted to read:
9 7. Foster parent rights. The department is required to consider a foster parent who
10 is not a relative and who has had a child placed in that foster parent's home for a continuous
11 period of one year or more to have the same rights as an adult relative of the child.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill establishes that a foster parent who has had a child in that foster parent's home
1144 for a continuous period of one year or more has the same rights as an adult relative of the
1155 child.
