1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 34-B MRSA §3012 is enacted to read:
3 §3012. Certified recovery residences eligible for Bridging Rental Assistance Program
4 1. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the
5 following terms have the following meanings.
6 A. "Certified recovery residence" means a residence that is certified pursuant to the
7 requirements established in Title 5, section 20005, subsection 22.
8 B. "Program" means the Bridging Rental Assistance Program established in section
9 3011 and administered by the department.
10 2. Eligibility of certified recovery residences. Notwithstanding any provision of law
11 to the contrary, a certified recovery residence is eligible to accept program housing
12 vouchers, as long as the certified recovery residence meets all applicable standards and
13 conditions established by the department for the program.
14 3. Eligibility of residents. An individual residing in a certified recovery residence is
15 eligible to apply for a program housing voucher, as long as the individual meets all
16 applicable standards and conditions established by the department for the program.
17 Residency in a certified recovery residence may not be construed as a barrier to application
18 or receipt of such a voucher.
19 4. Guidance. The department shall provide guidance to certified recovery residences
20 and housing assistance programs to ensure consistent application of this section, including
21 public education efforts to inform residents of certified recovery residences and providers
22 in certified recovery residences of their rights and responsibilities under this section.
23 5. Rulemaking. The department shall adopt or amend rules as necessary to implement
24 the provisions of this section. Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine
25 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
26 SUMMARY
2277 This bill provides that certified recovery residences that are certified pursuant to the
2288 requirements established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 20005, subsection
2299 22 are eligible to accept housing vouchers under the Bridging Rental Assistance Program
3300 as long as they meet all applicable program standards and conditions. It also provides that
3311 individuals residing in certified recovery residences are eligible to apply for Bridging
3322 Rental Assistance Program housing vouchers as long as they meet all applicable program
3333 standards and conditions.
