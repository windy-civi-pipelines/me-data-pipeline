Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 590
S.P. 274 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Data from State Agencies in the Legislative Process
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 19, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on State and Local Government pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator TALBOT ROSS of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 3 MRSA §163-A, sub-§7, as amended by PL 2015, c. 102, §6, is further
3 amended to read:
4 7. Committee assistance. To provide research, analysis and bill drafting assistance
5 for joint standing or select committees, including, but not limited to, the joint standing
6 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over appropriations and financial affairs
7 and other legislative agencies, and to collect and assemble racial and other demographic
8 data from state agencies related to legislation before joint standing or select committees;
9 SUMMARY
1100 This bill requires nonpartisan legislative staff to collect and assemble racial and other
1111 demographic data from state agencies related to legislation before joint standing or select
1122 committees of the Legislature.
