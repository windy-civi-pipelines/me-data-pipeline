APPROVED CHAPTER
APRIL 14, 2025 15
BY GOVERNOR RESOLVES
STATE OF MAINE
_____
IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD
TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE
_____
S.P. 285 - L.D. 672
Salmon Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge between Buxton and Hollis renamed. Resolved: That the
Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3708 on Route 202, which crosses the
Saco River between the Town of Buxton and the Town of Hollis and is currently known as
the Salmon Falls Bridge, the Salmon Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge.
