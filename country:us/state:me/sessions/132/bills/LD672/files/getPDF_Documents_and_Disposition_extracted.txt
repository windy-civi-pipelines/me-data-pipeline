Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 672
S.P. 285 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Hollis the Salmon Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 20, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BAILEY of York.
Cosponsored by Representative BLIER of Buxton and
Senator: INGWERSEN of York, Representative: CARLOW of Buxton.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge between Buxton and Hollis renamed. Resolved: That the
2 Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3708 on Route 202, which crosses the
3 Saco River between the Town of Buxton and the Town of Hollis and is currently known as
4 the Salmon Falls Bridge, the Salmon Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3708 on
77 Route 202 between the Town of Buxton and the Town of Hollis the Salmon Falls Veterans
88 Memorial Bridge.
