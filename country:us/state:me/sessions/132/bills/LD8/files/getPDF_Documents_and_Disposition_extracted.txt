Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 8
S.P. 20 In Senate, January 8, 2025
Submitted by the Judicial Department pursuant to Joint Rule 204.
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on January 6, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Judiciary pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator CARNEY of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 4 MRSA c. 2 is enacted to read:
3 CHAPTER 2
4 GENERAL PROVISIONS
5 §71. Term start date for judicial officers
6 The term of appointment of a judicial officer appointed pursuant to the Constitution of
7 Maine, Article V, Part First, Section 8 begins on the date that the judicial officer is sworn
8 into office.
9 §72. Inapplicability of Title 5, section 6 qualification requirements
10 The provisions of Title 5, section 6 do not apply to judicial officers appointed pursuant
11 to the Constitution of Maine, Article V, Part First, Section 8.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill clarifies that the term of appointment of a judicial officer appointed pursuant
1144 to the Constitution of Maine begins on the date of swearing in and that the qualification
1155 requirements of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5, section 6 do not apply to such a judicial
1166 officer.
