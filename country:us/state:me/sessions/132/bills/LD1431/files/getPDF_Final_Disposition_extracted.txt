9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 940, L.D. 1431, “An Act to Implement
10 an Interstate Voter Registration Cross-check Program”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to End Information Sharing of Voter Registration Information Through a
13 Contract with a Nonprofit Corporation of Member States'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 21-A MRSA §161, sub-§2-B, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 398, Pt. UUUU,
17 §1 and affected by §7, is repealed.
18 Sec. 2. Terminate membership agreement with Electronic Registration
19 Information Center, Inc. By January 1, 2026, the Secretary of State shall, on behalf of
20 the State, terminate the State's membership agreement with the Electronic Registration
21 Information Center, Inc. that was entered into pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title
22 21-A, section 161, subsection 2-B.
23 Sec. 3. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
24 allocations are made.
25 SECRETARY OF STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
26 Bureau of Corporations, Elections and Commissions 0692
27 Initiative: Provides funding for one Management Analyst I position, one Public Service
28 Manager I position, software maintenance and licensing, technology and other costs to
29 implement and maintain a software system to perform voter registration cross-checks.
30 GENERAL FUND 2025-26 2026-27
31 POSITIONS - LEGISLATIVE COUNT 2.000 2.000
32 Personal Services $254,144 $263,293
33 All Other $1,677,588 $1,663,558

21 Sec. 4. Effective date. That section of this Act that repeals the Maine Revised
32 Statutes, Title 21-A, section 161, subsection 2-B takes effect January 1, 2026.'
4 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
5 number to read consecutively.
6 SUMMARY
77 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment repeals, on
88 January 1, 2026, the provision of law requiring the Secretary of State to contract with a
99 nonprofit corporation of member states for periodic sharing of voter registration
1100 information, including information on names and addresses of voters in member states, and
1111 directing the Secretary of State to periodically update the central voter registration system
1122 based on the information obtained. The amendment also directs the Secretary of State to
1133 terminate the State's membership agreement with the Electronic Registration Information
1144 Center, Inc. by January 1, 2026.
