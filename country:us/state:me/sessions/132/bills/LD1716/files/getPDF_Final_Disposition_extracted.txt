1 L.D. 1716
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 669, L.D. 1716, “An Act to Require
10 Family Life Education to Include a Human Growth and Development Curriculum”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in §1912 in subsection 2 in the last line (page 1, line 11 in
12 L.D.) by striking out the following: "3" and inserting the following: '5'
13 Amend the bill in section 1 in §1912 in subsection 3 in paragraph A in the first line
14 (page 1, line 13 in L.D.) by striking out the following: ", at least 3 minutes in duration,"
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in §1912 by striking out all of subsection 4 (page 1, lines
16 19 to 21 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
17 '4. Parental opt-in. A school shall provide the human growth and development
18 discussion only to a student whose parent or guardian has provided written permission for
19 the student to participate.'
20 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
21 number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment changes from grade 3 to grade 5 the grade by which schools that offer
2244 comprehensive family life education must offer a human growth and development
2255 discussion. It removes the requirement that the high-definition ultrasound video be at least
2266 3 minutes in duration. It also adds a parental opt-in requirement.
