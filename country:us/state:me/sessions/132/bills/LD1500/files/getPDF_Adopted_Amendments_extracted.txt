1 L.D. 1500
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 HOUSING AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 984, L.D. 1500, “An Act to Establish
10 the Maine Community Development Financial Institution Fund to Support Small
11 Businesses, Rural Economic Development and Affordable Housing”
12 Amend the bill by inserting before section 1 the following:
13 'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §135, as amended by PL 2021, c. 231, §1, is further amended by
14 enacting after the 7th paragraph a new paragraph to read:
15 The Treasurer of State may deposit, if funds are available, an amount not to exceed
16 $10,000,000 in each calendar year with community development financial institutions
17 authorized to do business in the State at a rate of return not more than 2% per year below
18 the rate of return otherwise obtainable had the funds been invested with other financial
19 institutions for a similar term, as determined by the Treasurer of State, for periods not to
20 exceed 2 years. For the purposes of this paragraph, "community development financial
21 institution" has the same meaning as in section 13056-K, subsection 1, paragraph A.'
22 Amend the bill in section 1 in §13056-K in subsection 2 by striking out all of the first
23 blocked paragraph (page 1, lines 28 to 32 in L.D.).
24 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
25 number to read consecutively.
26 SUMMARY
2277 This amendment reallocates a provision in the bill pertaining to the deposit of funds by
2288 the Treasurer of State from within the section of law establishing the Maine Community
2299 Development Financial Institution Program to the section of law that governs the
3300 limitations on the deposit of funds by the Treasurer of State.
3311 FISCAL NOTE REQUIRED
3322 (See attached)
