16 'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §8606-A, sub-§8 is enacted to read:
17 8. Annual assessment and reporting. The department shall annually assess adult
18 education needs in the State relative to funding appropriated by the Legislature. By
19 December 3, 2025 and annually thereafter, the department shall submit a report to the joint
20 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters with the
21 findings of the assessment. The report must include:
22 A. Funding allocated to each adult education program in the State;
23 B. The full funding amount that each adult education program in the State is eligible
24 for and the amount that figure has been prorated by the department;
25 C. The impact of funding on adult education program outcomes;
26 D. Additional funding needs;
27 E. A plan for gradually increasing annual funding to adult education programs in order
28 to reach 100% of the State's obligation for adult education funding in accordance with
29 subsection 4; and
30 F. Recommendations on improvements to adult education.'
31 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 5 and inserting the following:
32 'Sec. 5. Distribution of funding. The Department of Education shall distribute all
33 funding attributable to adult education programs in the essential programs and services

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1140, L.D. 1705
1 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
2 number to read consecutively.
3 SUMMARY
44 This amendment removes from the bill provisions related to the establishment of the
55 Career Advancement and Navigation Program. The amendment adds additional
66 requirements to the Department of Education's annual assessment and report, including the
77 full funding amount each adult education program is eligible for and a plan to achieve 100%
88 funding for adult education. The amendment also requires the department to distribute
99 funding directly to adult education programs rather than through the school administrative
1100 unit. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
