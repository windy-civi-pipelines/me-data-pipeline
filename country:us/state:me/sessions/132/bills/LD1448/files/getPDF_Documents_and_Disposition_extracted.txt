132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1448
S.P. 589 In Senate, April 3, 2025
Launch in Northfield
Reference to the Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife suggested and ordered
printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator MOORE of Washington. (BY REQUEST)
Cosponsored by Representative TUELL of East Machias.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §12709 is enacted to read:
3 §12709. Bog Lake public boat launch winter access
4 The department shall ensure that the gate to the Bog Lake public boat launch in the
5 Town of Northfield is open from January 1st to March 15th each year.
6 SUMMARY
77 This bill requires the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to ensure the gate to
88 the Bog Lake public boat launch in the Town of Northfield is open from January 1st to
99 March 15th each year.
