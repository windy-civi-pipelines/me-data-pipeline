Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 312
An Act Regarding the Rates of Speed at Which School Buses Travel
Received by the Clerk of the House on January 30, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative SWALLOW of Houlton.
Cosponsored by Senator STEWART of Aroostook.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §2074, sub-§1, ¶C, as enacted by PL 1993, c. 683, Pt. A, §2
3 and affected by Pt. B, §5, is amended to read:
4 C. Twenty-five miles per hour in a business or residential district or built-up portion
5 unless otherwise posted; and
6 Sec. 2. 29-A MRSA §2074, sub-§1, ¶D, as amended by PL 2005, c. 577, §30, is
7 further amended to read:
8 D. Forty-five miles per hour The posted speed limit on all other public ways unless
9 otherwise posted; and.
10 Sec. 3. 29-A MRSA §2074, sub-§1, ¶E, as amended by PL 2009, c. 9, §1, is
11 repealed.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill removes language limiting a school bus to a speed of 45 miles per hour,
1144 including when transporting pupils.
