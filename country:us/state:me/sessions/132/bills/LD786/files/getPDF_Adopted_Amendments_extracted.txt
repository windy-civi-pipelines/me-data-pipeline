2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 CRIMINAL JUSTICE AND PUBLIC SAFETY
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 346, L.D. 786, “An Act to Promote
10 Public Safety and Retain Essential First Responders by Funding the Maine Length of
11 Service Award Program”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Promote Public Safety and Retain Essential First Responders by
14 Converting the Maine Length of Service Award Program Trust Fund to a Nonlapsing
15 Fund'
16 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
17 following:
18 'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §3372, sub-§16, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 352, §1, is amended
19 to read:
20 16. Investment of program funds. The board shall establish a program trust fund
21 within which the funds paid into the program must be deposited. A participant shall select
22 investments for the amounts credited to the participant's program account from a menu of
23 investment options. Distributions of accrued service awards must be made from the
24 program trust fund in accordance with the program provisions. The program trust fund
25 must be established and maintained in accordance with applicable sections of the United
26 States Internal Revenue Code. Subject to review and approval by the Treasurer of State,
27 the program trust fund investment options made available to participants must be selected
28 by the board. Any unexpended balance in the program trust fund at the end of a year may
29 not lapse and must be carried forward to be available for expenditure by the board in the
30 subsequent year for program purposes.'
31 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
32 number to read consecutively.
