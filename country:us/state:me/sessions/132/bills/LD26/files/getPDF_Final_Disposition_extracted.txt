Section 1:
APPROVED CHAPTER
MAY 23, 2025 94
BY GOVERNOR PUBLIC LAW
STATE OF MAINE
_____
IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD
TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE
_____
S.P. 41 - L.D. 26
Disbursed from the Maine Ground and Surface Waters Clean-up and
Response Fund for Certain Personal Services of the Department of
Environmental Protection
Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §551, sub-§5, ¶A, as amended by PL 2015, c. 319, §16, is further
amended to read:
A. Administrative expenses, personal services and equipment costs of the department
related to the administration and enforcement of this subchapter and subchapter 2‑B,
except that total disbursements for personal services may not exceed $7,000,000 per
fiscal year adjusted on an annual basis based on the Consumer Price Index as published
by the United States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor Statistics;
