Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1212
S.P. 501 In Senate, March 20, 2025
Support the Promotion and Use of Modern Wood Heating
Reference to the Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology suggested and ordered
printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator HARRINGTON of York.
Cosponsored by Representatives: FOSTER of Dexter, MCINTYRE of Lowell, PAUL of
Winterport.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Modern wood heating. Resolved: That the Efficiency Maine Trust, in
2 collaboration with the Department of Economic and Community Development, shall work
3 with representatives of the modern wood heating system industry to study and make
4 recommendations relating to programs and incentives encouraging the promotion and use
5 of modern wood heating systems in the State. The Efficiency Maine Trust shall submit a
6 report related to the study to the Joint Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and
7 Technology by December 3, 2025. The report must include recommendations for
8 Efficiency Maine Trust programs and incentives supporting the promotion and use of
9 modern wood heating systems in the State. The joint standing committee may report out a
10 bill related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
11 SUMMARY
1122 This resolve requires the Efficiency Maine Trust, in collaboration with the Department
1133 of Economic and Community Development, to work with representatives of the modern
1144 wood heating system industry to study and make recommendations relating to programs
1155 and incentives encouraging the promotion and use of modern wood heating systems in the
1166 State. The Efficiency Maine Trust must submit a report related to the study to the Joint
1177 Standing Committee on Energy, Utilities and Technology and the joint standing committee
1188 may report out a bill related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd
1199 Legislature.
