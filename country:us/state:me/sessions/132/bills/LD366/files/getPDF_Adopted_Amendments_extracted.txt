1 L.D. 366
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 TAXATION
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 158, L.D. 366, “An Act to Ensure
10 Consistency of Income Tax Deductions for Retired Members of the Uniformed Services”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §5122, sub-§2, ¶M-2, as amended by PL 2023, c. 523, Pt. B,
14 §1, is further amended by amending subparagraph (2), division (c) to read:
15 (c) "Military retirement plan" means retirement plan benefits received as a
16 result of service in the active or reserve components of the United States Army,
17 Navy, Air Force, Marines, Coast Guard or Space Force. For tax years
18 beginning on or after January 1, 2026, "military retirement plan" means
19 retirement plan benefits received as a result of service in the active or reserve
20 components of the uniformed services of the United States as defined in 37
21 United States Code, Section 101(3).'
22 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
23 number to read consecutively.
24 SUMMARY
2255 This amendment clarifies the definition of "military retirement plan" for purposes of
2266 the pension benefit deduction by referencing a definition in the United States Code.
