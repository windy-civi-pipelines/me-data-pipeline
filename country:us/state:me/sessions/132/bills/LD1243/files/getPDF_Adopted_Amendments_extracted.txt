3 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 818, L.D. 1243, “An Act Regarding the
10 Licensing of Assisted Living Facilities”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph B in the 6th line (page 1, line 19 in L.D.) by
12 striking out the following: "had begun" and inserting the following: 'received municipal
13 approval and permits and project financing approvals prior to March 1, 2023 and began'
14 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph B in the last line (page 1, line 21 in L.D.) by
15 inserting after the following: "no" the following: 'quality of care and services or resident
16 rights'
17 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
18 number to read consecutively.
19 SUMMARY
2200 This amendment includes a requirement that an assisted living facility that is applying
2211 for another license at the same license level must have received all municipal approvals
2222 and permits and financing approvals prior to March 1, 2023 in order to be granted a license.
2233 It clarifies that any violations of quality of care and services or resident rights would
2244 disqualify the applicant from being issued a license.
