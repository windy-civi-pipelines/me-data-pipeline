5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 243, L.D. 343, “An Act to Direct the
10 Public Utilities Commission to Seek Informational Bids Regarding Small Modular Nuclear
11 Reactors in the State”
12 Amend the bill in section 1 in §124 by striking out all of subsection 2 (page 1, lines 11
13 to 19 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
14 '2. Requests for informational bids. By October 1, 2026, and triennially thereafter,
15 the commission shall request informational bids regarding the establishment of a small
16 modular nuclear reactor in the State. The informational bids must include the costs of
17 establishing a modular reactor in the State, either through manufacture on site or transport
18 into the State, the proposed location of a modular reactor, the time needed to establish a
19 modular reactor in the State, the annual operational costs of a modular reactor and any other
20 information the commission determines necessary. The commission shall issue protective
21 orders pursuant to section 1311-A to protect the information provided by persons
22 submitting bids. The bids are not binding on the State or the person submitting the bid.'
23 Amend the bill in section 1 in §124 by striking out all of subsection 3 (page 1, lines 20
24 to 25 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
25 '3. Triennial report. By January 15, 2027, and triennially thereafter, the commission
26 shall submit to the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over
27 energy and utility matters a report regarding the informational bids received by the
28 commission pursuant to subsection 2. The commission shall provide information in the
29 report regarding the informational bids received pursuant to subsection 2 in a manner that
30 protects the identity of persons submitting bids. The joint standing committee may report
31 out a bill based on the report in the same legislative session in which the report is received.'
32 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
33 number to read consecutively.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 243, L.D. 343
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment requires the Public Utilities Commission to request informational bids
33 regarding the establishment of a small modular nuclear reactor in the State by October 1,
44 2026, and triennially thereafter, instead of requiring the commission to conduct an annual
55 request for proposals for informational bids starting on October 1st, as proposed in the bill.
66 It also directs the commission to issue protective orders to protect the information provided
77 by persons submitting bids. The amendment changes the reporting date requirement in the
88 bill from an annual report to be submitted beginning January 15, 2026 to a triennial report
99 to be submitted beginning January 15, 2027.
