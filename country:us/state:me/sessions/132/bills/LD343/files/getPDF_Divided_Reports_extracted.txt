7 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
8 HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 243, L.D. 343, “An Act to Direct the Public
9 Utilities Commission to Seek Informational Bids Regarding Small Modular Nuclear
10 Reactors in the State”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Ensure Periodic Assessment for Clean Energy Technology'
13 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
14 following:
15 'Sec. 1. 2 MRSA §9, sub-§3, ¶C, as amended by PL 2013, c. 541, §1, is further
16 amended by amending subparagraph (1), division (d) to read:
17 (d) Include a description of activities undertaken pursuant to paragraph H; and
18 Sec. 2. 2 MRSA §9, sub-§3, ¶C, as amended by PL 2013, c. 541, §1, is further
19 amended by amending subparagraph (1), division (e) to read:
20 (e) Include a description of the State's activities relating to the expansion of
21 natural gas service, any actions taken by the office to expand access to natural
22 gas in the State and any recommendations for actions by the Legislature to
23 expand access to natural gas in the State.; and
24 Sec. 3. 2 MRSA §9, sub-§3, ¶C, as amended by PL 2013, c. 541, §1, is further
25 amended by enacting a new subparagraph (1), division (f) to read:
26 (f) Include a section that reviews and evaluates emerging energy technologies
27 that could contribute to meeting the greenhouse gas emissions reduction goals
28 established under Title 38, section 576-A, including but not limited to energy
29 storage and small modular nuclear reactors.'
30 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
31 number to read consecutively.
32 SUMMARY
3333 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It requires the Director of the
3344 Governor's Energy Office to include in the biennial update to the comprehensive state
3355 energy plan a section that renews and evaluates emerging energy technologies that could
