8 HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 525, L.D. 818, “An Act to Allow Expenditure
9 of Maine Clean Election Act Funding for the Care of Candidates' Dependents”
10 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 6 in the first blocked paragraph in the last line
11 (page 1, line 22 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "campaign" the following: ',
12 except that "campaign-related purposes" does not include caregiving services provided by
13 a candidate or the candidate's spouse or domestic partner for the care of the dependent of
14 that candidate or candidate's spouse or domestic partner'
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment clarifies that Maine Clean Election Fund funds may not be used for
1199 payment of caregiving services provided by a candidate or the candidate's spouse or
2200 domestic partner if the caregiving services are for the dependent of that candidate or
2211 candidate's spouse or domestic partner.
