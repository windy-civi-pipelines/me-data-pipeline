9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1303, L.D. 1944, “An Act to Protect
10 Children and Adults from Technology-facilitated Sexual Abuse”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Protect Individuals from the Threatened Unauthorized Dissemination of
13 Certain Private Images, Including Artificially Generated Private Images'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §4651, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2017, c. 455, §1, is further
17 amended to read:
18 2. Harassment. "Harassment" means:
19 A. Three or more acts of intimidation, confrontation, physical force or the threat of
20 physical force directed against any person, family or business that are made with the
21 intention of causing fear, intimidation or damage to personal property and that do in
22 fact cause fear, intimidation or damage to personal property; or
23 C. A single act or course of conduct constituting a violation of section 4681; Title 17,
24 section 2931; or Title 17‑A, section 201, 202, 203, 204, 207, 208, 209, 210, 210‑A,
25 211, 253, 254, 255‑A, 256, 258, 259‑A, 259‑B, 260, 261, 282, 283, 301, 302, 303, 506,
26 506‑A, 511, 511‑A, 556, 802, 805, 806, 852 or 853.; or
27 D. Communicating to a person a threat to commit, or to cause to be committed, a crime
28 of unauthorized dissemination of certain private images as described in Title 17-A,
29 section 511-A, consciously disregarding a substantial risk that the natural and probable
30 consequence of such a threat is to place the person who is depicted or who appears to
31 be depicted in the image in reasonable fear that the crime will be committed.
32 This definition does not include any act protected by law.
33 Sec. 2. 17-A MRSA §511-A, sub-§1, as enacted by PL 2015, c. 339, §1, is
34 amended to read:
Page 1 - 132LR0110(02)
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1303, L.D. 1944
1 1. A person is guilty of unauthorized dissemination of certain private images if the
2 person, with the intent to harass, torment or threaten the depicted person or another person,
3 intentionally or knowingly disseminates, displays or publishes a photograph, videotape,
4 film or digital recording an image of another person in a state of nudity or engaged in a
5 sexual act or engaged in sexual contact or an image that has been created or modified so
6 that it appears to show the depicted person in a state of nudity or engaged in a sexual act or
7 engaged in sexual contact in a manner in which there is no public or newsworthy purpose
8 when the person knows or should have known that the depicted person:
9 B. Is identifiable from the image itself or information displayed in connection with the
10 image; and
11 C. Has not consented to the dissemination, display or publication of the private image.
12 Sec. 3. 17-A MRSA §511-A, sub-§3, ¶D is enacted to read:
13 D. "Image" means something that is made, captured, generated or saved as a print,
14 negative, slide, motion picture, photograph, computer data file, animation, videotape,
15 livestream or other mechanically, electronically or chemically reproduced visual image
16 or material.
17 Sec. 4. 19-A MRSA §4102, sub-§1, ¶G, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 647, Pt. A, §3
18 and affected by Pt. B, §65, is amended to read:
19 G. Engaging in the unauthorized dissemination of certain private images as prohibited
20 pursuant to Title 17‑A, section 511‑A; or
21 Sec. 5. 19-A MRSA §4102, sub-§1, ¶H, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 647, Pt. A, §3
22 and affected by Pt. B, §65, is amended to read:
23 H. Engaging in aggravated sex trafficking or sex trafficking as described in Title 17‑A,
24 section 852 or 853, respectively.; or
25 Sec. 6. 19-A MRSA §4102, sub-§1, ¶I is enacted to read:
26 I. Communicating to a person a threat to commit, or to cause to be committed, a crime
27 of unauthorized dissemination of certain private images as described in Title 17-A,
28 section 511-A, consciously disregarding a substantial risk that the natural and probable
29 consequence of such a threat is to place the person who is depicted or who appears to
30 be depicted in the image in reasonable fear that the crime will be committed.'
31 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
32 number to read consecutively.
33 SUMMARY
3344 This amendment changes the title of the bill and makes the following changes to the
3355 bill.
36 1. It removes the provisions of the bill expanding the crimes of sexual exploitation of
37 a minor, dissemination of sexually explicit material and possession of sexually explicit
38 material.
39 2. It amends the provision of the bill affecting the crime of unauthorized dissemination
40 of certain private images to provide that a person commits this crime if the person, without
41 consent and with the intent to harass, torment or threaten the depicted person or another
Page 2 - 132LR0110(02)
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1303, L.D. 1944
412 person, intentionally or knowingly disseminates, displays or publishes an image that has
423 been created or modified so that it appears to show the depicted person in a state of nudity
434 or engaged in a sexual act or engaged in sexual contact.
4 3. It expands the definition of "harassment" under the protection from harassment laws
5 and the definition of "abuse" under the protection from abuse laws to allow a person to seek
6 a protection order against another person who threatens to commit the crime of
7 unauthorized dissemination of certain private images if the natural and probable
8 consequence of the threat is to place the person who is depicted or who appears to be
9 depicted in the image in reasonable fear that the crime will be committed.
