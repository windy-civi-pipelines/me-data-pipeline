38 §1420-Q. Apprentice insurance producer license
Page 1 - 132LR1307(01)
1 1. License authorized. The superintendent may issue an apprentice insurance
2 producer license in accordance with this subsection without requiring an examination. An
3 individual applying for a license as an apprentice insurance producer shall apply to the
4 superintendent in the form and manner determined by the superintendent and declare under
5 penalty of refusal, suspension or revocation of the license that the statements made in the
6 application are true, correct and complete to the best of the individual’s knowledge and
7 belief. Before approving an application and issuing a license, the superintendent must find
8 that the individual:
9 A. Is at least 18 years of age;
10 B. Has a high school diploma or its equivalent;
11 C. Is employed by a licensed insurance producer who has certified to the
12 superintendent that the licensed insurance producer will supervise the apprentice
13 insurance producer licensee and will assume responsibility for all acts of the apprentice
14 insurance producer licensee for a period not to exceed 10,000 hours; and
15 D. Has paid any required fees set forth in section 601.
16 2. Limitations. The superintendent may limit the issuance of apprentice insurance
17 producer licenses to no more than 2 apprentice licenses per licensed insurance producer.
18 The superintendent may limit the authority of an apprentice insurance producer licensee in
19 any way determined necessary to protect insureds and the public and may by order revoke
20 an apprentice insurance producer license if the interest of insureds or the public is
21 endangered.
22 3. Renewal. After having completed 10,000 hours as an apprentice insurance producer,
23 an apprentice insurance producer licensee may not renew the license or engage in any
24 insurance activities without obtaining an insurance producer license in accordance with the
25 requirements of this subchapter.
26 4. Legal responsibility. The licensed insurance producer who employs an apprentice
27 insurance producer licensee assumes all legal responsibility for all acts of the apprentice
28 insurance producer licensee who is employed by the licensed insurance producer.
29 SUMMARY
3300 This bill establishes an apprentice insurance producer license.
