1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §242, first ¶, as amended by PL 2013, c. 16, §2, is further amended
3 to read:
4 The Office of the State Auditor must be organized in the manner the State Auditor
5 considers best suited to the accomplishment of its functions. The office may have those
6 auditors, assistants and employees as the State Auditor may require, classified, titled and
7 compensated as the State Auditor considers appropriate, but they are subject to the Civil
8 Service Law.
9 SUMMARY
1100 This bill provides that the auditors, assistants and employees in the Office of the State
1111 Auditor are classified, titled and compensated as the State Auditor considers appropriate.
