5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 26, L.D. 13, “An Act to Provide Funds
10 Necessary for the Production and Delivery of Election Materials by the Secretary of State
11 and to Reconvene the Working Group to Study Polling Places at Schools”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Provide Funds Necessary for the Production and Delivery of Election
14 Materials by the Secretary of State'
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in §611 in subsection 1 in the last line (page 1, line 12 in
16 L.D.) by inserting after the following: "purpose." the following: 'The unexpended balance
17 in the fund is limited to $1,500,000.'
18 Amend the bill in section 1 in §611 in subsection 2 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 14 in
19 L.D.) by inserting after the following: "30th" the following: 'biennially'
20 Amend the bill in section 1 in §611 in subsection 2 in the 3rd line (page 1, line 15 in
21 L.D.) by striking out the following: "$266,000" and inserting the following: '$410,000'
22 Amend the bill in section 1 in §611 by inserting after subsection 3 the following:
23 '4. Limitations on transfers; excess funds. Notwithstanding any provision of the law
24 to the contrary, if the transfer under subsection 2 or subsection 3 would cause the
25 unexpended balance in the fund to exceed the limit established in subsection 1, the transfer
26 must be reduced to an amount equal to the difference of $1,500,000 and the unexpended
27 balance in the fund on the date of the transfer. By June 30th of each fiscal year, the State
28 Controller shall transfer from the fund to the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund
29 any amount in excess of the limit established in subsection 1.'
30 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following:
31 'Sec. 2. Transfer of funds. Notwithstanding the September 30, 2025 date of
32 transfer under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 21-A, section 611, subsection 2, the
33 transfer of $410,000 to occur on or before September 30, 2025 pursuant to Title 21-A,
34 section 611, subsection 2 must occur on or before September 30, 2025 or the effective date
35 of this section, whichever occurs later.

117 '
12 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
13 number to read consecutively.
14 SUMMARY
1155 This amendment adjusts the ongoing transfer to the Production and Delivery of
1166 Election Materials Fund, established in the bill, from the unappropriated surplus of the
1177 General Fund from $266,000 annually to $410,000 biennially. It also limits the
1188 unexpended balance in the new fund to $1,500,000.
1199 The amendment provides that the biennial transfer of funds from the unappropriated
2200 surplus of the General Fund to the new fund and the transfer of funds as a result of carrying
2211 forward and transferring any remaining balances in the Department of the Secretary of
2222 State, Bureau of Corporations, Elections and Commissions program, General Fund account
2233 at the end of each fiscal year must be reduced by any amount that would create an
2244 unexpended balance in excess of $1,500,000. It also provides that by June 30th of each
2255 fiscal year, the State Controller must transfer from the new fund to the unappropriated
2266 surplus of the General Fund any amount in excess of $1,500,000.
2277 The amendment also removes the section reconvening the working group to study
2288 polling places at schools.
