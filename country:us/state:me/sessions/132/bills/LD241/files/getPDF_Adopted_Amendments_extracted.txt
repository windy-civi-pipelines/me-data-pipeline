7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 107, L.D. 241, “An Act to Authorize the
10 Public Utilities Commission to Approve Rate Adjustments for Low-income Water Utility
11 Ratepayers”
12 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
13 following:
14 'Sec. 1. 35-A MRSA §6115 is enacted to read:
15 §6115. Low-income assistance
16 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, a water utility or group of water
17 utilities may petition the commission for approval of a low-income assistance program to
18 allow a qualifying low-income residential ratepayer to receive a discount or credit applying
19 to the amount the residential ratepayer would otherwise owe to the water utility. The
20 commission may approve the proposed discount or credit if it determines that the proposal
21 is a cost-effective means of keeping water affordable for low-income residential ratepayers.
22 The commission may not require a water utility to initiate a low-income assistance program
23 pursuant to this section.
24 For the purposes of this section, "residential ratepayer" means a customer who is
25 considered residential under the terms and conditions of the water utility.'
26 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
27 number to read consecutively.
28 SUMMARY
2299 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment allows, instead of requires as in the
3300 bill, the Public Utilities Commission to approve the rate change if it determines that the
3311 proposal is a cost-effective way to keep water affordable for low-income residential
