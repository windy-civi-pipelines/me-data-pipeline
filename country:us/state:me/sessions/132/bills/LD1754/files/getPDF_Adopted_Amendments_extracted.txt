20 SUMMARY
2211 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, does the following.
22 1. It amends the definition of "case equivalent" to mean the equivalent volume of 12
23 750-milliliter units.
24 2. It explicitly states that direct shipments of spirits are exempt from the Department
25 of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of Alcoholic Beverages and Lottery
26 Operations' state liquor contract.
27 3. It requires a direct shipper of spirits to register all spirits to be shipped into the State
28 with the bureau in a manner determined by the bureau.
29 4. It requires the bureau to determine and set the retail price at which spirits are sold
30 for direct shipment to consumers.
31 5. It provides that an out-of-state distiller, rectifier, importer for a distillery or importer
32 of a foreign brand of distilled spirits, who is the primary source of supply, may obtain a
33 direct shipper license for importing spirits.
34 6. It requires a direct shipper of spirits to register with the Department of
35 Administrative and Financial Services, Maine Revenue Services.
36 7. It requires a direct shipper to pay to the bureau the difference between the retail
37 price approved by the bureau and the supplier price submitted to the bureau by the direct
38 shipper.
39 8. It adds to direct shipper reporting requirements the unique tracking number for each
40 shipment and the total amount of tax owed to the State.
