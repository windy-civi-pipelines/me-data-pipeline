1 L.D. 899
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
4 Reproduced and distributed under the direction of the Secretary of the Senate.
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 388, L.D. 899, “An Act to Strengthen
10 the Requirements for Medical Payments Coverage”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 24-A MRSA §2910-A, sub-§4, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 182, §1, is
14 amended to read:
15 B. Medical payments coverage in a casualty insurance policy is assignable only by
16 written agreement between the insured and the casualty insurer on a form prescribed
17 or approved by the superintendent. Benefits under medical payments coverage must
18 be applied as directed by the insured.'
19 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
20 number to read consecutively.
21 SUMMARY
2222 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment requires the assignment of medical
2233 payments coverage to be in writing on a form prescribed or approved by the Superintendent
2244 of Insurance.
