10 Prevention and Support Program”
11 Amend the amendment in the first instructional paragraph in the last 4 lines (page 1,
12 lines 13 to 16 in amendment) by striking out the following: "The department shall
13 implement the requirements of this section to the extent possible given available resources
14 and shall develop its budget for the program to include state matching funds for federal
15 funds, regardless of whether federal funding is received. The department may seek and
16 receive private grant funds to support the program." and inserting the following: 'The
17 department shall implement the requirements of this chapter within existing budgeted
18 resources. The department shall develop its budget for the requirements of this chapter to
19 include state matching funds for federal funds, regardless of whether federal funding is
20 received. The department may seek and receive private grant funds to support the
21 requirements of this chapter.'

23 'Sec. 4. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
24 allocations are made.
25 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF

27 Initiative: Provides allocations to establish a fund and allow expenditures.

HOUSE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 1112, L.D. 1677
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment applies the provisions of Committee Amendment "A" to the
33 requirements of the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 22, chapter 279, which is enacted in the
44 bill, rather than just to one section of that chapter, and it requires the Department of Health
55 and Human Services to implement the requirements of the entire chapter within existing
66 budgeted resources. It replaces the appropriations and allocations section.
