15 SUMMARY
1166 This amendment requires the Department of Health and Human Services to implement
1177 the Alzheimer's Disease and Related Dementias Prevention and Support Program to the
1188 extent possible given available resources and develop its budget for the program to include
1199 state matching funds for federal funds, regardless of whether federal funding is received. It
2200 provides that the department may seek and receive private grant funds to support the
2211 program. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section.
