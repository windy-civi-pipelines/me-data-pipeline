5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 503, L.D. 1214, “An Act to Designate
10 the Second Congressional District "Acadia"”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 1 MRSA §72, sub-§20-A is enacted to read:
14 20-A. Second Congressional District. "Second Congressional District" or "Second
15 District," as used in statute, must also be known as "Acadia." Each occurrence of the term
16 "Second Congressional District" or "Second District" in statute must be followed by the
17 modifier "also known as Acadia."
18 Sec. 2. 20-A MRSA §401, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2013, c. 15, §1, is further
19 amended to read:
20 1. Appointment. The state board consists of 9 members and, beginning in the 2007-
21 2008 school year, 2 nonvoting student members, one junior and one senior in high school.
22 All members are appointed by the Governor. Four members must reside in the State's First
23 Congressional District at the time of appointment, 4 members must reside in the State's
24 Second Congressional District, also known as Acadia, at the time of appointment and one
25 member may reside in either the First Congressional District or the Second Congressional
26 District, also known as Acadia, at the time of appointment. One of the student members
27 must attend school in the State's First Congressional District at the time of appointment and
28 the other student member must attend school in the State's Second Congressional District,
29 also known as Acadia, at the time of appointment. Each appointment is subject to review
30 by the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education
31 matters and to confirmation by the Senate.
32 Sec. 3. 21-A MRSA §1205-A, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 2021, c. 552, Pt. C, §1, is
33 amended to read:
34 2. Second District, also known as Acadia. The Second District, also known as
35 Acadia, consists of the counties of Androscoggin, Aroostook, Franklin, Hancock, Oxford,

7 SUMMARY
88 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, amends the bill. The
99 amendment enacts a new section of law establishing that the term "Second Congressional
1100 District" or "Second District" as used in statute must also be known as "Acadia." Each
1111 occurrence of the term "Second Congressional District" or "Second District" in statute must
1122 be followed by the modifier "also known as Acadia."
