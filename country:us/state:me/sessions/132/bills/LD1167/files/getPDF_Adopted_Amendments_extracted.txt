3 HOUSING AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 772, L.D. 1167, “Resolve, to Create a
10 Pilot Program to Assist Nonprofit Housing Developers in Rehabilitating Existing Aging
11 Housing Stock for First-time Home Buyers”
12 Amend the resolve in section 1 in the 4th line (page 1, line 4 in L.D.) by inserting after
13 the following: "State" the following: 'and to provide relocation services to tenants who must
14 vacate a unit being rehabilitated under the program'
15 Amend the resolve in section 1 in the 4th and 5th lines (page 1, lines 4 and 5 in L.D.)
16 by striking out the following: "The program must rehabilitate 15 units, and a grant issued
17 by the program may be in an amount up to $80,000 per unit." and inserting the following:
18 'The Maine State Housing Authority shall establish the maximum and minimum grant
19 amounts with the goal to rehabilitate 15 housing units.'
20 Amend the resolve in section 2 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 7 in L.D.) by inserting after
21 the following: "including" the following: ', but not limited to,'
22 Amend the resolve by inserting after section 2 the following:
23 'Sec. 3. Relocated tenant. Resolved: That a tenant who has to vacate a unit during
24 the unit's rehabilitation under the program and who is a first-time home buyer must have
25 the right of first refusal to purchase the vacated unit upon project completion.'
26 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 4 and inserting the following:
27 'Sec. 4. Appropriations and allocations. Resolved: That the following
28 appropriations and allocations are made.
29 HOUSING AUTHORITY, MAINE STATE
30 Existing Housing Stock Rehabilitation Program N510
31 Initiative: Provides one-time funds for a pilot program that provides grants to nonprofit
32 housing developers to rehabilitate existing aging housing stock for purchase by qualified
33 first-time home buyers and to provide relocation services to tenants who must vacate a unit
34 being rehabilitated under the program.

8 SUMMARY
99 This amendment requires relocation services be provided under the pilot program to
1100 tenants who have to vacate a unit during the unit's rehabilitation under the pilot program.
1111 The amendment provides that the tenant who has to vacate the unit and who is a first-time
1122 home buyer has the right of first refusal to purchase the unit. The amendment requires that
1133 the Maine State Housing Authority establish maximum and minimum grant amounts with
1144 the goal to rehabilitate 15 units.
