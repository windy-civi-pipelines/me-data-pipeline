1 L.D. 741
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 HOUSING AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 483, L.D. 741, “An Act to Increase
10 Preparedness for Solar Power Adoption in Maine”
11 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following:
12 'Sec. 2. Rule exceptions. In adopting rules to define "new commercial buildings" in
13 the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 10, section 9722, subsection 6, paragraph B,
14 subparagraph (4), the Technical Building Codes and Standards Board shall:
15 1. Exclude commercial buildings that have received all necessary permits and
16 approvals before July 1, 2026;
17 2. Exclude buildings that receive funding from the Maine State Housing Authority; and
18 3. Consider any additional exclusions based on building size, orientation or any other
19 relevant factor.
20 Sec. 3. Rulemaking. In adopting by rule Appendix CB of the 2021 edition of the
21 International Energy Conservation Code published by the International Code Council for
22 the construction of new commercial buildings pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title
23 10, section 9722, subsection 6, paragraph B, subparagraph (4), the Technical Building
24 Codes and Standards Board may consider changes to Appendix CB, including:
25 1. Building orientations for which a commercial building may be excluded;
26 2. The required floor area of the electrical energy storage system-ready area;
27 3. The required reservation of space in the main electrical service panel to allow
28 installation of a dual-pole circuit breaker; and
29 4. Any other changes the board considers appropriate.'
30 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
31 number to read consecutively.
