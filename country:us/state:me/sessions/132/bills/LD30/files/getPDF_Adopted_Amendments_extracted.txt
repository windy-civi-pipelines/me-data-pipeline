5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 47, L.D. 30, “Resolve, Directing the
10 Department of Transportation to Implement the Recommendations of the Portland to
11 Auburn Rail Use Advisory Council Regarding the Rail Line from Portland to Yarmouth ”
12 Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'Resolve, to Direct the Department of Transportation to Implement the
14 Recommendations of the Portland to Auburn Rail Use Advisory Council'
15 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following:
16 'Sec. 1. Trail construction on Berlin Subdivision. Resolved: That, based on the
17 majority recommendation of the Portland to Auburn Rail Use Advisory Council and
18 pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 23, section 7107, the Department of
19 Transportation shall, subject to available funding resources, permitting and municipal
20 agreements, remove state-owned inactive existing railroad track along the section known
21 as the Berlin Subdivision between the City of Portland and the City of Auburn, from a point
22 north of the swing bridge at Back Cove in Portland at or near mile marker 1.7 to mile
23 marker 26.5 in Auburn, and replace the track with an interim bicycle and pedestrian trail
24 surfaced with pavement or gravel or stone dust on the existing rail bed.'
25 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
26 section number to read consecutively.
27 SUMMARY
2288 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, expands the direction
2299 to the Department of Transportation to implement the recommendations of the Portland to
3300 Auburn Rail Use Advisory Council. The amendment directs the Department of
3311 Transportation to remove state-owned inactive existing railroad track along the section
3322 known as the Berlin Subdivision between the City of Portland and the City of Auburn,
