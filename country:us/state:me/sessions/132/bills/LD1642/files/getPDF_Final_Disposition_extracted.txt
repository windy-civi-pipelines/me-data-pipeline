18 'Sec. 1. 10 MRSA c. 205-B, headnote is amended to read:
19 CHAPTER 205-B
20 AUTOMATIC SUBSCRIPTION OR HEALTH CLUB MEMBERSHIP RENEWAL
21 Sec. 2. 10 MRSA §1210-C, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is amended by
22 amending the section headnote to read:
23 §1210-C. Cancellation of subscriptions or memberships
24 Sec. 3. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶A, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is
25 amended to read:
26 A. "Automatic subscription or health club membership renewal" means an offer or
27 agreement to provide any of the following goods or services for a specified time and
28 price that is automatically renewed at the end of a definite term for a subsequent term
29 unless the consumer cancels affirmatively acts to cancel the agreement or to reject
30 further provision of the goods or services:
31 (1) Online magazines, journals and periodicals;
32 (2) Online media players;

5 (7) Health club memberships.
6 Sec. 4. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is
7 amended to read:
8 B. "Extended automatic subscription or health club membership renewal" means an
9 automatic subscription or health club membership renewal with a specified
10 subscription term of 12 months or more, in which the subscription or membership
11 automatically renews for a specified term of more than one month unless the consumer
12 cancels affirmatively acts to cancel the subscription or membership or to reject further
13 provision of the goods or services.
14 Sec. 5. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶B-1 is enacted to read:
15 B-1. "Health club" means an establishment that provides health club services intended
16 to improve or maintain the user's physical condition or appearance through exercise.
17 "Health club" includes, but is not limited to, fitness centers, gyms, health studios,
18 exercise clubs and facilities that offer access to equipment, classes or amenities such
19 as pools, saunas or courts. "Health club" does not include:
20 (1) An establishment operated by a nonprofit organization;
21 (2) An establishment operated by a public or private school, college or university;
22 (3) An establishment operated by the State, a political subdivision of the State or
23 the Federal Government;
24 (4) An establishment in which health club services are incidental to the primary
25 purpose of the business; and
26 (5) A health care facility as defined by Title 22, section 328, subsection 8.
27 Sec. 6. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶B-2 is enacted to read:
28 B-2. "Health club membership" means an agreement under which a consumer pays or
29 commits to pay a health club for ongoing access to health club services, whether on a
30 recurring basis or for a specified term. "Health club membership" includes any
31 agreement entered into online, through a mobile app or over the Internet.
32 Sec. 7. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶B-3 is enacted to read:
33 B-3. "Health club services" means services, privileges and rights offered by a health
34 club for use of its equipment, classes or facilities, including but not limited to access
35 to exercise equipment, wellness programs, swimming pools, playing courts and fitness-
36 related amenities.
37 Sec. 8. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§1, ¶G, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is
38 amended to read:
39 G. "Seller" means a person who sells, leases or offers to sell or lease automatic
40 subscription or health club membership renewals or extended automatic subscription

3 Sec. 9. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is amended
4 to read:
5 2. Method of cancellation of automatic subscription or health club membership
6 renewal; required seller disclosure; misrepresentation. A seller may not make an
7 automatic subscription or health club membership renewal offer to a consumer in this State
8 unless the seller presents that consumer with an a clear, conspicuous and easily accessible
9 disclosure of all material terms of the renewal offer and of the methods that the consumer
10 may use to cancel the subscription or membership. A seller shall obtain the express consent
11 of a consumer to automatic subscription or health club membership renewal prior to
12 enrollment through a check box, electronic signature requirement or other affirmative
13 action by the consumer agreeing to the renewal offer. The seller must provide for online
14 cancellation of the subscription by any means of communicating information over a
15 computer network or health club membership by a consumer using the same method and
16 in the same manner by which the consumer initially agreed to the subscription or
17 membership. If a phone number is also provided for the purposes of cancellation of the
18 subscription or membership, the number must be toll‑free and must be prominently
19 displayed in the disclosure. The disclosure must also include, but is not limited to,
20 information regarding the amount and frequency of subscription or membership charges
21 and the seller's refund policy upon cancellation. In marketing, offering or selling a
22 subscription or membership with an automatic subscription or health club membership
23 renewal, a seller may not make any misrepresentation of a material fact associated with that
24 renewal offer.
25 Sec. 10. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§3, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is amended
26 to read:
27 3. Extended automatic subscriptions subscription or health club membership
28 renewals; additional notice requirement. A seller may not make an extended automatic
29 subscription or health club membership renewal offer to a consumer in this State unless the
30 seller complies with the same requirements and restrictions applicable to automatic
31 subscription or health club membership renewals under subsection 2 and additionally
32 notifies the consumer of the automatic renewal in accordance with this subsection. Notice
33 must be provided to the consumer no less No more than 30 days and no more than 60 days
34 before the cancellation deadline pursuant to the automatic subscription renewal. The seller
35 must provide for online cancellation of the subscription by any means of communicating
36 information over a computer network. The notice before the renewal date for an extended
37 automatic subscription or health club membership renewal, the seller shall provide notice
38 to the consumer regarding the renewal, which must disclose clearly and conspicuously:
39 A. That unless the consumer cancels the subscription or membership it will
40 automatically renew; and
41 B. Where the consumer can obtain details regarding the automatic subscription
42 renewal and cancellation procedure.
43 Sec. 11. 10 MRSA §1210-C, sub-§4, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is amended
44 to read:

SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to S.P. 650, L.D. 1642
1 4. Application. This chapter applies only to an agreement entered into or renewed
2 after January 1, 2020 under which a seller makes an automatic subscription or health club
3 membership renewal or extended automatic subscription or health club membership
4 renewal offer to a consumer in this State.
5 Sec. 12. 10 MRSA §1210-D, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 175, §1, is amended to read:
6 §1210-D. Violation
7 1. Violations. A violation of this section chapter is a violation of the Maine Unfair
8 Trade Practices Act. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, in addition to
9 remedies authorized pursuant to the Maine Unfair Trade Practices Act, a person found in
10 violation of this chapter based on unauthorized subscription or membership or other
11 charges billed to or paid by a consumer is required to provide to the consumer a refund or
12 credit for 3 times the unauthorized amounts billed or paid.
13 2. Exceptions. An action may not be brought under the Maine Unfair Trade Practices
14 Act if a seller violates this chapter as the result of an error and provides a full refund or
15 credit for all amounts billed to or paid by the consumer from the date of the subscription or
16 membership renewal until the date of the termination of the subscription or membership or
17 the date of the subsequent notice of renewal, whichever occurs first.
18 3. Enforcement; rules. The Attorney General shall enforce the provisions of this
19 chapter and shall bring necessary actions for violations of this chapter in accordance with
20 this section. The Attorney General may adopt rules necessary for the enforcement of this
21 chapter. Rules adopted by the Attorney General pursuant to this subsection are routine
22 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
23 Sec. 13. Application. Notwithstanding the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 10, section
24 1210-C, subsection 4, this Act applies only to offers or agreements entered into or renewed
25 on or after January 1, 2026 under which a seller makes an automatic subscription or health
26 club membership renewal or extended automatic subscription or health club membership
27 renewal offer to a consumer in this State.' '
28 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
29 section number to read consecutively.
30 SUMMARY
3311 This amendment incorporates the changes made to the bill by Committee Amendment
3322 "A" and clarifies that the automatic renewal provisions of the bill also apply to health club
3333 memberships.
