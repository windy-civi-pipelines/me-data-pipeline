1 L.D. 1461
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 EDUCATION AND CULTURAL AFFAIRS
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 953, L.D. 1461, “An Act to Prohibit
10 School Boards from Implementing or Enforcing Mask Mandates”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Prohibit School Boards from Implementing or Enforcing Mask Mandates
13 Unless Directed to Adopt a Mask Mandate by a State or Local Health Authority'
14 Amend the bill in section 1 in §6558 in subsection 2 in the first line (page 1, line 8 in
15 L.D.) by striking out the following: "A" and inserting the following: 'Except as provided in
16 subsection 3, a'
17 Amend the bill in section 1 in §6558 by striking out all of subsections 3 and 4 (page 1,
18 lines 10 to 14 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
19 '3. Exception. A school board may adopt a mask mandate upon direction from a state
20 or local health authority.'
21 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
22 number to read consecutively.
23 SUMMARY
2244 This amendment provides that a school board may adopt a mask mandate upon
2255 direction from a state or local health authority. The amendment also removes the
2266 provisions of the bill related to enforcement and penalties for a school board that
2277 implements or enforces a mask mandate.
