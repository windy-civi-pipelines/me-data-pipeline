8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1004, L.D. 1519, “An Act to Create a
9 Stewardship Program for Electronic Smoking Devices and Related Products”
10 Amend the bill in section 1 in §1617 in subsection 1 in paragraph B in the last 4 lines
11 (page 1, lines 13 to 16 in L.D.) by striking out the following: ""Electronic smoking device"
12 does not include drugs, devices or combination products authorized for sale by the United
13 States Food and Drug Administration, as those terms are defined in the Federal Food, Drug,
14 and Cosmetic Act."
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment removes from the definition of "electronic smoking device" the
1199 exclusion for drugs, devices and products authorized for sale by the United States Food and
2200 Drug Administration.
