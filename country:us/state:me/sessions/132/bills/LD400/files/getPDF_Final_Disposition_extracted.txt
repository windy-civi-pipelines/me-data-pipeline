1 L.D. 400
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
4 STATE OF MAINE
5 SENATE
6 132ND LEGISLATURE
7 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 254,
9 L.D. 400, “Resolve, Directing the Department of Public Safety, Office of the State Fire
10 Marshal to Compile a Statewide Inventory of Aqueous Film-forming Foam Concentrate”
11 Amend the amendment by inserting after the 6th instructional paragraph the following:
12 'Sec. 4. Transfer from Maine Solid Waste Management Fund to General
13 Fund unappropriated surplus. Resolved: That, notwithstanding any provision of
14 law to the contrary, the State Controller shall transfer $85,732 on or before June 30, 2026
15 and $86,378 on or before June 30, 2027 from the Maine Solid Waste Management Fund
16 established under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 38, section 2201 to the unappropriated
17 surplus of the General Fund to offset the cost of one limited-period Office Assistant II
18 position and related costs regarding the creation of an inventory of aqueous film-forming
19 foam concentrate in the State.'
20 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
21 section number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment requires the State Controller to transfer funds from the Maine Solid
2244 Waste Management Fund to the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund to offset the
2255 cost of one limited-period Office Assistant II position and related costs.
