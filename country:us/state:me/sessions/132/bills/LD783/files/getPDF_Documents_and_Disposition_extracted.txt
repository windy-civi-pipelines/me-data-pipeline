S.P. 341 In Senate, March 4, 2025
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 26, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Education and Cultural Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator TIPPING of Penobscot.
Cosponsored by Representative OSHER of Orono and
Senators: BALDACCI of Penobscot, BENNETT of Oxford, DUSON of Cumberland, LIBBY
of Cumberland, RAFFERTY of York, Representatives: BRENNAN of Portland, DILL of Old
Town, RANA of Bangor.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
3 allocations are made.
4 EDUCATION, DEPARTMENT OF
5 Adult Education 0364
6 Initiative: Provides ongoing funds to the Maine Multicultural Center in Bangor for one or
7 more positions to establish a comprehensive program for foreign-trained workers through
8 the Maine Multicultural Center. The positions will develop and coordinate the
9 comprehensive program, which must be designed to serve foreign-trained workers with an
10 emphasis on foreign-trained professionals and seek private funds to support the center's
11 programs and operations.
12 GENERAL FUND 2025-26 2026-27
13 All Other $190,000 $190,000
14 __________ __________
15 GENERAL FUND TOTAL $190,000 $190,000

1173 This bill provides ongoing funds to the Maine Multicultural Center in Bangor for one
1184 or more positions to establish a comprehensive program for foreign-trained workers
1195 through the Maine Multicultural Center.
