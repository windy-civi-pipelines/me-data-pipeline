3 AGRICULTURE, CONSERVATION AND FORESTRY
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1151, L.D. 1724, “Resolve, Authorizing
10 the Baxter State Park Authority to Convey Certain Land in Cumberland County”
11 Amend the resolve in the public land preamble in the 13th paragraph in the 2nd line
12 (page 2, line 5 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "and" and inserting the following:
13 'now, therefore, be it'
14 Amend the resolve in the public land preamble by striking out all of the 14th paragraph
15 (page 2, lines 6 to 9 in L.D.)
16 Amend the resolve in section 3 in the 5th line (page 2, line 29 in L.D.) by striking out
17 the following: "used" and inserting the following: 'deposited in the Public Reserved Lands
18 Acquisition Fund established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 12, section 1850,
19 subsection 2, and must be used by the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and
20 Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands'
21 Amend the resolve by inserting after section 3 the following:
22 'Sec. 4. Notification. Resolved: That the Baxter State Park Director shall notify
23 owners of property on Long Reach Lane in the Town of Harpswell abutting the parcel
24 described in section 1 and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having
25 jurisdiction over parks and lands matters at least 14 days in advance of any public meeting
26 at which the Baxter State Park Authority will consider a proposed conveyance of the Austin
27 Cary Lot described in section 1.
28 Sec. 5. Opportunity for review by legislative committee. Resolved: That a
29 member of the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over parks
30 and lands matters who receives notification under section 4 may notify the Baxter State
31 Park Director of concerns and may in writing request review of the proposed conveyance
32 by the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over parks and lands
33 matters. A member who requests review under this section shall notify the chairs of the
34 joint standing committee of the request.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1151, L.D. 1724
1 When a request for legislative review is received under this section, the director may
2 not finalize the conveyance until the joint standing committee of the Legislature having
3 jurisdiction over parks and lands matters has held a public meeting to review the proposed
4 conveyance.'
5 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
6 section number to read consecutively.
7 SUMMARY
88 This amendment removes from the preamble language relating to the Department of
99 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Parks and Lands, which is not relevant
1100 to this resolve. The amendment also provides that all money received from the sale of the
1111 lot must be deposited into the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry,
1122 Bureau of Parks and Lands, Public Reserved Lands Acquisition Fund rather than entrusting
1133 the funds to Baxter State Park.
14 The amendment requires the Baxter State Park Director to notify owners of property
15 on Long Reach Lane in the Town of Harpswell abutting the Austin Cary Lot and the joint
16 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over parks and lands matters at
17 least 14 days in advance of any public meeting at which the Baxter State Park Authority
18 will consider a proposed conveyance of the Austin Cary Lot. The amendment also provides
19 that a member of the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over
20 parks and lands matters who receives notification may notify the Baxter State Park Director
21 of concerns and may in writing request review of the proposed conveyance by the joint
22 standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over parks and lands matters.
23 When a request for legislative review is received, the director may not finalize the
24 conveyance until the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over
25 parks and lands matters has held a public meeting to review the proposed conveyance.
