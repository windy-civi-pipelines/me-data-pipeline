132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 565
S.P. 244 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Homestead Property Tax Exemption Laws
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 19, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Taxation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BALDACCI of Penobscot.
Cosponsored by Senators: BAILEY of York, RAFFERTY of York, Representative:
MATLACK of St. George.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §681, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2005, c. 647, §2 and affected
3 by §5, is further amended to read:
4 2. Homestead. "Homestead" means any residential property, including cooperative
5 property, in this State assessed as real property owned by an applicant or held in a revocable
6 living trust for the benefit of the applicant and occupied by the applicant as the applicant's
7 permanent residence or owned by a cooperative housing corporation and occupied as a
8 permanent residence by a resident who is a qualifying shareholder. A "homestead" does
9 not include any real property used solely for commercial purposes.
10 SUMMARY
1111 Current law provides that one of the qualifications of a homestead for the homestead
1122 property tax exemption is that real property held in trust must be in a revocable living trust.
1133 This bill removes the requirement that the living trust be revocable.
