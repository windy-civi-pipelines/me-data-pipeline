132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 47
H.P. 11 House of Representatives, January 8, 2025
Crooked Brook Dam
Submitted by the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife pursuant to Joint Rule 204.
Received by the Clerk of the House on January 6, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Inland Fisheries and Wildlife pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint
Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative MASON of Lisbon.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §12457, sub-§1, ¶A, as amended by PL 2013, c. 499, §1, is
3 further amended by amending subparagraph (5) to read:
4 (5) There is no fishing in or from the fishway at the Sheepscot Lake Dam in the
5 Town of Palermo in Waldo County, Chain of Ponds Dam in Chain of Ponds
6 Township in Franklin County, Long Pond Dam in Seven Ponds Township in
7 Franklin County, Beaver Pond Dam in Seven Ponds Township in Franklin County,
8 Little Island Pond Dam in Seven Ponds Township in Franklin County, Pushaw
9 Lake Dam in the Town of Hudson in Penobscot County, Davis Pond Dam in the
10 Town of Eddington in Penobscot County, Leonard's Mills Dam on Blackman
11 Stream in the Town of Bradley in Penobscot County, Souadabscook Stream Dam
12 at Grist Mill Road in the Town of Carmel in Penobscot County and, Webber Pond
13 Dam in the Town of Vassalboro in Kennebec County and Crooked Brook Dam in
14 the Town of Danforth in Washington County;
15 SUMMARY
1166 This bill establishes an exception to the 150-foot fishing setback around the Crooked
1177 Brook Dam, which will allow fishing closer to the fishway but still prohibit fishing in or
1188 from the fishway for safety reasons. The area around the fishway is currently closed to
1199 fishing within 150 feet of the dam.
