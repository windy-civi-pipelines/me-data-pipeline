5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 437, L.D. 1020, “An Act to Repeal the
10 Laws Providing for the Construction of a Connector to Gorham and to Resell Land Taken
11 Under Those Laws to Previous Property Owners”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Repeal the Laws Providing for the Construction of a Connector to Gorham'
14 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 4.
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment removes the provisions requiring the Maine Turnpike Authority to
1199 sell any property that was acquired for the construction of a connector from Route 114 in
2200 South Gorham to an interchange on the Maine Turnpike back to the prior property owner,
2211 or the prior property owner's heirs, assigns or successors, at the same amount for which the
2222 authority purchased the land or to transfer the land to the municipality for no consideration.
2233 FISCAL NOTE REQUIRED
2244 (See attached)
