3 VETERANS AND LEGAL AFFAIRS
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 136, L.D. 213, “An Act to Increase the
10 Sales Commission of Retailers Selling Lottery Tickets”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 8 MRSA §374, sub-§1, ¶K, as amended by PL 2011, c. 310, §7, is further
14 amended to read:
15 K. The In addition to lottery sales compensation as provided in section 374-A, the
16 manner and amount of compensation to be paid to persons licensed to sell lottery tickets
17 or shares necessary to provide for the adequate availability of tickets or shares to
18 prospective buyers and for the convenience of the general public;
19 Sec. 2. 8 MRSA §374-A is enacted to read:
20 §374-A. Lottery sales compensation
21 The commission shall reduce the total amount to be paid in prizes to instant lottery
22 ticket and share winners by 3% and distribute that money to persons licensed to sell lottery
23 tickets or shares proportional to the number of lottery tickets and shares sold.'
24 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
25 number to read consecutively.
26 SUMMARY
2277 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment provides that in addition to
2288 compensation paid as sales commission to persons licensed to sell lottery tickets or shares,
2299 the commission must reduce the total amount to be paid in prizes to instant lottery ticket
3300 and share winners by 3% and distribute that money to persons licensed to sell lottery tickets
3311 or shares proportional to the number of lottery tickets and shares sold.
