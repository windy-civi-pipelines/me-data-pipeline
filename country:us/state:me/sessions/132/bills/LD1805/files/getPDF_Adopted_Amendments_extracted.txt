13 'An Act to Establish a Post-judgment Review Process for Crimes Committed by
14 Victims of Sex Trafficking and Sexual Exploitation'
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in the headnote in the first line (page 1, line 4 in
16 L.D.) by striking out the following: "POST-CONVICTION REVIEW" and inserting the
17 following: 'POST-JUDGMENT MOTION FOR RELIEF'
18 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2402 by striking out all of subsection 2 (page
19 2, lines 23 to 28 in L.D.).
20 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2402 in subsection 3 in the 2nd line (page 2,
21 line 30 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "reverse" and inserting the following: 'nullify'
22 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2402 in subsection 3 in the 4th line (page 2,
23 line 32 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "post-conviction review" and inserting the
24 following: 'post-judgment'
25 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2402 by renumbering the subsections to read
26 consecutively.
27 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2403 in subsection 1 in the 3rd line (page 2,
28 line 38 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "reversed" and inserting the following:
29 'nullified'
30 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2403 in subsection 2 in the first line (page 3,
31 line 1 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "reverse" and inserting the following: 'nullify'
32 Amend the bill in section 1 in c. 312 in §2403 by striking out all of subsection 3 (page
33 3, lines 4 to 8 in L.D.).
