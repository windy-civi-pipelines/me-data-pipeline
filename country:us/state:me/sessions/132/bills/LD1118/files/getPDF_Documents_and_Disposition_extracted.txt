1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §12724 is enacted to read:
3 §12724. Maine Free Community College Program
4 The Maine Free Community College Program, referred to in this section as "the
5 program," is established in accordance with this section.
6 1. Eligibility. The system shall issue a waiver for the payment of tuition and mandatory
7 fees for students eligible for the program pursuant to this subsection. To be eligible for the
8 program, a student must:
9 A. Enroll in an associate degree, diploma or certificate program at a college under
10 section 12714;
11 B. Live in the State for the duration of the enrollment in a college; and
12 C. Accept all available federal and state grants, scholarships and other sources of
13 funding offered to the student;
14 A waiver is valid for up to twice the stated completion time for an eligible student's program
15 of study.
16 2. Report. Beginning January 1, 2027 and annually thereafter, the system shall submit
17 a report to the Governor and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having
18 jurisdiction over education matters and the joint standing committee of the Legislature
19 having jurisdiction over appropriations and financial affairs on the status of the program.
20 SUMMARY
2211 This bill establishes the Maine Free Community College Program, which directs the
2222 Maine Community College System to issue a waiver for the payment of tuition and
2233 mandatory fees for students who are eligible for the program. The bill also establishes
2244 eligibility criteria and directs the Maine Community College System to submit an annual
2255 report to the Governor and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having
2266 jurisdiction over education matters and the joint standing committee of the Legislature
2277 having jurisdiction over appropriations and financial affairs.
