Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 188
An Act to Permit the Sale of Motor Vehicles on Sundays
Reference to the Committee on Transportation suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative MORRIS of Turner.
Cosponsored by Representatives: BOYER of Poland, CHAPMAN of Auburn, LANCE of
Paris, LIBBY of Auburn, POIRIER of Skowhegan.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 17 MRSA §3203, as repealed and replaced by PL 2003, c. 452, Pt. I, §54
3 and affected by Pt. X, §2, is repealed.
4 Sec. 2. 17 MRSA §3203-A, as enacted by PL 1995, c. 87, §2, is repealed.
5 Sec. 3. 29-A MRSA §903, sub-§1, ¶H, as enacted by PL 1993, c. 683, Pt. A, §2
6 and affected by Pt. B, §5, is amended to read:
7 H. Violation of the Maine Unfair Trade Practices Act, or Title 17, section 3203;
8 Sec. 4. 29-A MRSA §1108, sub-§1, ¶G, as enacted by PL 1993, c. 683, Pt. A, §2
9 and affected by Pt. B, §5, is amended to read:
10 G. Violation of Title 5, sections 206 to 212; Title 17, section 3203; or Title 30‑A,
11 sections 3751 to 3760;
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill repeals the current prohibition on sales of motor vehicles on Sunday.
