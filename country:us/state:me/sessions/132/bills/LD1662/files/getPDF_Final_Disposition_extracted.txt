5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1103, L.D. 1662, “An Act to Amend the
10 Laws Regarding Zoning and Land Use Restrictions to Limit Certain Requirements to
11 Municipalities with Populations of More than 10,000”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Amend the Laws Regarding Zoning and Land Use Restrictions to Limit
14 Certain Requirements to Municipalities with Populations of More than 4,000'
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1 in the 5th line (page 1, line 8 in L.D.) by
16 striking out the following: "10,000" and inserting the following: '4,000'
17 Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 2 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 22 in L.D.) by
18 striking out the following: "10,000" and inserting the following: '4,000'
19 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 3.
20 Amend the bill in section 4 in subsection 1-C in the last line (page 1, line 38 in L.D.)
21 by striking out the following: "10,000" and inserting the following: '4,000'
22 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
23 number to read consecutively.
24 SUMMARY
2255 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, changes the population
2266 thresholds provided in the bill so that they apply to municipalities with a population of
2277 more than 4,000. The amendment also removes an unnecessary provision in the bill.
