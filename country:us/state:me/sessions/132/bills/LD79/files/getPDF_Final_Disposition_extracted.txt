H.P. 43 - L.D. 79
the General Wallace H. Nutting Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Main Street Bridge between Biddeford and Saco renamed.
Resolved: That the Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3423, which
crosses the Saco River West Channel on Main Street between the City of Biddeford and
the City of Saco and is currently called the Main Street Bridge, the General Wallace H.
Nutting Memorial Bridge.
