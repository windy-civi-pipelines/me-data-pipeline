4 the City of Saco and is currently called the Main Street Bridge, the General Wallace H.
5 Nutting Memorial Bridge.
6 SUMMARY
77 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3423 on
88 Main Street between the City of Biddeford and the City of Saco the General Wallace H.
99 Nutting Memorial Bridge.
