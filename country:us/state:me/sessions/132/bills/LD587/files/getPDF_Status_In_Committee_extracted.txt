8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to S.P. 270,
9 L.D. 587, “An Act to Require Cardiac Emergency Response Plans and Athletic Emergency
10 Action Plans for Schools and School-sponsored Athletic Events”
11 Amend the amendment by striking out the substitute title and substituting the
12 following:
13 'An Act to Require School Boards to Adopt a Policy on Automated External
14 Defibrillator Access at School-sponsored Athletic Events'
15 Amend the amendment in section 1 in §6304-A in the first indented paragraph in the
16 3rd line (page 1, line 21 in amendment) by striking out the following: "events" and inserting
17 the following: 'athletic events in which students are participating on school property,
18 including team practices'
19 Amend the amendment in section 1 in §6304-A in the first indented paragraph in the
20 4th line (page 1, line 22 in amendment) by striking out the following: "event." and inserting
21 the following: 'athletic event. An automated external defibrillator acquired by a school
22 administrative unit pursuant to this section must be tested and maintained according to the
23 manufacturer's operational guidelines. Appropriate first aid, ambulance, rescue service or
24 other appropriate local emergency medical service providers must be notified of the
25 location and type of automated external defibrillator acquired.'
26 Amend the amendment by inserting after section 1 the following:
27 'Sec. 2. 20-A MRSA §6304-B is enacted to read:
28 §6304-B. Athletic emergency action plans; automated external defibrillator
29 A school administrative unit shall develop and implement an athletic emergency action
30 plan based on recommendations from a nationally recognized heart health organization or
31 nationally recognized athletic training organization that addresses the appropriate, venue-
32 specific use of an automated external defibrillator by school personnel to respond to an
33 individual experiencing sudden cardiac arrest or a similar life-threatening emergency while
34 attending or participating in a school-sponsored athletic event on school property, including
35 a team practice.'
36 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
37 section number to read consecutively.

SENATE AMENDMENT
SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to S.P. 270, L.D. 587
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A." It requires a school
33 administrative unit to develop and implement a policy that provides access to automated
44 external defibrillators at all school-sponsored athletic events in which students are
55 participating on school property, including team practices, as opposed to the committee
66 amendment, which requires the policy to apply to all school-sponsored events.
7 The amendment also adds the following requirements with respect to automated
8 external defibrillators.
9 1. It requires that an automated external defibrillator acquired by a school
10 administrative unit must be tested and maintained according to the manufacturer's
11 operational guidelines.
12 2. It requires that first aid and local emergency medical service providers be notified
13 of the location and type of automated external defibrillator acquired.
14 3. It adds a new section that requires school administrative units to develop and
15 implement an athletic emergency action plan based on recommendations from a nationally
16 recognized heart health organization or nationally recognized athletic training organization
17 that addresses the appropriate, venue-specific use of an automated external defibrillator by
18 school personnel to respond to a cardiac arrest or similar emergency at an athletic event on
19 school property, including a team practice.
