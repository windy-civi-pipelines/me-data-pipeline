2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 EDUCATION AND CULTURAL AFFAIRS
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 270, L.D. 587, “An Act to Require
10 Cardiac Emergency Response Plans and Athletic Emergency Action Plans for Schools and
11 School-sponsored Athletic Events”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Require School Boards to Adopt a Policy on Automated External
14 Defibrillator Access at School Events'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §6304-A is enacted to read:
18 §6304-A. Policy for access to automated external defibrillators
19 Beginning in the 2026-2027 school year, a school administrative unit shall develop and
20 implement a policy that provides access to automated external defibrillators at all school-
21 sponsored events. The policy must include how a person can access an automated external
22 defibrillator while attending a school-sponsored event.'
23 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
24 number to read consecutively.
25 SUMMARY
2266 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires school
2277 administrative units to develop and implement a policy that provides access to automated
2288 external defibrillators at all school-sponsored events. The policy must include how a person
2299 can access an automated external defibrillator while attending a school-sponsored event.
