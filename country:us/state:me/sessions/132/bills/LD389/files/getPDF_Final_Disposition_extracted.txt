1 L.D. 389
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 171, L.D. 389, “Resolve, to Increase
10 Transparency and Evaluate Emergency Response Through a COVID-19 Review
11 Commission”
12 Amend the resolve in the emergency preamble by striking out all of the 2nd paragraph
13 (page 1, lines 3 to 11 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
14 'Whereas, this resolve establishes the COVID-19 Review Commission to study the
15 State's response to the COVID-19 emergency; and'
16 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 5 and inserting the following:
17 'Sec. 5. Duties. Resolved: That the commission shall:
18 1. Study the State's response to coronavirus disease 2019, referred to in this resolve as
19 "COVID-19," and state laws, rules and policies governing that response in the State;
20 2. Review overall primary and secondary health, education and economic effects on
21 communities, including racial and ethnic minority populations, of COVID-19 infections
22 and deaths and the response of the State to mitigate those effects;
23 3. Review state, county and municipal government preparedness and response to the
24 declared COVID-19 emergency;
25 4. Review state and local disease surveillance and detection capabilities; and
26 5. Study any other matters related to the COVID-19 response as required by the
27 commission.'
28 Amend the resolve in section 6 in the 2nd line (page 2, line 32 in L.D.) by inserting
29 after the following: "commission" the following: ', except that Legislative Council staff
30 support is not authorized when the Legislature is in regular or special session'
31 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
32 section number to read consecutively.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 171, L.D. 389
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment clarifies the duties of the COVID-19 Review Commission. It clarifies
33 that Legislative Council staff support is not authorized when the Legislature is in regular
44 or special session.
