2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
4 Reproduced and distributed under the direction of the Clerk of the House.
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 173, L.D. 270, “Resolve, Regarding
10 Legislative Review of Portions of Chapter 850: Health Plan Accountability, a Major
11 Substantive Rule of the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of
12 Insurance”
13 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following:
14 'Sec. 1. Adoption. Resolved: That final adoption of portions of Chapter 850:
15 Health Plan Accountability, a provisionally adopted major substantive rule of the
16 Department of Professional and Financial Regulation, Bureau of Insurance that has been
17 submitted to the Legislature for review pursuant to the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 5,
18 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A, is authorized only if the following changes are made:
19 1. The rule is amended in Section 8(G)(1)(c)(i) to remove the requirement that a
20 written adverse health care treatment decision include the name and title of the person or
21 persons evaluating the appeal and replace it with a requirement that the decision attest to
22 the credentials of the person or persons evaluating the appeal and that the person or persons
23 evaluating the appeal were not involved in the initial decision and a requirement that the
24 carrier identify a point of contact by name, address and telephone number to answer
25 specific questions from the enrollee; and
26 2. The rule is amended in Section 9(B)(2)(b)(i) to remove the requirement that a
27 written adverse benefit determination that does not involve a health care treatment decision
28 include the name and title of the person or persons participating in the grievance review
29 process and replace it with a requirement that the determination attest to the credentials of
30 the person or persons participating in the grievance review process and that the person or
31 persons participating in the grievance review process were not involved in the initial
32 determination and a requirement that the carrier identify a point of contact by name, address
33 and telephone number to answer specific questions from the enrollee.'
34 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
35 section number to read consecutively.
