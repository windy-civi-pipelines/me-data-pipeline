1 L.D. 777
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 INLAND FISHERIES AND WILDLIFE
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 502, L.D. 777, “An Act to Standardize
10 the Muzzle-loading Season Statewide”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'Resolve, Directing the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to Study
13 Lengthening the Muzzle-loading Season for Deer in Certain Wildlife Management
14 Districts'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
16 'Sec. 1. Study of muzzle-loading season for deer and expanding season in
17 certain wildlife management districts. Resolved: That the Department of Inland
18 Fisheries and Wildlife shall study lengthening the muzzle-loading season for deer beyond
19 the 6-day season currently established in department rule in additional wildlife
20 management districts in the State where winter conditions are less severe.
21 Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, by January 1, 2026, the department shall submit a
22 report to the Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife that includes an
23 overview of the study findings pursuant to section 1 and any recommendations to identify
24 additional wildlife management districts where the length of the special muzzle-loading
25 season on deer could be extended. The report must include a map identifying the muzzle-
26 loading season for deer in each wildlife management district in the State and highlight on
27 the map wildlife management districts that are currently permitted by rule to have a longer
28 season than 6 days, wildlife management districts that the department determines could
29 have an extended season and wildlife management districts that the department determines
30 should continue to have a 6-day season. The committee may report out a bill related to the
31 report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.'
32 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
33 number to read consecutively.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 502, L.D. 777
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve. The amendment directs the
33 Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to study lengthening the 6-day muzzle-loading
44 season on deer in wildlife management districts in additional wildlife management districts
55 in the State where winter conditions are less severe. The amendment directs the department
66 to report back to the Joint Standing Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife by January
77 1, 2026 with findings and recommendations and gives the joint standing committee the
88 authority to report out a bill based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd
99 Legislature.
