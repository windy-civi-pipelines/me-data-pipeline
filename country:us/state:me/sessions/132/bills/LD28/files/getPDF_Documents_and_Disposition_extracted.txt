132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 28
S.P. 45 In Senate, January 8, 2025
Duane Bickford Fire Academy
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on January 6, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Criminal Justice and Public Safety pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator CYRWAY of Kennebec.
Cosponsored by Senator: BRADSTREET of Kennebec.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Firefighter training facility in Fairfield renamed. Resolved: That
2 the Town of Fairfield shall designate the firefighter training facility in Fairfield, currently
3 known as the Twin Rivers Fire Academy, the Duane Bickford Fire Academy.
4 SUMMARY
55 This resolve directs the Town of Fairfield to designate the firefighter training facility
66 in Fairfield the Duane Bickford Fire Academy.
