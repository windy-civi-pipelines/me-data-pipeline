9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 63, L.D. 16, “An Act to Establish
10 Separate Prosecutorial Districts in Downeast Maine”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §254, sub-§7, as enacted by PL 1987, c. 737, Pt. A, §2 and
14 Pt. C, §106 and amended by PL 1989, c. 6 and c. 9, §2 and c. 104, Pt. C, §§8 and 10, is
15 further amended to read:
16 7. Prosecutorial District Number 7. There shall be is one district attorney for
17 Hancock and Washington Counties, which shall is to be known as "Prosecutorial District
18 Number 7." The district attorney shall must be elected by the voters of Hancock and
19 Washington Counties in the manner set forth in section 251.
20 This subsection is repealed January 1, 2027.
21 Sec. 2. 30-A MRSA §254, sub-§7-A is enacted to read:
22 7-A. Prosecutorial District Number 7. Beginning January 1, 2027, there is one
23 district attorney for Hancock County, which is to be known as "Prosecutorial District
24 Number 7." The district attorney must be elected by the voters of Hancock County in the
25 manner set forth in section 251.
26 Sec. 3. 30-A MRSA §254, sub-§9 is enacted to read:
27 9. Prosecutorial District Number 9. Beginning January 1, 2027, there is one district
28 attorney for Washington County, which is to be known as "Prosecutorial District Number
29 9." The district attorney must be elected by the voters of Washington County in the manner
30 set forth in section 251.
31 Sec. 4. Prosecutorial District Number 9; district attorney. The first district
32 attorney for Prosecutorial District Number 9 must be elected in the manner set forth in the
33 Maine Revised Statutes, Title 30-A, section 251 in an election held on the Tuesday
34 following the first Monday of November 2026.

14 SUMMARY
1155 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, separates Washington
1166 County from Prosecutorial District Number 7 and creates a new Prosecutorial District
1177 Number 9 for Washington County, effective January 1, 2027. The amendment requires an
1188 election for the district attorney for Prosecutorial District Number 7 to be held on the
1199 Tuesday following the first Monday of November 2026. The amendment also adds an
2200 appropriations and allocations section funding the conversion of one existing prosecutor
2211 position to a district attorney position for Washington County.
