5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1012, L.D. 1554, “An Act to Require
10 Sexual Harassment Prevention Training for Restaurant Employees”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'Resolve, Directing the Department of Labor to Report on Sexual Harassment'
13 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
14 'Sec. 1. Department of Labor to report to Legislature on sexual
15 harassment. Resolved: That the Department of Labor shall submit a report, using
16 available data, to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor detailing compliance and
17 noncompliance by employers with sexual harassment policies in the workplace, including
18 the requirements found in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 26, chapter 7, subchapter 4-B.
19 The department shall include any available data on compliance or noncompliance by
20 employers and any recommendations, including suggested legislation, for ways to
21 strengthen employer compliance with sexual harassment policies and strengthen sexual
22 harassment prevention efforts in the workplace. The report must be submitted no later than
23 January 1, 2026. The Joint Standing Committee on Labor may report out a bill related to
24 the contents of the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.'
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
26 number to read consecutively.
27 SUMMARY
2288 This amendment replaces the bill with a resolve. The resolve directs the Department of
2299 Labor to submit a report, using available data, to the Joint Standing Committee on Labor
3300 detailing compliance and noncompliance by employers with sexual harassment policies in
3311 the workplace. The department must include any recommendations, including suggested
3322 legislation, on ways to strengthen employer compliance with sexual harassment policies
3333 and strengthen sexual harassment prevention efforts in the workplace. The Joint Standing
