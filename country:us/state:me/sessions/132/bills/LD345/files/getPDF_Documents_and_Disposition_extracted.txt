Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 345
Resolve, to Rename a Bridge in the Town of Roque Bluffs the Hope
Bridge
Received by the Clerk of the House on January 30, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative STROUT of Harrington.
Cosponsored by Senator MOORE of Washington.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Roque Bluffs renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
2 Transportation shall designate Bridge 3964 on Johnson Cove Road in the Town of Roque
3 Bluffs, which crosses the Englishman River and is currently known as the Englishman
4 River Bridge, the Hope Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3964 on
77 Johnson Cove Road in the Town of Roque Bluffs the Hope Bridge.
