Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1083
H.P. 705 House of Representatives, March 18, 2025
Assess Existing Water Retention Structures and Water Levels
Received by the Clerk of the House on March 14, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Environment and Natural Resources pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant
to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative MASON of Lisbon.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Water retention structures assessment. Resolved: That the Department
2 of Environmental Protection shall assess the water retention structures, including dams, in
3 the State to examine erosion issues and other effects of rising water levels. The department
4 shall also assess water levels in lakes and ponds that contain water retention structures.
5 Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, by December 3, 2025, the Department of
6 Environmental Protection shall submit a report based on its assessment in section 1 for
7 presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
8 SUMMARY
99 This resolve directs the Department of Environmental Protection to assess the water
1100 retention structures, including dams, in the State to examine erosion issues and other effects
1111 of rising water levels and to also assess water levels in lakes and ponds that contain water
1122 retention structures. The department is required to submit a report based on its assessment
1133 by December 3, 2025 for presentation to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd
1144 Legislature.
