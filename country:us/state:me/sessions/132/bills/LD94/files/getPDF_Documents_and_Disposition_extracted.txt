132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 94
H.P. 59 House of Representatives, January 8, 2025
Reference to the Committee on Health and Human Services suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative GRAHAM of North Yarmouth.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §1596, as amended by PL 2023, c. 416, §1, is further amended
3 by amending the section headnote to read:
4 §1596. Abortion and miscarriage data
5 Sec. 2. 22 MRSA §1596, sub-§1, ¶B, as repealed and replaced by PL 1989, c. 274,
6 §1, is repealed.
7 Sec. 3. 22 MRSA §1596, sub-§3, as amended by PL 2019, c. 262, §1, is repealed.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill eliminates the requirement that health care professionals must report to the
1100 Department of Health and Human Services each occurrence of a miscarriage of a fetus of
1111 less than 20 weeks gestation.
