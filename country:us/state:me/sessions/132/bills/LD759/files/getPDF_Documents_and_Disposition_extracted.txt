132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 759
S.P. 298 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Reference to the Committee on Health and Human Services suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator HICKMAN of Kennebec.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §1834 is enacted to read:
3 §1834. Side rails for beds in long-term care facilities
4 A long-term care facility shall equip a resident's bed with side rails if the resident or
5 the resident's legal guardian requests side rails or if side rails are necessary for the safety
6 of the resident. For purposes of this section, "long-term care facility" has the same meaning
7 as in section 2421-A, subsection 25.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill requires a long-term care facility to equip a resident's bed with side rails if the
1100 resident or the resident's legal guardian requests side rails or if side rails are necessary for
1111 the safety of the resident.
