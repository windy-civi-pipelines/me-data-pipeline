11 SUMMARY
1122 This amendment clarifies that the authority may receive, invest and expend on behalf
1133 of the fund money in the Logging and Fishing Enterprise Loan Fund established in the bill
1144 from gifts, grants and bequests as well as from state appropriations and allocations. The
1155 amendment also increases the administrative costs the Finance Authority of Maine may
1166 charge the fund. The amendment directs the authority to incorporate certain information
1177 on the program into the annual report the authority is required to submit to the Legislature.
1188 The amendment also makes technical changes to the bill.
