Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 248
S.P. 115 In Senate, January 21, 2025
Examiner the Director of Medical Examiner Operations
Submitted by the Department of the Attorney General pursuant to Joint Rule 204.
Reference to the Committee on Judiciary suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator CARNEY of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3022, sub-§2-A, as amended by PL 2023, c. 412, Pt. FF, §1, is
3 further amended to read:
4 2-A. Appointment of office administrator Director of Medical Examiner
5 Operations. The Chief Medical Examiner may appoint one office administrator a Director
6 of Medical Examiner Operations, who shall serve at the pleasure of the Chief Medical
7 Examiner. The office administrator Director of Medical Examiner Operations shall
8 perform such duties as may be delegated by the Chief Medical Examiner. Notwithstanding
9 any other provisions provision of law to the contrary, the compensation of the Chief
10 Medical Examiner's office administrator Director of Medical Examiner Operations must
11 be fixed by the Chief Medical Examiner subject to approval by the Attorney General.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill renames the office administrator of the Office of Chief Medical Examiner
1144 within the Department of the Attorney General the Director of Medical Examiner
1155 Operations.
