9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 562, L.D. 876, “An Act to Support Rural
10 Resilience and Emergency Response Through the Maine Service Fellows Program”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Support the Maine Service Fellows Program'
13 Amend the bill by striking out all of sections 1, 2 and 3.
14 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
15 number to read consecutively.
16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment removes the provision of the bill directing the Maine Commission for
1188 Community Service, in conjunction with the Department of Defense, Veterans and
1199 Emergency Management, to compile a list of volunteers who are available at the request of
2200 the Governor during states of emergency to provide emergency response and support
2211 services in coordination with the Maine Emergency Management Agency.
