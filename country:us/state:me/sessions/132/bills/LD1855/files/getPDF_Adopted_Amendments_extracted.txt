3 VETERANS AND LEGAL AFFAIRS
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1239, L.D. 1855, “An Act to Impose an
10 Excise Tax on Spirits-based Cocktails Containing No More than 12 Percent Alcohol”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Repeal the Low-alcohol Spirits Product Tax'
13 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
14 following:
15 'Sec. 1. 28-A MRSA §1365, as amended by PL 2021, c. 658, §231, is repealed.'
16 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
17 number to read consecutively.
18 SUMMARY
1199 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. Current law requires each
2200 certificate of approval holder that manufactures low-alcohol spirits products to pay a tax of
2211 30¢ on each gallon of low-alcohol spirits product sold to a wholesale licensee. The
2222 amendment repeals the law establishing that tax.
