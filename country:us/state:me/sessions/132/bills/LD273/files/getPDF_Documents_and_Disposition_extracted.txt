Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 273
An Act to Preserve Legislative Authority over Investigating
Committees
Reference to the Committee on State and Local Government suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative LEE of Auburn.
Cosponsored by Senator HAGGAN of Penobscot and
Representatives: ABDI of Lewiston, HENDERSON of Rumford, LOOKNER of Portland,
MILLIKEN of Blue Hill, POIRIER of Skowhegan, ROEDER of Bangor, SINCLAIR of Bath,
SUPICA of Bangor.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 3 MRSA §411, as enacted by PL 1975, c. 593, §3, is amended to read:
3 §411. Creation
4 Whenever When the Legislature delegates to a committee the power to administer
5 oaths, issue subpoenas and take depositions in connection with any study or investigation,
6 such that committee shall automatically become becomes an investigating committee for
7 the purpose of such the study or investigation and shall be is subject to the provisions of
8 this chapter, whether or not such that power is utilized used by the committee in the course
9 of such the study or investigation. When the Legislature delegates to a committee the
10 power to administer oaths, issue subpoenas and take depositions, the Legislature has the
11 sole authority to determine the membership and scope of that committee.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This bill amends the laws governing legislative investigating committees to provide
1144 that when the Legislature delegates to a committee the power to administer oaths, issue
1155 subpoenas and take depositions, the Legislature has the sole authority to determine the
1166 membership and scope of that committee.
