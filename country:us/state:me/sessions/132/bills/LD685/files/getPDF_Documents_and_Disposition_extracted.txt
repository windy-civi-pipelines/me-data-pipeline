132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 685
S.P. 302 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Memorial Bridge
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 20, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator LIBBY of Cumberland.
Cosponsored by Representative WALKER of Naples.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Sebago renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
2 Transportation shall designate Bridge 6230 on Sebago Road in the Town of Sebago, which
3 crosses the Northwest River and is currently known as the Mill Pond Bridge, the Sebago
4 Veterans Memorial Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 6230 on
77 Sebago Road in the Town of Sebago the Sebago Veterans Memorial Bridge.
