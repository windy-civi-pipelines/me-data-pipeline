132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 569
S.P. 249 In Senate, February 25, 2025
Memorial Bridge
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 19, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator LIBBY of Cumberland.
Cosponsored by Representative: LANIGAN of Sanford.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Shapleigh renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
2 Transportation shall designate Bridge 3637 on Route 11 in the Town of Shapleigh, which
3 crosses Pump Box Brook and is currently known as the Shapleigh Bridge, the Shapleigh
4 Veterans Memorial Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 3637 on
77 Route 11 in the Town of Shapleigh the Shapleigh Veterans Memorial Bridge.
