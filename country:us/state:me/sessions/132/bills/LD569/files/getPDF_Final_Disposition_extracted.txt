Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Shapleigh renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
Transportation shall designate Bridge 3637 on Route 11 in the Town of Shapleigh, which
crosses Pump Box Brook and is currently known as the Shapleigh Bridge, the Shapleigh
Veterans Memorial Bridge.
