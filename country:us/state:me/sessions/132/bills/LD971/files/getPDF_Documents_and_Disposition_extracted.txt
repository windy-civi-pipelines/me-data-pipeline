2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §12555, as repealed and replaced by PL 2003, c. 655, Pt. B, §265
3 and affected by §422, is repealed.
4 SUMMARY
55 This bill repeals the law that requires a person, while trapping for baitfish in inland
66 waters of the State with the use of a baitfish trap, to check the baitfish trap or cause the trap
77 to be checked at least once in every 7 days.
