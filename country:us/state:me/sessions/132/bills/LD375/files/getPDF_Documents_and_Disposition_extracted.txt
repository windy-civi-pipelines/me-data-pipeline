132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 375
S.P. 170 In Senate, February 3, 2025
an Online Degree Program
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on January 30, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Education and Cultural Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator STEWART of Aroostook.
Cosponsored by Senators: BAILEY of York, CARNEY of Cumberland, DUSON of
Cumberland, LIBBY of Cumberland, RAFFERTY of York.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. University of Maine System to establish online degree program at
3 the University of Maine School of Law. The University of Maine System shall
4 establish an online degree program at the University of Maine School of Law. By
5 November 4, 2026, the University of Maine System shall submit a report related to the
6 establishment of the online degree program to the joint standing committee of the
7 Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill requires the University of Maine System to establish an online degree program
1100 at the University of Maine School of Law. By November 4, 2026, the University of Maine
1111 System must submit a report related to the establishment of the online degree program to
1122 the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over education matters.
