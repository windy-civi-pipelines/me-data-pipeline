9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 838, L.D. 1263, “An Act Regarding

12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act Regarding Penalties for Fentanyl Trafficking When That Trafficking Results
14 in Serious Bodily Injury of a Person'
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph L in the 3rd line (page 1, line 6 in L.D.) by
16 inserting after the following: "W drug" the following: ', other than fentanyl powder'
17 Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph L-1 in the first line (page 1, line 9 in L.D.) by
18 striking out the following: "resulting from an overdose"
19 Amend the bill in section 2 in paragraph L-1 in the last line (page 1, line 12 in L.D.)
20 by striking out the following: "resulting from the overdose"
21 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
22 number to read consecutively.
23 SUMMARY
2244 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, changes the title. It
2255 amends the bill to remove the requirement that the serious bodily injury is the result of an
2266 overdose.
