8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 414, L.D. 984, “An Act to Require Every
10 State and Municipal Building to Have at Least One Automated External Defibrillator”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Require State-owned or Leased Buildings Occupied by 50 or More
13 Executive Branch Employees to Have at Least One Automated External Defibrillator
14 and One Bleeding Control Kit'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §1755 is enacted to read:
18 §1755. Automated external defibrillators and bleeding control kits
19 1. Definitions. As used in this section, unless the context otherwise indicates, the
20 following terms have the following meanings.
21 A. "Automated external defibrillator" has the same meaning as in Title 22, section
22 2150-C, subsection 1, paragraph A.
23 B. "Bleeding control kit" means a first aid response kit that contains, at a minimum:
24 (1) A commercial grade tourniquet endorsed by a combat casualty care committee
25 under the United States Department of Defense;
26 (2) Wound-packing gauze;
27 (3) A compression bandage;
28 (4) A hemostatic dressing endorsed by a combat casualty care committee under the
29 United States Department of Defense;
30 (5) A pair of nonlatex gloves;
31 (6) A pair of scissors;
32 (7) A marker pen;
Page 1 - 132LR1031(02)
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 414, L.D. 984
1 (8) Instructional documents on bleeding control developed by the United States
2 Department of Defense or by a nationally recognized professional medical
3 association for surgeons or a successor organization; and
4 (9) Any additional item that:
5 (a) Is approved by a law enforcement agency or emergency medical services
6 provider;
7 (b) Can adequately treat a traumatic injury; and
8 (c) Can be stored in a bleeding control kit.
9 C. "State-owned building" means a nonresidential building owned or leased by the
10 State occupied by 50 or more executive branch employees performing job duties.
11 2. Automated external defibrillators and bleeding control kits. The Department
12 of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General Services shall ensure that each
13 state-owned building is equipped with at least one automated external defibrillator and one
14 bleeding control kit, subject to sufficient funding. Any ongoing maintenance costs for the
15 automated external defibrillators and bleeding control kits must be incorporated into the
16 operating expenses of the building in which they are located.
17 3. Funding. If insufficient funding is appropriated to meet the requirements of
18 subsection 2, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General
19 Services may accept any public or private funds that may be available for carrying out the
20 purposes of subsection 2.
21 Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
22 allocations are made.
23 ADMINISTRATIVE AND FINANCIAL SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
24 Bureau of General Services - Capital Construction and Improvement Reserve Fund
25 0883
26 Initiative: Provides funding to equip each state-owned or leased building managed by the
27 executive branch that has 50 or more employees with at least one automated external
28 defibrillator and one bleeding control kit.

36 SUMMARY
3377 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It requires that the Department
3388 of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General Services ensure that each
3399 state-owned building managed by the executive branch or building leased and managed by
4400 the executive branch that is occupied by 50 or more state employees is equipped with at

411 least one automated external defibrillator and one bleeding control kit, subject to sufficient
422 funding. It also provides that if insufficient funding is appropriated to meet these
433 requirements, the Department of Administrative and Financial Services, Bureau of General
444 Services is authorized to accept any public or private funds that may be available for
455 carrying out these requirements.
