9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1007, L.D. 1522, “An Act to Establish
10 the Maine Eviction Prevention Program”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in sub-c. 15 in §4994-D in subsection 1 in the first 2 lines
12 (page 1, lines 9 and 10 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "and administer"
13 Amend the bill in section 1 in sub-c. 15 in §4994-D by striking out all of subsection 2
14 (page 1, lines 16 to 18 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
15 '2. Rental assistance. A participant in the program is eligible to receive up to $800
16 per month in rental assistance paid directly to the participant's landlord for up to 12 months.'
17 Amend the bill in section 1 in sub-c. 15 in §4994-D by inserting after subsection 6 the
18 following:
19 '7. Program administration. The Maine State Housing Authority shall delegate
20 administration of the program and provide appropriate funding to one or more of the
21 following: a designated community action agency as described by Title 22, section 5324;
22 a municipal housing authority; or any other qualified entity as determined by the Maine
23 State Housing Authority. An entity delegated to administer the program shall develop a
24 method of verifying a participant's eligibility for the rental assistance every 6 months after
25 initial approval. An entity delegated to administer the program shall limit administrative
26 costs of the program to 12% or less of distributions received.
27 8. Rules. The Maine State Housing Authority may adopt rules to implement the
28 provisions of this subchapter. Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are routine
29 technical rules as defined in Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.'
30 Amend the bill in section 1 in sub-c. 15 in §4994-D by striking out all of the last
31 indented paragraph (page 1, lines 35 to 37 in L.D.).
32 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following:
33 'Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
34 allocations are made.

12 SUMMARY
1133 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, requires the Maine
1144 State Housing Authority to delegate administration of the Maine Eviction Prevention
1155 Program established in the bill and allows, but does not require, the authority to adopt rules
1166 to implement the program. The amendment changes the assistance provided under the
1177 program to up to $800 per month paid directly to the participant's landlord for up to 12
1188 months. It also reduces the ongoing appropriation from $25,000,000 to $10,000,000 in
1199 each fiscal year of the biennium.
