1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 37-B MRSA ยง1130, sub-ยง5 is enacted to read:
3 5. Loan requests. A municipality or quasi-municipal corporation or district may
4 request a loan pursuant to subsection 2 of up to $1,000,000 per application with a payback
5 period between 5 and 30 years, but in no case may the payback period be longer than the
6 useful life of the proposed project. The department may require a municipality or quasi-
7 municipal corporation or district to provide matching funds in order to be considered for a
8 loan under this section. The department may establish a loan forgiveness program for loans
9 granted under this section.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill establishes a maximum limit of $1,000,000 per loan application under the
1122 Dam Repair and Reconstruction Fund and establishes a loan payback period between 5 and
1133 30 years, but no longer than the useful life of the proposed project. The bill also allows the
1144 department to require an applicant to provide matching funds in order to be considered for
1155 a loan and allows the department to establish a loan forgiveness program.
