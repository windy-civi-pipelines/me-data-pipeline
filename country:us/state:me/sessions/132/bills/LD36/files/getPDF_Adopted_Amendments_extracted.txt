14 number to read consecutively.
15 SUMMARY
1166 This amendment amends the bill to provide that, for the purposes of the uncontrolled
1177 hazardous substance site laws, the Board of Environmental Protection must hold a hearing
1188 on an order regarding an uncontrolled hazardous substance site as expeditiously as possible,
1199 but in no case later than 90 days after receipt of an application for a hearing.
