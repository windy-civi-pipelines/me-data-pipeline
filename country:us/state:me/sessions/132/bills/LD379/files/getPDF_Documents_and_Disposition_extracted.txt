Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 379
S.P. 138 In Senate, February 4, 2025
Fire Emergency Response and Planning
Submitted by the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry pursuant to Joint
Rule 204.
Reference to the Committee on Judiciary suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BLACK of Franklin.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA §8904, sub-§1 is enacted to read:
3 1. Confidentiality. Emergency plans of action that contain any of the following
4 information are confidential and exempt from public records requirements pursuant to Title
5 1, chapter 13:
6 A. Personal contact information;
7 B. Information related to gaining access to gates, locks or roads;
8 C. Proprietary information of a landowner; or
9 D. Emergency response information acquired by the director through forest fire
10 preparedness and planning activities.
11 Sec. 2. 12 MRSA §8904, sub-§2 is enacted to read:
12 2. Disclosure of confidential information. The director may disclose information
13 that is considered confidential under subsection 1 to another state agency for the purposes
14 of emergency incident response and planning.
15 SUMMARY
1166 This bill modifies the provisions of law governing forest fire control to clarify that
1177 certain emergency plans of action formulated by the Department of Agriculture,
1188 Conservation and Forestry, Bureau of Forestry are considered confidential and are exempt
1199 from public records requests under the Freedom of Access Act if they contain any of the
2200 following: personal contact information; information related to gaining access to gates,
2211 locks or roads; proprietary information of a landowner; and emergency response
2222 information acquired by the Director of the Bureau of Forestry. The director may disclose
2233 confidential information to another state agency for the purposes of emergency incident
2244 response and planning.
