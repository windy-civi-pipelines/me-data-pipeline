S.P. 59 - L.D. 37
S. Cash Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Old Orchard Beach renamed. Resolved: That the
Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3643 on Seaside Avenue in the Town
of Old Orchard Beach, which crosses Goose Fare Creek and is currently known as the
Goose Fare Bridge, the Captain Christopher S. Cash Memorial Bridge.
