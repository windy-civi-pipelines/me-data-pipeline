132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1663
H.P. 1104 House of Representatives, April 15, 2025
Data Organization
Reference to the Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial Services
suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative MORRIS of Turner.
Cosponsored by Representative: LIBBY of Auburn.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §8720 is enacted to read:
3 §8720. Reporting of average amount paid by public payors
4 The organization shall report the average amount paid by public payors, including but
5 not limited to Medicare and the MaineCare program, on its publicly accessible website that
6 reports the average amount paid for common health care procedures at different health care
7 settings in the same manner that it reports the average amount paid by commercial payors.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill requires the Maine Health Data Organization to report the average amount
1100 paid by public payors, including but not limited to Medicare and the MaineCare program,
1111 on its publicly accessible website that reports the average amount paid for common health
1122 care procedures at different health care settings in the same manner that it reports the
1133 average amount paid by commercial payors.
