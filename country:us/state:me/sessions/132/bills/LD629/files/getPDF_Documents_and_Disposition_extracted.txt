An Act to Restore Matching Funding to the Maine Development
Foundation
Received by the Clerk of the House on February 20, 2025. Referred to the Committee on

pursuant to Joint Rule 401.

Printed on recycled paper

3 allocations are made.
4 DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION, MAINE
5 Development Foundation 0198
6 Initiative: Provides ongoing funds to restore matching funds for private contributions to
7 support the foundation's economic, workforce and community development initiatives
8 across the State, particularly in distressed communities and industries.
9 GENERAL FUND 2025-26 2026-27
10 All Other $500,000 $500,000
11 __________ __________
12 GENERAL FUND TOTAL $500,000 $500,000
13
9 SUMMARY
1140 This bill provides ongoing funds to restore matching funds for private contributions to

1162 development initiatives across the State, particularly in distressed communities and
