Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1447
S.P. 588 In Senate, April 3, 2025
Expedite Reimbursement of All Vendors
Reference to the Committee on Health and Human Services suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator STEWART of Aroostook.
Cosponsored by Representative HENDERSON of Rumford and
Senator: BERNARD of Aroostook, Representatives: BABIN of Fort Fairfield, CARUSO of
Caratunk.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services to reimburse claims
2 after receipt of cost reports. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human
3 Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual to require the
4 department to reimburse at least 75% of a submitted claim for services rendered within 90
5 days of receipt.
6 SUMMARY
77 This resolve requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules
88 in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual to require the department to reimburse at least
99 75% of a submitted claim for services rendered within 90 days of receipt.
