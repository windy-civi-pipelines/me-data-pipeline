7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 998, L.D. 1514, “An Act to Change the
10 Laws Regarding Real Estate Appraisers”
11 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1 and inserting the following:
12 'Sec. 1. 32 MRSA §14011, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2007, c. 402, Pt. GG, §3, is
13 further amended to read:
14 2. Members. The board consists of 7 9 members appointed by the Governor. Each
15 member must be a resident of this State. The board consists of:
16 A. Two public members as defined in Title 5, section 12004‑A members who are
17 actively engaged in the business of banking, including lending for the purpose of
18 buying real property, or who are consumers of appraisals in the regular course of their
19 business; and
20 B. Five members who hold valid appraiser licenses, including at least one member
21 who holds a certified general license. and one member who is a certified residential
22 licensee at the time of the member's initial appointment;
23 C. One member who is actively engaged in the business of real estate brokerage as
24 defined in section 13001, subsection 2; and
25 D. One member who is employed by a credit union and who is actively engaged in the
26 mortgaging of real property as governed by Title 33, chapter 9.'
27 Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 1 in the 3rd line (page 1, line 17 in L.D.) by
28 striking out the following: "having a transaction value of less than $1,000,000" and
29 inserting the following: 'having a transaction value of less than $1,000,000'
30 Amend the bill in section 2 in subsection 1 in the 4th line (page 1, line 18 in L.D.) by
31 striking out the following: "having a transaction value of less than $250,000 $400,000" and
32 inserting the following: 'having a transaction value of less than $250,000 as specified in the
33 criteria adopted and published by the appraiser qualifications board in effect at the time of
34 the transaction'

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 998, L.D. 1514
1 Amend the bill in section 3 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 28 in L.D.) by inserting after
2 the following: "member of the" the following: 'Department of Professional and Financial
3 Regulation,'
4 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
5 number to read consecutively.
6 SUMMARY
77 This amendment increases the membership of the Department of Professional and
88 Financial Regulation, Board of Real Estate Appraisers from 7 to 9 members and amends
99 the qualifications for certain members of the board. It also amends the scope of license
1100 provisions for residential real property appraisers by providing that a license holder may
1111 appraise certain residential property as specified in criteria adopted by the appraiser
1122 qualifications board.
