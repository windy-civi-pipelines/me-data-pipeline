Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 26, 2025. Referred to the Committee

DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator CARNEY of Cumberland.
Cosponsored by Representative KUHN of Falmouth.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. Transfer to Department of Health and Human Services, Office of
3 Behavioral Health. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, on or before
4 June 30, 2026, the State Controller shall transfer $561,283 from the Department of Health
5 and Human Services, Opioid Use Disorder Prevention and Treatment Fund, Other Special
6 Revenue Funds account to the Department of Health and Human Services, Office of
7 Behavioral Health, Other Special Revenue Funds account.
8 Sec. 2. Transfer to unappropriated surplus of General Fund.
9 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, on or before June 30, 2026, the State
10 Controller shall transfer $179,500 from the Department of Health and Human Services,
11 Opioid Use Disorder Prevention and Treatment Fund, Other Special Revenue Funds
12 account to the unappropriated surplus of the General Fund.
13 Sec. 3. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
14 allocations are made.
15 ATTORNEY GENERAL, DEPARTMENT OF THE
16 District Attorneys Salaries 0409
17 Initiative: Appropriates one-time funds to establish one limited-period Assistant District
