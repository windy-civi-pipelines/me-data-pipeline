132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1527
S.P. 611 In Senate, April 8, 2025
Return of Absentee Ballots
Reference to the Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BENNETT of Oxford.
Cosponsored by Speaker FECTEAU of Biddeford.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 21-A MRSA §752, first ¶, as amended by PL 1985, c. 357, §8, is further
3 amended to read:
4 At least 3 months before any election, the Secretary of State shall furnish each
5 municipality with a reasonable number of dated absentee ballot applications. A reasonable
6 time, not less than 30 days unless an emergency exists, before any election, the Secretary
7 of State shall furnish each municipality with a reasonable number of absentee ballots and
8 postage prepaid return envelopes.
9 SUMMARY
1100 This bill requires the Secretary of State, when providing municipalities with absentee
1111 ballots, to also provide postage prepaid return envelopes for those absentee ballots.
