Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 331
Resolve, Directing the Department of Health and Human Services to
Ensure Timely Reimbursement Under MaineCare Regarding
Hospital Cost Reports
Received by the Clerk of the House on January 30, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Health and Human Services pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint
Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative DRINKWATER of Milford.
Cosponsored by Senator BERNARD of Aroostook and
Representatives: COLLAMORE of Pittsfield, DUCHARME of Madison, FOLEY of Wells,
HAGGAN of Hampden, HALL of Wilton, MASON of Lisbon, POIRIER of Skowhegan,
QUINT of Hodgdon.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Department of Health and Human Services to reimburse hospitals
2 after receipt of cost reports. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human
3 Services shall amend its rules in Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III,
4 Section 45, Hospital Services, to require the department to reimburse at least 75% of the
5 as-filed settlement pursuant to a hospital's cost reports within 90 days of receipt.
6 Sec. 2. Existing resources. Resolved: That the Department of Health and Human
7 Services shall implement the requirements of this resolve within existing resources.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to amend its rules in
1100 Chapter 101: MaineCare Benefits Manual, Chapter III, Section 45, Hospital Services, to
1111 require the department to reimburse at least 75% of the as-filed settlement pursuant to a
1122 hospital's cost reports within 90 days of receipt. It requires the department to accomplish
1133 this within existing resources.
