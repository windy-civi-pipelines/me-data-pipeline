9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 231, L.D. 331, “Resolve, Directing the
10 Department of Health and Human Services to Ensure Timely Reimbursement Under
11 MaineCare Regarding Hospital Cost Reports”
12 Amend the resolve by inserting after the title the following:
13 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
14 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
15 Whereas, Maine hospitals are currently experiencing extreme financial pressures,
16 exacerbated by the MaineCare funding shortfall; and
17 Whereas, certain MaineCare reimbursements to hospitals are made years after service
18 is delivered; and
19 Whereas, accelerating MaineCare reimbursements to hospitals before the expiration
20 of the 90-day period will help to alleviate the acute financial strain on the health care system
21 in this State; and
22 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
23 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
24 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
25 therefore, be it'
26 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 2 and inserting the following:
27 'Sec. 2. Appropriations and allocations. Resolved: That the following
28 appropriations and allocations are made.
29 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
30 Department of Health and Human Services Central Operations 0142
31 Initiative: Provides funding to establish one Staff Auditor II position to review and verify
32 hospital cost reports and prepare payments for settlement. The initiative also provides
33 funding for related All Other costs.
