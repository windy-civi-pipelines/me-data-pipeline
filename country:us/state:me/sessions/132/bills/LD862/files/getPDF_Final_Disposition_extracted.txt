H.P. 548 - L.D. 862
Crossing Bridge the Scarborough Veterans Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Scarborough renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
Transportation shall designate Bridge 5260 on Pine Point Road, which crosses the Boston
and Maine Railroad and Snow Canning Road in the Town of Scarborough and is currently
known as the Pine Point Crossing Bridge, the Scarborough Veterans Memorial Bridge.
