Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 509
S.P. 227 In Senate, February 11, 2025
Electricians
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 7, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Labor pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator TIMBERLAKE of Androscoggin.
Cosponsored by Representative LYMAN of Livermore Falls and
Senators: BICKFORD of Androscoggin, BLACK of Franklin, CYRWAY of Kennebec,
FARRIN of Somerset.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 32 MRSA §1202-B, sub-§4, ¶A, as enacted by PL 2023, c. 327, §2, is
3 amended to read:
4 A. A journeyman electrician may make electrical installations while employed by a
5 supervising master electrician, limited electrician or electrical company. A person
6 licensed under this subsection may supervise:
7 (1) Two helper electricians who are enrolled in, or have completed, a 576-hour
8 course of study as adopted by the board;
9 (2) Two apprentice electricians; or
10 (3) One Three helper electrician electricians.
11 SUMMARY
1122 This bill changes the number of helper electricians who may be supervised by a
1133 journeyman electrician. Under current law, a journeyman electrician may supervise one
1144 helper electrician or 2 helper electricians if they are enrolled in or have completed a certain
1155 educational program. This bill eliminates this distinction and allows a journeyman
1166 electrician to supervise 3 helper electricians.
