5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 758, L.D. 1951, “An Act to Promote
10 Food Processing and Manufacturing Facility Expansion and Create Jobs”
11 Amend the bill in section 8 in paragraph A in the 7th line (page 2, line 7 in L.D.) by
12 striking out the following: "2027" and inserting the following: '2026'
13 Amend the bill by inserting after section 11 the following:
14 'Sec. 12. 36 MRSA §5219-VV, sub-§5, ¶A, as amended by PL 2019, c. 607, Pt.
15 C, §7 and c. 659, Pt. H, §6, is further amended to read:
16 A. On or before March 1st of each year, a certified applicant shall file a report with
17 the commissioner for the tax year ending during the immediately preceding calendar
18 year, referred to in this subsection as "the report year," containing year." The report
19 must include, at a minimum, metrics and other progress measures, including the
20 following information:
21 (1) The number of full‑time employees based in the State of the certified applicant
22 on the last day of the report year; and
23 (2) The incremental amount of qualified investment made in the report year.;
24 (3) The total number of full-time employees based in the State in the report year;
25 (4) The number of jobs offered by the certified applicant during the report year
26 above the base level of employment; and
27 (5) The number of jobs that have been added since the day the certificate of
28 approval was issued.
29 The commissioner may prescribe forms for the annual report described in this
30 paragraph. The commissioner shall provide copies of the report to the assessor, to the
31 Office of Program Evaluation and Government Accountability and to the joint standing
32 committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over taxation matters at the time the
33 report is received.'
