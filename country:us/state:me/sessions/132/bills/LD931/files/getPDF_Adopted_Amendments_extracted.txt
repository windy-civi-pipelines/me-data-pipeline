1 L.D. 931

3 CRIMINAL JUSTICE AND PUBLIC SAFETY
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 596, L.D. 931, “An Act to Amend the
10 Law Allowing Incarcerated Pretrial or Presentence Individuals to Be Credited Time for
11 Participation in Voluntary Work Projects in a Jail”
12 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'An Act to Amend the Law Allowing Individuals Subject to Pretrial or Presentence
14 Incarceration to Be Credited Time for Participation in Work Projects Within a Jail'
15 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
16 following:
17 'Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §1606, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2021, c. 169, §1, is further
18 amended to read:
19 2. Sentence prorated. Inmates participating in a public works-related project or an
20 improvement of property owned by a charitable organization under subsection 1 and
21 inmates participating in a work project within a jail under subsection 1-B may have their
22 sentences to the jail prorated at the rate of up to one day removed from the sentences for
23 every 16 hours of participation in the project, except that inmates committed to the custody
24 of the sheriff for nonpayment of fines under Title 17‑A, section 1711 must have their
25 sentences prorated at the rate that is applicable to the individual inmate pursuant to Title
26 17‑A, section 1711, subsection 4, paragraph A, subparagraph (1).'
27 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
28 number to read consecutively.
29 SUMMARY
3300 This amendment replaces the bill and provides that an inmate who is detained at a
3311 county jail or regional jail pretrial or presentence and who voluntarily participates in work
3322 projects within the jail is eligible to have the inmate's sentence to the jail prorated at the
3333 rate of up to one day removed from the sentence for every 16 hours of participation in the
3344 project.
