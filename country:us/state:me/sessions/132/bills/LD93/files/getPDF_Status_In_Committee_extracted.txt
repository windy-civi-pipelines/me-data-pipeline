8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 58,
9 L.D. 93, “An Act to Reduce Cost and Increase Access to Disease Prevention by Expanding
10 the Universal Childhood Immunization Program to Include Adults”
11 Amend the amendment in section 10 in paragraph E in the 3rd line (page 1, line 34 in
12 amendment) by inserting after the following: "1st." the following: 'The board shall develop
13 the list to enable providers to make meaningful choices among available vaccine products.'
14 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
15 section number to read consecutively.
16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment amends Committee Amendment "A" by requiring that the Maine
1188 Vaccine Board, in developing the list of vaccines to be made available by the Universal
1199 Immunization Program, must develop the list to enable providers to make meaningful
2200 choices among available vaccine products.
