Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 657
An Act to Modify the Law Governing Municipal General Assistance
by Designating Broadband Internet Access Service and Wireless
Access Point Technology as Basic Necessities
Received by the Clerk of the House on February 20, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Health and Human Services pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint
Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative SIMMONS of Waldoboro.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §4301, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2019, c. 126, §2, is further
3 amended to read:
4 1. Basic necessities. "Basic necessities" means food, potable water, clothing, shelter,
5 fuel, electricity, nonelective medical services as recommended by a physician,
6 nonprescription drugs, telephone where when it is necessary for medical reasons,
7 broadband Internet access service, wireless access point technology and any other
8 commodity or service determined essential by the overseer in accordance with the
9 municipality's ordinance and this chapter. "Basic necessities" do does not include security
10 deposits for rental property, except for emergency purposes. For the purposes of this
11 subsection, "emergency purposes" means any situation in which no other permanent
12 lodging is available unless a security deposit is paid. For the purposes of this subsection,
13 "broadband Internet access service" has the same meaning as in Title 10, section 1500-U,
14 subsection 1, paragraph A. For the purposes of this subsection, "wireless access point
15 technology" means technology that allows devices with wireless capacity to connect to a
16 network wirelessly.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This bill modifies the provisions of law governing municipal general assistance to
1199 designate broadband Internet access service and wireless access point technology as basic
2200 necessities.
