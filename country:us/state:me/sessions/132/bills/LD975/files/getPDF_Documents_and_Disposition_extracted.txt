15 SUMMARY
1166 This bill repeals the laws authorizing abortion in the State. The bill also repeals the
1177 crimes of elevated aggravated assault on a pregnant person and domestic violence elevated
1188 aggravated assault on a pregnant person and instead defines "human being" and "person"
1199 for purposes of the Maine Criminal Code, including for purposes of the statutes prohibiting
2200 murder, assault, domestic violence assault and other offenses against a human being or a
2211 person, to include all human beings beginning at the moment of conception.
