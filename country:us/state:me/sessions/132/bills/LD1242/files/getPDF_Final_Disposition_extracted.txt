COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 817, L.D. 1242
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, adds energy storage
33 units at a picnic area to the lease agreement the Department of Transportation is required
44 to enter into under the bill. The amendment reduces, from 2 to one, the number of electric
55 vehicle charging stations at a solar carport canopy that are required in order to allow a
66 person to amortize the payment of sales and use taxes on the materials and equipment
77 purchased to construct the solar carport canopy and any electric vehicle charging station
88 under the solar carport canopy. The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations
99 section.
