9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 707, L.D. 1085, “Resolve, Directing the
10 Judicial Branch to Perform an Inspection and Study of the Rumford Courthouse Building”
11 Amend the resolve by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'Resolve, Directing the State Court Administrator to Perform an Inspection and
13 Study of the Rumford Courthouse Building'
14 Amend the resolve by striking out everything after the title and inserting the following:
15 'Sec. 1. State Court Administrator to study Rumford Courthouse building;
16 report. Resolved: That the State Court Administrator shall perform an inspection and
17 evaluation of the entire space within the building located at 145 Congress Street in the
18 Town of Rumford, including space within the building currently occupied by the judicial
19 branch, the town, the county or any other organization or entity. By February 1, 2027, the
20 State Court Administrator shall submit a report to the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial
21 Court and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary
22 matters describing:
23 1. All required improvements and modifications, including improvements and
24 modifications required to ensure accessibility and safety, as well as any suggested
25 reallocation of space within the building necessary both to maintain and to expand the
26 District Court services provided within the building; an estimate of the cost for
27 implementing these improvements and modifications; and an estimate of the time required
28 to complete these improvements and modifications; and
29 2. All required improvements and modifications, including improvements and
30 modifications required to ensure accessibility and safety, as well as any suggested
31 reallocation of space within the building necessary both to maintain and to expand the
32 Superior Court services provided within the building; an estimate of the cost for
33 implementing these improvements and modifications; and an estimate of the time required
34 to complete these improvements and modifications.

16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment replaces the resolve and changes the title. The amendment directs the
1188 State Court Administrator to submit a report to the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial
1199 Court and the joint standing committee of the Legislature having jurisdiction over judiciary
2200 matters by February 1, 2027, describing all required improvements and modifications and
2211 any suggested reallocation of space within the Rumford Courthouse building necessary
2222 both to maintain and to expand the District Court services provided within the building and
2233 to maintain and expand the Superior Court services provided within the building; an
2244 estimate of the cost for implementing these improvements and modifications; and an
2255 estimate of the time required to complete these improvements and modifications.
2266 The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section that provides
2277 one-time funding in fiscal year 2025-26 for costs associated with inspecting and evaluating
2288 the Rumford Courthouse building as required by the amendment.
