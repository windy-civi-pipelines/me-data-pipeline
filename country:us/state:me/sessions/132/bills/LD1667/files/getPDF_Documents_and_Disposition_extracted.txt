Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Authorization to sue State. Resolved: That, notwithstanding any
2 provision of law to the contrary, Hemphill Farms, Inc., in Presque Isle, which claims to
3 have suffered damages in connection with actions taken by the Department of Agriculture,
4 Conservation and Forestry, is authorized to bring a civil action against the Department of
5 Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry. This resolve is a waiver of the State's defense of
6 immunity under the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 14, chapter 741.
7 Notwithstanding the application of any statute of limitations barring this action, this
8 action may be brought in the Aroostook County Superior Court within one year from the
9 effective date of this resolve. Liability and damages, including punitive damages, must be
10 determined according to state law as in litigation between individuals. The action may be
11 heard by a Justice of the Superior Court or by a jury. The Maine Rules of Civil Procedure
12 govern the conduct of the action. The Attorney General shall appear, answer and defend
13 the action. To the extent possible, the Attorney General shall conduct negotiations in good
14 faith to resolve this action by settlement.
15 The Treasurer of State shall pay any settlement determined by agreement of the parties
16 or any judgment, including costs and interest, upon final process issued by the Superior
17 Court or, if applicable, the Supreme Judicial Court.
18 SUMMARY
1199 This resolve authorizes Hemphill Farms, Inc., in Presque Isle, which claims to have
2200 suffered damages in connection with actions taken by the Department of Agriculture,
2211 Conservation and Forestry, to sue the department for damages resulting from those actions.
