9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 801, L.D. 1226, “An Act to Protect
10 Consumers by Licensing Residential Building Contractors”
11 Amend the bill by inserting after section 10 the following:
12 'Sec. 11. Transfer from General Fund unappropriated surplus; Office of
13 Professional and Occupational Regulation, Department of Professional and
14 Financial Regulation, fiscal year 2025-26. Notwithstanding any provision of law to
15 the contrary, the State Controller shall transfer $620,126 from the unappropriated surplus
16 of the General Fund to the Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation, Other
17 Special Revenue Funds account within the Department of Professional and Financial
18 Regulation no later than October 1, 2025 to support the establishment and start-up costs of
19 the Residential Construction Board established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32,
20 section 19802.
21 Sec. 12. Transfer from General Fund unappropriated surplus; Office of
22 Professional and Occupational Regulation, Department of Professional and
23 Financial Regulation, fiscal year 2026-27. Notwithstanding any provision of law to
24 the contrary, the State Controller shall transfer $664,222 from the unappropriated surplus
25 of the General Fund to the Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation, Other
26 Special Revenue Funds account within the Department of Professional and Financial
27 Regulation no later than July 15, 2026 to support the operating costs of the Residential
28 Construction Board established in the Maine Revised Statutes, Title 32, section 19802.
29 Sec. 13. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
30 allocations are made.
31 PROFESSIONAL AND FINANCIAL REGULATION, DEPARTMENT OF
32 Administrative Services - Professional and Financial Regulation 0094
33 Initiative: Allocates funds for the general operating costs necessary to support the
34 Residential Construction Board.
