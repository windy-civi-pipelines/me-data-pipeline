Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 635
Resolve, to Direct the Attorney General to Drop the Lawsuit Filed
Against Big Oil Companies Concerning Climate Change
Received by the Clerk of the House on February 20, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Judiciary pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative TUELL of East Machias.
Cosponsored by Senator HAGGAN of Penobscot and
Representatives: FOSTER of Dexter, JAVNER of Chester, MINGO of Calais, MORRIS of
Turner, POIRIER of Skowhegan, SMITH of Palermo, STROUT of Harrington, WOOD of
Norway.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Attorney General to withdraw from lawsuit filed against oil
2 companies. Resolved: That the Department of the Attorney General shall dismiss and
3 withdraw from the case State of Maine v. BP, PLC et al, currently pending in the United
4 States District Court, District of Maine, Case Number: 2:2025cv00001-NT.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of the Attorney General to dismiss and withdraw
77 from the case State of Maine v. BP, PLC et al, currently pending in the United States
88 District Court, District of Maine.
