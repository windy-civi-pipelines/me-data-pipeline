22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment replaces the minimum wage for the State with a gradual
2244 implementation of a regional living wage provided by the Massachusetts Institute of
2255 Technology, Living Wage Calculator, or its successor organization. The gradual
2266 implementation will occur over 5 years, beginning January 1, 2026. The amendment
2277 divides the State into regions based on counties for the purposes of a regional living wage.
2288 The bill provides that, through December 31, 2025, the minimum hourly wage is $14.65,
2299 which is the minimum hourly wage as of January 1, 2025. Beginning on January 1, 2026,
3300 the minimum hourly wage will be replaced by 80% of the Massachusetts Institute of
3311 Technology Living Wage as of January 1, 2025, increasing by 5% yearly until 2030.
3322 Beginning January 1, 2031, the minimum wage will be 100% of the Massachusetts Institute
3333 of Technology Living Wage for the State as a whole as of January 1st of the previous year.
3344 The amendment also adds an appropriations and allocations section to the bill.
