1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 36 MRSA ยง1760, sub-ยง96 is enacted to read:
3 96. Wheelchair adapted vehicles. Beginning January 1, 2026, sales of automobiles
4 adapted to accommodate a wheelchair actively occupied by an individual while the vehicle
5 is operated from the wheelchair or by another occupant of the vehicle or automobiles for
6 which the owner attests such modifications are necessary and will be completed within 6
7 months of the vehicle's purchase.
8 As used in this subsection, "adapted to accommodate a wheelchair" means equipped with
9 altered controls or a mechanical lifting device designed to accommodate a wheelchair.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill exempts from the sales and use tax sales of automobiles adapted for the use
1122 of occupants in wheelchairs or intended to be so adapted within 6 months of purchase.
