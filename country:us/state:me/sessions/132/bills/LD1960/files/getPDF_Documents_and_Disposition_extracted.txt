Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1960
S.P. 767 In Senate, May 7, 2025
Products Containing Ingestible Hemp from the Tax Imposed on
Tobacco Products
Reference to the Committee on Veterans and Legal Affairs suggested and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator TALBOT ROSS of Cumberland.
Cosponsored by Representative BOYER of Poland and
Senators: BAILEY of York, BENNETT of Oxford, HICKMAN of Kennebec, TIMBERLAKE
of Androscoggin, Representatives: CHAPMAN of Auburn, FAIRCLOTH of Bangor,
FREDERICKS of Sanford, SUPICA of Bangor.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §4403-A, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 2023, c. 441, Pt. E, §20 and
3 affected by §28, is amended to read:
4 2. Tobacco products containing cannabis or ingestible consumer products
5 containing hemp. The tax imposed on tobacco products does not apply to the sale of an
6 electronic smoking device or other tobacco product containing adult use cannabis subject
7 to tax under chapter 723 or, an electronic smoking device or other tobacco product
8 containing cannabis or cannabis products subject to control under Title 22, chapter 558‑C
9 or an electronic smoking device or other tobacco product containing ingestible consumer
10 products containing hemp or cannabidiol derived from hemp. For the purposes of this
11 subsection, "hemp" has the same meaning as in Title 7, section 2231, subsection 1-A,
12 paragraph D.
13 SUMMARY
1144 This bill exempts from the tax imposed on tobacco products an electronic smoking
1155 device or other tobacco product containing ingestible consumer products containing hemp
1166 or cannabidiol derived from hemp.
