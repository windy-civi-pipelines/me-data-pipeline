APPROVED CHAPTER
MARCH 25, 2025 4
BY GOVERNOR RESOLVES
STATE OF MAINE
_____
IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD
TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE
_____
S.P. 198 - L.D. 464
Veterans Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Denmark renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
Transportation shall designate Bridge 5410 on West Main Street, which crosses Moose
Pond Outlet in the Town of Denmark and is currently known as the Brackett Mill Bridge,
the Brackett Mill Veterans Memorial Bridge.
