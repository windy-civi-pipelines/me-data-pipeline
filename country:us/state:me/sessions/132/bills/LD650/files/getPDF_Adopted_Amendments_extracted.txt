1 L.D. 650
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 418, L.D. 650, “An Act to Support
10 Municipal Public Health”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in §453 by striking out all of subsection 1 (page 1, lines 4
12 to 16 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
13 '1. Membership; administration. A municipality may appoint a local board of health
14 consisting of a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 7 members besides the local health officer
15 appointed pursuant to section 451, one of whom must be a physician, an advanced practice
16 nurse or a physician assistant, if available in the community. Appointees must have
17 knowledge, education and experience in medicine or public health, including but not
18 limited to community mental health, public health nursing, public and community health,
19 social work and communications. To the extent possible, the board must be representative
20 of the population served. When first appointed, members of the board must be appointed
21 one for one year, one for 2 years and one for 3 years. Subsequent appointments must be for
22 3-year terms. The local health officer is secretary ex officio of the local board of health and
23 shall keep a record of all proceedings. The local board of health constitutes an advisory
24 body to the local health officer.'
25 Amend the bill in section 1 in §453 in subsection 2 in paragraph A in the last line (page
26 1, line 21 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "rule" the following: 'and are based in
27 scientific approaches'
28 Amend the bill in section 1 in §453 in subsection 2 in paragraph C in the last line (page
29 1, line 26 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "officer" the following: ', but shall
30 prioritize activities overseen by the local health officer'
31 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
32 number to read consecutively.
33 SUMMARY
3344 This amendment adds language allowing a municipal local board of health seat filled
3355 by a physician to instead be filled by a physician, advanced practice nurse or physician
