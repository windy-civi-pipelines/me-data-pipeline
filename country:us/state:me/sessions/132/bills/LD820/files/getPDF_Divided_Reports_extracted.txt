8 SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 527,
9 L.D. 820, “RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine to
10 Establish the Right to Hunt and Fish”
11 Amend the amendment by inserting after the title the following:
12 'Amend the resolution by striking out the title and substituting the following:
13 'RESOLUTION, Proposing an Amendment to the Constitution of Maine to Establish
14 the Right to Hunt and Fish and to Protect Wildlife and Fish from Pollution'
15 Amend the resolution in that part designated "Constitution, Art. IX, §26" in §26 in
16 the first line (page 1, line 5 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "and fish" the
17 following: '; common trust; protection from pollution' '
18 Amend the amendment in the first instructional paragraph in the 4th line (page 1, line
19 15 in amendment) by inserting after the following: "Legislature" the following: 'and by the
20 direct initiative of legislation pursuant to Article IV, Part Third, Section 18'
21 Amend the amendment by inserting after the first instructional paragraph the
22 following:
23 'Amend the resolution in that part designated "Constitution, Art. IX, §26" by
24 inserting at the end a new paragraph to read:
25 'Wildlife and fish are the common trust of all people of this State, including future
26 generations, and they may not be polluted, degraded or made unhealthful. The Legislature
27 shall provide for their protection. Every person may enforce this right against any party
28 that pollutes, degrades or makes wildlife or fish unhealthful through legal proceedings
29 subject to laws enacted by the Legislature and by the direct initiative of legislation pursuant
30 to Article IV, Part Third, Section 18.' '
31 Amend the amendment by inserting after the 2nd instructional paragraph the following:
32 'Amend the resolution in the question in the 3rd line (page 1, line 21 in L.D.) by striking
33 out the following: ", and" and inserting the following: ','
34 Amend the resolution in the question in the last line (page 1, line 22 in L.D.) by
35 inserting after the following: "wildlife" the following: 'and to provide that wildlife and fish
36 must be protected from pollution' '

SENATE AMENDMENT “ ” to COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “A” to H.P. 527, L.D. 820
1 Amend the amendment by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
2 section number to read consecutively.
3 SUMMARY
44 This amendment provides that wildlife and fish are the common trust of all people of
55 this State, including future generations, and they may not be polluted, degraded or made
66 unhealthful; that the Legislature must provide for their protection; and that every person
77 may enforce this right against any party that pollutes, degrades or makes wildlife or fish
88 unhealthful through legal proceedings subject to laws enacted by the Legislature. The
99 amendment also provides that the provisions of the constitutional resolution are subject to
1100 laws enacted by the direct initiative of legislation, in addition to laws enacted by the
1111 Legislature as provided in the resolution.
