1 L.D. 1276
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
4 Reproduced and distributed under the direction of the Clerk of the House.
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 851, L.D. 1276, “An Act to Align
10 Intrastate Commercial Motor Vehicle Insurance Requirements with Federal Standards”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 29-A MRSA §1611, sub-§2, ¶G is enacted to read:
14 G. For intrastate commercial motor vehicles, there is a combined single limit of
15 $750,000.
16 Sec. 2. Application. This Act applies to all commercial motor vehicle liability
17 insurance policies executed, delivered, issued for delivery, continued or renewed in this
18 State on or after January 1, 2026. For purposes of this Act, all policies are deemed to be
19 renewed no later than the next yearly anniversary of the contract date.'
20 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
21 number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment reallocates the provision of the bill
2244 establishing a minimum insurance requirement for intrastate commercial motor vehicles to
2255 the section of law that governs minimum insurance requirements for other types of motor
2266 vehicles for hire and makes clear that the minimum insurance requirements for commercial
2277 motor vehicles apply to insurance policies issued or renewed on or after January 1, 2026.
