Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1186
H.P. 791 House of Representatives, March 20, 2025
Feasibility of Ferry Service to Monhegan Island
Reference to the Committee on Transportation suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative CRAFTS of Newcastle.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Monhegan Island ferry service study. Resolved: That the Department
2 of Transportation shall work with island and ferry service stakeholders to study adding
3 ferry service to Monhegan Island. The study must include an assessment of the economic
4 and social benefits and challenges of implementing ferry service, the infrastructure
5 resources and needs on the island and mainland and the overall feasibility of such a service.
6 The department may, but is not required to, invite the participation in the study of any
7 of the following persons as stakeholders:
8 1. A Monhegan Plantation representative, such as the First Assessor or the First
9 Assessor's designee;
10 2. A year-round resident with a vested interest in Monhegan Island;
11 3. A representative of the Department of Transportation who is not involved with the
12 Maine State Ferry Service;
13 4. A representative of the Maine State Ferry Service;
14 5. A member of the House of Representatives, who if invited must be appointed by the
15 Speaker of the House; and
16 6. A member of the Senate, who if invited must be appointed by the President of the
17 Senate.
18 Sec. 2. Report. Resolved: That, no later than December 3, 2025, the Department
19 of Transportation shall submit a report that includes its findings and recommendations
20 under section 1, including any suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing Committee on
21 Transportation. The joint standing committee may report out a bill to the Second Regular
22 Session of the 132nd Legislature.
23 SUMMARY
2244 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to work with island and ferry
2255 service stakeholders to study the feasibility of adding ferry service to Monhegan Island.
2266 The department must submit a report by December 3, 2025 to the Joint Standing Committee
2277 on Transportation, which is authorized to submit legislation to the Second Regular Session
2288 of the 132nd Legislature.
