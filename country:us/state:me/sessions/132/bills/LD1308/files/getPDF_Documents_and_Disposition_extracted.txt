Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1308
S.P. 538 In Senate, March 27, 2025
to Establish a Working Group to Explore Opportunities to Maintain
Public Access of Land
Reference to the Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife suggested and ordered
printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator BALDACCI of Penobscot.
Cosponsored by Representatives: BRIDGEO of Augusta, DILL of Old Town.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife working group; access
2 to land. Resolved: That the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife shall convene
3 a working group to explore opportunities to work with commercial landowners to establish
4 public-private partnerships designed to maintain public access to privately owned land. For
5 the purposes of this section, "commercial landowner" means an owner of land used
6 primarily for commercial purposes. By December 3, 2025, the department shall submit a
7 report, including recommendations and any suggested legislation, to the Joint Standing
8 Committee on Inland Fisheries and Wildlife. The joint standing committee may submit
9 legislation related to the report to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd Legislature.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This resolve directs the Department of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife to convene a
1122 working group to explore opportunities to work with commercial landowners to establish
1133 public-private partnerships designed to maintain public access to privately owned land.
