Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 480
An Act to Support Healthy Weight by Providing MaineCare
Coverage for Certain Weight Loss Medications
Received by the Clerk of the House on February 6, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Health and Human Services pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint
Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative GRAHAM of North Yarmouth.
Cosponsored by Representatives: ANKELES of Brunswick, EATON of Deer Isle, ROBERTS
of South Berwick, SALISBURY of Westbrook, TERRY of Gorham.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3174-PPP is enacted to read:
3 §3174-PPP. Reimbursement for certain weight loss medications
4 The department shall provide reimbursement under the MaineCare program for
5 glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists that are approved by the federal Food and Drug
6 Administration for the treatment of obesity. The department shall restrict reimbursement
7 under the MaineCare program of glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists to medications
8 prescribed by a primary care provider or a bariatric specialist and shall require a prior
9 authorization of the medication.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill requires the Department of Health and Human Services to provide
1122 reimbursement under the MaineCare program for glucagon-like peptide-1 receptor agonists
1133 that are approved by the federal Food and Drug Administration for the treatment of obesity.
1144 The department must restrict reimbursement to medications prescribed by a primary care
1155 provider or a bariatric specialist and must require a prior authorization of the medication.
