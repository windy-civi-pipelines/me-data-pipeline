Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1050
H.P. 679 House of Representatives, March 14, 2025
Antlerless Deer Permit to a Junior Hunter
Received by the Clerk of the House on March 12, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Inland Fisheries and Wildlife pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to Joint
Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative WOODSOME of Waterboro.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 12 MRSA ยง11152, sub-ยง5-B is enacted to read:
3 5-B. Permit transfer to junior hunter. A person with a valid junior hunting license
4 may take an antlerless deer if another person who is 18 years of age or older and holds a
5 valid antlerless deer permit transfers that permit to that junior hunter by identifying the
6 name and address of the transferee on the permit as well as any other information
7 reasonably requested by the commissioner. The transferor shall return the permit to the
8 department within 48 hours after the transfer of the permit. Upon transfer of the antlerless
9 deer permit to a junior hunter, the transferor may not hunt antlerless deer but shall provide
10 direct supervision of the junior hunter while hunting.
11 The commissioner shall record a transfer under this subsection. A valid antlerless deer
12 permit must be in the possession of the transferee in order for the transferee to hunt
13 antlerless deer.
14 SUMMARY
1155 This bill allows a person who is 18 years of age or older and in possession of a valid
1166 antlerless deer permit to transfer that permit to a person with a valid junior hunting license.
1177 The bill establishes a process for this transfer: the permit holder must identify the name and
1188 address of the junior hunter on the permit and any additional information, if requested by
1199 the Commissioner of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife and return the permit to the Department
2200 of Inland Fisheries and Wildlife within 48 hours after the transfer of the permit. The
2211 transferor may not hunt antlerless deer but must provide supervision of the junior hunter
2222 while hunting.
