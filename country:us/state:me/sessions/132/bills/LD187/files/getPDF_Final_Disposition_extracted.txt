7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 120, L.D. 187, “An Act to Prohibit
10 Labor Organizations from Imposing Mandatory Service Fees on Nonmembers”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Prohibit Public Labor Organizations from Imposing Mandatory Service
13 Fees on Nonmembers'
14 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 1.
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment changes the title and removes the provision of the bill that provides
1199 that an employee in the private sector who has refrained from joining or participating in a
2200 union may not be required to pay to the organization that is the bargaining agent for the
2211 employee a service fee that represents that employee's pro rata share of those expenditures
2222 that are germane to the organization's representational activities.
