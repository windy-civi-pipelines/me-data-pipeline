20 certified historic structure is also a certified affordable housing project, then the credit
21 allowed under this section is increased by a further 10 percentage points.'
22 Amend the bill by striking out all of section 3 and inserting the following:
23 'Sec. 3. Application. This Act applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1,
24 2025.'
25 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
26 number to read consecutively.

3300 amendment provides that the amount of the tax credit in the bill may be increased by a
3311 further 10 percentage points for a rehabilitation that is also a certified affordable housing
