132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 796
S.P. 362 In Senate, March 4, 2025
Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 27, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator NANGLE of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Windham and Gorham renamed. Resolved: That the
2 Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 2787 on Route 202, which crosses the
3 Presumpscot River between the Town of Windham and the Town of Gorham and is
4 currently known as the South Windham Bridge, the Little Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 2787 on
77 Route 202 between the Town of Windham and the Town of Gorham the Little Falls
88 Veterans Memorial Bridge.
