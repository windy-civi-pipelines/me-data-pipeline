_____
S.P. 362 - L.D. 796
Veterans Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Windham and Gorham renamed. Resolved: That the
Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 2787 on Route 202, which crosses the
Presumpscot River between the Town of Windham and the Town of Gorham and is
currently known as the South Windham Bridge, the Little Falls Veterans Memorial Bridge.
