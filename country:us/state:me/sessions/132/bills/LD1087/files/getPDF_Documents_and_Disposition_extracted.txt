132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1087
H.P. 709 House of Representatives, March 18, 2025
Construction Costs
Received by the Clerk of the House on March 14, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Education and Cultural Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed pursuant to
Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative BOYER of Cape Elizabeth.
Cosponsored by Representatives: MCCABE of Lewiston, MITCHELL of Cumberland,
Senators: CARNEY of Cumberland, PIERCE of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §15907-A is enacted to read:
3 §15907-A. State share of school construction costs on or after July 1, 2026
4 Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, beginning July 1, 2026, the
5 State's share of the total cost of a school construction project must be calculated in
6 accordance with this section.
7 1. State's share. The State's share of the total cost of a school construction project for
8 a school administrative unit is the greater of:
9 A. Twenty-five percent of the total cost of the school construction project; and
10 B. A percentage equal to the state share percentage of the school administrative unit's
11 total cost of education as determined under section 15688. For the purposes of this
12 paragraph, "state share percentage" has the same meaning as in section 15672,
13 subsection 31.
14 2. Rulemaking. The state board may adopt or amend rules related to the State's share
15 of the total cost of a school construction project as necessary to implement this section.
16 Rules adopted pursuant to this subsection are major substantive rules pursuant to Title 5,
17 chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
18 SUMMARY
1199 This bill provides that, beginning July 1, 2026, the State's share of the total cost of a
2200 school construction project is either 25% of the total cost of the school construction project
2211 or a percentage equal to the state share percentage of the school administrative unit's total
2222 cost of education as determined in the essential programs and services funding formula,
2233 whichever is greater. The bill also allows the State Board of Education to adopt or amend
2244 rules related to the State's share of the total cost of a school construction project.
