COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1296, L.D. 1935
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment is the minority report of the committee. This amendment removes the
33 provision from the bill that provides that a person, company, firm, partnership or
44 corporation who drills, drives or bores a water well or geothermal heat exchange well
55 without being licensed commits a Class D crime. The amendment also decreases the civil
66 violation fine from not more than $25,000, as in the bill, to not more than $10,000.
