21 SUMMARY
2222 This amendment, which is the majority report of the committee, removes the provision
2233 of the bill that directs the State Archivist to collect and preserve firsthand accounts from
2244 the State's congressional delegation and staff and others connected to the events of January
2255 6, 2021, to create educational materials based on those accounts and to make those
2266 materials and accounts available to the public through an online archive and physical
2277 exhibits. The amendment also changes a statutory section number to avoid a conflict with
2288 recently enacted law.
