1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §341-G, first ¶, as amended by PL 2003, c. 245, §2, is further
3 amended to read:
4 There is established the The Board of Environmental Protection Fund is established to
5 be used by the board as a nonlapsing fund to carry out its duties under this Title.
6 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the funds identified in
7 subsection 1 transfer annually to the Board of Environmental Protection Fund in an amount
8 not to exceed $325,000 $450,000. Money in the Board of Environmental Protection Fund
9 may only be expended in accordance with allocations approved by the Legislature.
10 SUMMARY
1111 This bill increases from $325,000 annually to $450,000 annually the cap on the amount
1122 that may be transferred to the Board of Environmental Protection Fund from the Maine
1133 Environmental Protection Fund, the Maine Ground and Surface Waters Clean-up and
1144 Response Fund, the Maine Hazardous Waste Fund and the Uncontrolled Sites Fund.
