9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 701, L.D. 1802, “An Act to Implement
10 the Recommendations of the Maine Commission on Public Defense Services to Clarify
11 When an Indigent Criminal Defendant Is Entitled to Counsel at State Expense”
12 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph A in subparagraph (3) in the last line (page 1,
13 line 11 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "or"
14 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph A in subparagraph (4) in the last line (page 1,
15 line 14 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "defendant;" the following: 'or'
16 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph A by inserting after subparagraph (4) the
17 following:
18 '(5) The accused is subject to conditions of preconviction bail pursuant to chapter
19 105-A, subchapter 2;'
20 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
21 number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment provides that, in addition to the qualifying factors established in the
2244 bill to determine whether an indigent criminal defendant is entitled to counsel at state
2255 expense because there is a risk upon conviction that the accused may be sentenced to a term
2266 of imprisonment, an indigent criminal defendant is entitled to such counsel if the defendant
2277 is subject to conditions of preconviction bail.
