Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 732
An Act to Prohibit Copyrights for Works of State Government
Reference to the Committee on State and Local Government suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative POIRIER of Skowhegan.
Cosponsored by Representatives: DUCHARME of Madison, GRIFFIN of Levant, MORRIS of
Turner, QUINT of Hodgdon.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 1 MRSA §30 is enacted to read:
3 §30. Copyright for works of State prohibited
4 Works of the State may not be afforded copyright protection, including under 17 United
5 States Code or any subsequent federal law. This section does not apply to merchandise sold
6 by a department or agency and does not preclude the State from receiving and holding a
7 copyright transferred to the State by assignment, bequest or otherwise or a copyright of a
8 work of the State existing prior to the effective date of this section.
9 Sec. 2. 27 MRSA §85-A, sub-§9, as enacted by PL 1987, c. 465, §2, is amended
10 to read:
11 9. Research and publications. To carry on research to increase knowledge in the
12 several fields of museum activity and to provide interpretive and information services,
13 including the dissemination and recording of information gathered through research.
14 Protection through copyright law may be obtained;
15 SUMMARY
1166 This bill prohibits the use of copyright protection for any work of the State except for
1177 merchandise sold by a department or agency. This bill does not preclude the State from
1188 receiving and holding a copyright transferred to the State by assignment, bequest or
1199 otherwise or a copyright of a work of the State existing prior to the effective date of this
2200 legislation.
