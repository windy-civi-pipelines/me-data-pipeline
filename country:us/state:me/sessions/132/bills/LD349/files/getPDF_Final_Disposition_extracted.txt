5 STATE OF MAINE

7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 249, L.D. 349, “An Act to Repeal the
10 Laws That Allow Same-day Voter Registration”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Amend the Laws Regarding Same-day Voter Registration'
13 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
14 following:
15 'Sec. 1. 21-A MRSA §121-A, first ¶, as amended by PL 2023, c. 291, §2 and
16 affected by §4, is further amended to read:
17 The deadline for receipt of voter registration applications submitted by mail or by a 3rd
18 person is the close of business on the 21st day before election day. The deadline for receipt
19 of voter registration applications submitted online is midnight on the 21st day before
20 election day. The deadline for receipt of voter registration applications transmitted by the
21 Department of the Secretary of State, Bureau of Motor Vehicles pursuant to section 232 or
22 by a source agency other than the Bureau of Motor Vehicles pursuant to section 233 is
23 midnight on the 7th day before election day. The deadline for in-person registration is the
24 close of the polls 2 p.m. on election day.
25 Sec. 2. 21-A MRSA §122, sub-§4, as amended by PL 2005, c. 453, §16, is further
26 amended to read:
27 4. Election day registration. The registrar shall accept registrations of applicants
28 who appear in person by 2 p.m. on election day. The registrar shall continue to accept
29 registrations after 2 p.m. from applicants who were present at the polling place by the
30 deadline. The registrar shall issue to each of these applicants a certificate entitling the
31 applicant to be placed on the incoming voting list at the voting place. Only one certificate
32 may be issued to a person. An applicant whose address has changed since the applicant
33 last voted must vote using the ballot or ballots for the new polling place, if applicable, on
34 election day.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 249, L.D. 349
1 Sec. 3. 21-A MRSA §122, sub-§5, as amended by PL 2013, c. 131, §7, is further
2 amended to read:
3 5. Alternative registration schedule for absentee voters. If the clerk receives a
4 properly completed absentee ballot application that is signed by a person who is not a
5 registered voter in the municipality, a presumption of the person's qualification as a voter
6 is established. The clerk shall send an absentee ballot to the voter at the address indicated,
7 along with a voter registration application under section 152. The completed registration
8 application must be returned to the clerk by the close of the polls on election day deadline
9 for in-person registration established in section 121-A in order for the ballot to be counted
10 and may not be sealed with the voted absentee ballot. If the application is received during
11 the closed period and the registrar is not satisfied as to the person's qualification as a voter,
12 the registrar shall follow the requirements of section 121, subsection 1‑A to place the
13 person's name on the incoming voting list and challenge the absentee ballot. An application
14 by telephone under section 753‑A, subsection 4 or an application by electronic means under
15 section 753‑A, subsection 6 does not establish a presumption of qualification under this
16 section and the requestor must submit a properly completed voter registration application
17 before the clerk may issue an absentee ballot.'
18 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
19 number to read consecutively.
20 SUMMARY
2211 This amendment replaces the bill. It changes the title and changes the deadline for in-
2222 person voter registration to 2 p.m. on election day. The registrar must accept a registration
2233 from an applicant present at the polling place by the deadline. The deadline by which an
2244 absentee voter's registration application must be returned to the clerk is also 2 p.m. on
2255 election day.
