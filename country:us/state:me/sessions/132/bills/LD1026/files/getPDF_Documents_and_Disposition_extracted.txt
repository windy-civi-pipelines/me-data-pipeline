Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1026
S.P. 444 In Senate, March 13, 2025
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on March 12, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Marine Resources pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator RENY of Lincoln.
Cosponsored by Representative FARRIN of Jefferson and
Senators: GROHOSKI of Hancock, MOORE of Washington, Representatives: CRAFTS of
Newcastle, HEPLER of Woolwich, STOVER of Boothbay, STROUT of Harrington.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 5 MRSA §3204, sub-§2, ¶E, as enacted by PL 2023, c. 643, Pt. DD, §2, is
3 amended to read:
4 E. Administer contracts with regional councils and regional planning and development
5 districts to provide technical assistance and resources to municipalities and tribal
6 governments on issues related to planning, climate resilience and development,
7 including but not limited to land use planning, planning for housing and other
8 residential development, climate resilience planning and related infrastructure
9 planning, building codes and other forms of local development assistance to support
10 state, regional and local goals; and
11 Sec. 2. 5 MRSA §3204, sub-§2, ¶F, as enacted by PL 2023, c. 643, Pt. DD, §2, is
12 amended to read:
13 F. Consult with and provide ongoing coordination with state agencies on programs
14 and issues related to planning technical assistance and funding to communities in this
15 State, including but not limited to the Department of Transportation; Department of
16 Environmental Protection; Department of Marine Resources; Department of Inland
17 Fisheries and Wildlife; Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry;
18 Department of Economic and Community Development; Department of Health and
19 Human Services; Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management,
20 Maine Emergency Management Agency; Department of Public Safety; Maine State
21 Housing Authority; Governor's Energy Office; Efficiency Maine Trust; the Maine
22 Historic Preservation Commission; and the Maine Redevelopment Land Bank
23 Authority.; and
24 Sec. 3. 5 MRSA §3204, sub-§2, ¶G is enacted to read:
25 G. Create and provide to municipalities, tribal governments and regional councils a
26 mapping template and technical assistance to allow a municipality, tribal government
27 or regional council to proactively map out areas of concern related to aquaculture lease
28 siting.
29 SUMMARY
3300 This bill adds to the duties of the Maine Office of Community Affairs the requirement
3311 to create and provide to municipalities, tribal governments and regional councils a mapping
3322 template and technical assistance to proactively map out areas of concern related
3333 aquaculture lease siting.
