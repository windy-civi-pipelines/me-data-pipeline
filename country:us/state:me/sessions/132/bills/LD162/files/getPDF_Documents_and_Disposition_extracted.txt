Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 162
H.P. 95 House of Representatives, January 14, 2025
Howell Memorial Bridge
Reference to the Committee on Transportation suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative THORNE of Carmel.
Cosponsored by Senator HAGGAN of Penobscot and
Representatives: CAMPBELL of Orrington, DUCHARME of Madison, HAGGAN of
Hampden, PAUL of Winterport, Senator: GUERIN of Penobscot.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. Bridge in Carmel renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
3 Transportation shall designate Bridge 2976 on Main Road, which crosses the former Maine
4 Central Railroad line in the Town of Carmel and is currently known as the Maine Central
5 Railroad Crossing Bridge, the Kevin M. Howell Memorial Bridge.
6 SUMMARY
77 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 2976 on
88 Main Road in the Town of Carmel the Kevin M. Howell Memorial Bridge.
