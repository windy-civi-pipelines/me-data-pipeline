COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 296, L.D. 681
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment provides that beginning in fiscal year 2025-26, and each fiscal year
33 thereafter, in addition to any adjustment for inflation, the Legislature must increase the
44 percentage of the Maine Maritime Academy's total state appropriation as a share of its total
55 operating cost by 2% above the baseline budget until the percentage of the Maine Maritime
66 Academy's total state appropriation as a share of the academy's total operating costs equals
77 at least the lesser of the percentages of the University of Maine System's and the Maine
88 Community College System's total state appropriation as a share of each respective
99 system's total operating costs. The amendment changes the percentage increase provided
1100 under the bill, which requires an annual increase by 5%.
11 The amendment also removes the provisions of the bill that establish the Commission
12 to Study the Funding of Public Institutions of Higher Education.
13 The amendment adds an appropriations and allocations section.
