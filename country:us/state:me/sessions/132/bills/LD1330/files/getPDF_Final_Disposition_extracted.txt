17 ownership licenses'
18 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
19 number to read consecutively.
20 SUMMARY
2211 This amendment includes limited ownership licenses and lifetime ownership licenses
2222 in those software licenses that are excluded from the definition of "lease" for purposes of
2233 the application of sales tax.
