132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 368
S.P. 160 In Senate, February 3, 2025
Similar Equipment Used in the Cannabis Industry
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on January 30, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Veterans and Legal Affairs pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator HICKMAN of Kennebec.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 CONCEPT DRAFT
3 SUMMARY
44 This bill is a concept draft pursuant to Joint Rule 208.
5 This bill proposes to amend the laws governing cannabis to address concerns about the
6 health effects of the use of radiation machines and similar equipment used to treat,
7 decontaminate or remediate cannabis flower and trim and to protect consumers from the
8 unknowing or unwanted use of products that have been processed using these machines or
9 equipment.
10 This bill also proposes to clarify the laws governing testing requirements for adult use
11 cannabis and cannabis products.
