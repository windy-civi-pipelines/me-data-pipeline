COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 117, L.D. 184
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment adds to the study commission created in the bill 2 members of the
33 judicial branch, appointed by the Chief Justice of the Supreme Judicial Court. The
44 amendment clarifies that the commission must consider how to make foreclosed property
55 available for sale in appropriate circumstances. Finally, the amendment directs the
66 commission to submit a report to both the Joint Standing Committee on Housing and
77 Economic Development and the Joint Standing Committee on Judiciary and authorizes
88 each committee to report out legislation to the Second Regular Session of the 132nd
99 Legislature.
