5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 1089, L.D. 1635, “An Act to Streamline
10 Municipal Referenda Recount Initiation”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in §2532 in the indented paragraph in the 5th line (page 1,
12 line 9 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "an automatic recount is required" and
13 inserting the following: 'a recount may be conducted upon the majority vote of the
14 municipal officers'
15 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
16 number to read consecutively.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This amendment amends the bill by removing the requirement that an automatic
1199 recount must be conducted when a municipal referendum passes or fails by less than 1%
2200 and instead providing that such a recount may be conducted upon the majority vote of the
2211 municipal officers.
