5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 29, L.D. 65, “An Act to Update the
10 Definition of "Coastal Wetlands" Under the Natural Resources Protection Act”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Update the Definition of "Coastal Wetlands" Under the Natural Resources
13 Protection Act and the Mandatory Shoreland Zoning Laws'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §436-A, sub-§1, as amended by PL 2005, c. 330, §10, is repealed
17 and the following enacted in its place:
18 1. Coastal wetlands. "Coastal wetlands" has the same meaning as in section 480-B,
19 subsection 2.
20 Sec. 2. 38 MRSA §480-B, sub-§2, as amended by PL 2005, c. 330, §13, is repealed
21 and the following enacted in its place:
22 2. Coastal wetlands. "Coastal wetlands" means:
23 A. All areas with vegetation present that is tolerant of salt water and occurs primarily
24 in a saltwater or estuarine habitat; and
25 B. All tidal and subtidal lands and any swamp, marsh, bog, beach, flat or other
26 contiguous lowland subject to tidal action during the highest astronomical tide as
27 determined by the United States Department of Commerce, National Oceanic and
28 Atmospheric Administration.
29 Coastal wetlands may include portions of coastal sand dunes.'
30 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
31 number to read consecutively.
