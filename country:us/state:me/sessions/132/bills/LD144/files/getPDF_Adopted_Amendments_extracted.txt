1 L.D. 144
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 MARINE RESOURCES
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 80, L.D. 144, “An Act to Create a
10 Limited Retail Seafood Harvester Certificate”
11 Amend the bill by inserting after the title and before the enacting clause the following:
12 'Emergency preamble. Whereas, acts and resolves of the Legislature do not
13 become effective until 90 days after adjournment unless enacted as emergencies; and
14 Whereas, the limited retail seafood harvester certificate, established in this legislation
15 and attached to an already existing retail seafood license, would make it easier for shellfish
16 harvesters, especially oyster growers, to sell their harvest directly to consumers without
17 having the additional expense related to the operation of a fixed facility or selling their
18 product through registered dealers; and
19 Whereas, it is important that this certificate be made available to seafood harvesters
20 as soon as possible, so they may sell their harvest from a vehicle during the upcoming
21 spring and summer seasons, including at farmers' markets; and
22 Whereas, the Department of Marine Resources has indicated that the department has
23 the resources necessary to implement this certificate immediately; and
24 Whereas, in the judgment of the Legislature, these facts create an emergency within
25 the meaning of the Constitution of Maine and require the following legislation as
26 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health and safety; now,
27 therefore,'
28 Amend the bill in section 1 in §6852 in subsection 4 in the 4th line (page 2, line 30 in
29 L.D.) by striking out the following: "$100" and inserting the following: '$50'
30 Amend the bill by adding before the summary the following:
31 'Emergency clause. In view of the emergency cited in the preamble, this legislation
32 takes effect when approved.'
33 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
34 number to read consecutively.
