APPROVED CHAPTER
JUNE 10, 2025 60
BY GOVERNOR RESOLVES
STATE OF MAINE
_____
IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD
TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE
_____
H.P. 389 - L.D. 621
Memorial Bridge
Sec. 1. Bridge in Mattawamkeag renamed. Resolved: That the Department
of Transportation shall designate Bridge 2522 on Main Street, which crosses the
Mattawamkeag River in the Town of Mattawamkeag and is currently known as the
Mattawamkeag Bridge, the Mattawamkeag Veterans Memorial Bridge.
