5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 806, L.D. 1231, “An Act Requiring
10 Ferries to Dock Overnight on Certain Islands for Emergency Purposes”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Require Legislative Approval of Proposed Permanent Changes to the
13 Overnight Docking of State Ferries on Islands'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 23 MRSA §4408 is enacted to read:
17 §4408. Overnight docking of ferries; legislative approval
18 1. Change to existing overnight docking practice. The Department of
19 Transportation may not without legislative approval change the existing overnight docking
20 practices of a state-owned ferry vessel that docks overnight on an island served by the
21 Maine State Ferry Service. The department shall seek legislative approval to change the
22 existing overnight docking practice of a ferry vessel that docks on an island served by the
23 Maine State Ferry Service by submitting legislation to the joint standing committee of the
24 Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters prior to the change. The joint
25 standing committee may report out a bill based on the suggested legislation.
26 2. Exceptions. Notwithstanding subsection 1, the Department of Transportation may
27 require a ferry vessel to temporarily dock at a mainland port or an alternative island port
28 for an emergency situation or another significant operational need. The department does
29 not need to seek legislative approval pursuant to subsection 1 for a temporary change to
30 docking practices pursuant to this subsection. For the purposes of this subsection,
31 "temporary change" means a change in the overnight docking of a state-owned ferry vessel
32 that docks on an island served by the Maine State Ferry Service that does not change the
33 common docking practice of that ferry vessel.'
34 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
35 number to read consecutively.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 806, L.D. 1231
1 SUMMARY
22 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. The amendment requires the
33 Department of Transportation to seek legislative approval prior to permanently changing
44 the overnight docking practices of a state-owned ferry vessel that docks on an island served
55 by the Maine State Ferry Service by submitting legislation to the joint standing committee
66 of the Legislature having jurisdiction over transportation matters. The amendment allows
77 the department, without seeking legislative approval, to require such a ferry vessel to
88 temporarily dock at a mainland port or an alternative island port for an emergency situation
99 or another significant operational need.
