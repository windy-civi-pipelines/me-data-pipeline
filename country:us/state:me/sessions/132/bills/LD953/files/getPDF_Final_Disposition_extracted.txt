1 L.D. 953
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 JUDICIARY
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 409, L.D. 953, “An Act to Change the
10 Definition of "Machine Gun" in the Maine Criminal Code”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 17-A MRSA §1051, sub-§2, as enacted by PL 1975, c. 499, §1, is amended
14 to read:
15 2. As used in this chapter, "machine gun" means a weapon of any description, by
16 whatever name known, loaded or unloaded, which is capable of discharging a number of
17 projectiles shots in rapid succession by one manual or mechanical action on the trigger or
18 firing mechanism.'
19 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
20 number to read consecutively.
21 SUMMARY
2222 This amendment replaces the word "projectiles" with the word "shots" in the definition
2233 of "machine gun" in the Maine Criminal Code.
