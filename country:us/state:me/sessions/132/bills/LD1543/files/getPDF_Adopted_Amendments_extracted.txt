9 SUMMARY
1100 This amendment provides that members of the Maine Green Schools Network may be
1111 invited by the Department of Education to participate in the network subject to available
1122 funding and requires the network to create a statewide community of practice among
1133 educators and administrators for the purpose of sharing curricula, instruction and
1144 assessment practices related to community resilience and changing ecosystems. The
1155 amendment also makes changes to the membership of the network and requires that any
1166 funds received by the department must be used in a manner consistent with the law
1177 governing the network as set out in the bill or as otherwise provided in law or the terms
1188 and conditions of the funds received.
