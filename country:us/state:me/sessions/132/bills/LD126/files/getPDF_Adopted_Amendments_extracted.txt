3 VETERANS AND LEGAL AFFAIRS
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 54, L.D. 126, “An Act to Expand the Use
10 of the Veterans' Homelessness Prevention Partnership Fund”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in §513-A in subsection 1 in paragraph C in the first line
12 (page 1, line 18 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "reimbursement" and inserting the
13 following: 'reimbursement disbursements'
14 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
15 number to read consecutively.
16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment clarifies that the Director of the Maine Bureau of Veterans' Services
1188 within the Department of Defense, Veterans and Emergency Management may provide
1199 disbursements, rather than reimbursement, to human services-based volunteer
2200 organizations that provide transitional housing to homeless veterans pursuant to
2211 collaborative agreements.
