1 L.D. 1080
2 Date: (Filing No. H- )
3 ENERGY, UTILITIES AND TECHNOLOGY
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 702, L.D. 1080, “An Act Prohibiting
10 Public Utilities from Requiring Deposits Based Solely on a Residential Customer's
11 Income”
12 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 1 in the 6th line (page 1, line 9 in L.D.) by
13 inserting after the following: "an applicant" the following: 'for residential service'
14 Amend the bill in section 2 in the first line (page 1, line 15 in L.D.) by striking out the
15 following: "The" and inserting the following: 'Within 30 days of the effective date of this
16 Act, the'
17 Amend the bill in section 2 in the last line (page 1, line 17 in L.D.) by striking out the
18 following: ", no later than October 1, 2025"
19 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
20 number to read consecutively.
21 SUMMARY
2222 This amendment clarifies that the provision of the bill that prohibits a public utility
2233 from requiring a deposit from an applicant based solely on the applicant's income applies
2244 only to applicants for residential service. The amendment also changes the date by which
2255 the Public Utilities Commission must initiate rulemaking.
