the following enacted in its place:

Section 2:
7. Delegation. A person acting under delegated authority pursuant to chapter 36 or 48
may not use or be governed by a license issued pursuant to this chapter while acting under
that delegated authority, and a person acting under a license issued pursuant to this chapter
may not be governed by delegated authority or engage in any activities delegated pursuant
to chapter 36 or 48 while acting under a license issued pursuant to this chapter.
