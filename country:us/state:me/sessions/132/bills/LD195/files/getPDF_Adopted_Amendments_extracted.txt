31 SUMMARY
3322 This amendment, which is one of 2 reports of the committee, changes the Small
3333 Business Capital Savings Account Program eligibility requirements for a small business by
3344 reducing the business size from no more than 99 full-time employees to no more than 49
3355 full-time employees. The amendment also amends the reporting requirement of the
3366 program to require submission of the report to the joint standing committee of the
3377 Legislature having jurisdiction over economic development matters as well as the
3388 committee having jurisdiction over taxation matters.
