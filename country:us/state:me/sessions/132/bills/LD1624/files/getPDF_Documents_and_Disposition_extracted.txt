1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
3 allocations are made.
4 EDUCATION, DEPARTMENT OF
5 Summer School Program N515
6 Initiative: Provides ongoing funding to school administrative units for summer school
7 programs and related credit recovery activities that will help high school students meet the
8 academic standards required to earn course credits and to graduate on time. School
9 administrative units may also use funding to support summer enrichment and intervention
10 programs for elementary and middle school students to strengthen foundational skills and
11 prevent learning loss. Priority must be given to school administrative units with student
12 poverty rates of 25% or higher, with additional consideration for students from asset-
13 limited and income-constrained households.

14 SUMMARY
1195 This bill provides ongoing funding to school administrative units for summer school
2106 programs and related credit recovery activities that will help high school students meet the
2117 academic standards required to earn course credits and to graduate on time. It also allows
2128 funding to be used to support summer enrichment and intervention programs for
2139 elementary and middle school students to strengthen foundational skills and prevent
2240 learning loss. Priority will be given to school administrative units with student poverty rates
2251 of 25% or higher, with additional consideration for students from asset-limited and income-
2262 constrained households.
