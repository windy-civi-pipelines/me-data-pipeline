13 'Sec. 1. Appropriations and allocations. The following appropriations and
14 allocations are made.
15 EDUCATION, DEPARTMENT OF
16 Summer School Program N515
17 Initiative: Provides ongoing funding to school administrative units that offer summer
18 school programs and related credit recovery activities that will help high school students
19 meet the academic standards required to earn course credits and to graduate on time. School
20 administrative units may also use funding to support summer enrichment and intervention
21 programs for elementary and middle school students to strengthen foundational skills and
22 prevent learning loss. Priority must be given to school administrative units with student
23 poverty rates of 25% or higher, with additional consideration for students from asset-
24 limited and income-constrained households.
