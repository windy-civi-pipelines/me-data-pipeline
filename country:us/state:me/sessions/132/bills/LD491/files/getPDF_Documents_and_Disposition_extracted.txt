Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 491
Resolve, Authorizing the Department of Agriculture, Conservation
and Forestry to Convey and Release the State's Interest in Certain
Real Property Located in the Town of Richmond
Received by the Clerk of the House on February 6, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed
pursuant to Joint Rule 401.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative CLUCHEY of Bowdoinham.
Cosponsored by Senator TEPLER of Sagadahoc.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Authority to convey and release property. Resolved: That,
2 notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Director of the Bureau of Parks
3 and Lands within the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry shall convey
4 and release the State's interest in the property described in section 2 to Wilhelmine Dennis
5 Oakes or the estate of Wilhelmine Dennis Oakes.
6 Sec. 2. Property interests to be conveyed and released. Resolved: That the
7 property authorized to be conveyed and released is a parcel of land with improvements
8 thereon, located in the Town of Richmond and shown as Lot 67 on Map R6 of the Town
9 of Richmond tax assessor maps.
10 Sec. 3. Repeal. Resolved: That this resolve is repealed 5 years from its effective
11 date.
12 SUMMARY
1133 This resolve authorizes the Department of Agriculture, Conservation and Forestry to
1144 convey to Wilhelmine Dennis Oakes, or the estate of Wilhelmine Dennis Oakes, any
1155 interest the State may have in property located in the Town of Richmond and shown as Lot
1166 67 on Map R6 of the Town of Richmond tax assessor maps.
