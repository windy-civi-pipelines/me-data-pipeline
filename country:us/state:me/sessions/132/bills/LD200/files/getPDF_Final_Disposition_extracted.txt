APPROVED CHAPTER
MARCH 25, 2025 8

STATE OF MAINE
_____
IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD
TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE
_____
S.P. 87 - L.D. 200
Standish
Sec. 1. Sebago Lake Road Crossing Bridge in Standish renamed. Resolved:
That the Department of Transportation shall designate Bridge 3907 on Chadbourne Road
in the Town of Standish, currently known as the Sebago Lake Road Crossing Bridge, the
Town of Standish Veterans Memorial Bridge.
