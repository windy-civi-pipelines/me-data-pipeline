3 INLAND FISHERIES AND WILDLIFE
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 857, L.D. 1322, “Resolve, to Establish
10 a Pilot Program to Enhance Youth Hunting Participation”
11 Amend the resolve in section 1 in the 4th line (page 1, line 4 in L.D.) by inserting after
12 the following: "program" the following: 'to run for calendar years 2026 and 2027'
13 Amend the resolve in section 4 in the first line (page 1, line 33 in L.D.) by striking out
14 the following: "annually" and inserting the following: 'by January 6, 2027 and by January
15 5, 2028'
16 Amend the resolve by striking out all of section 5 and inserting the following:
17 'Sec. 5. Legislation. Resolved: That in the report submitted pursuant to section 4
18 in 2028, the department may include suggested legislation based on department findings
19 during the 2-year pilot program under section 1. The joint standing committee of the
20 Legislature having jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters may submit
21 legislation based on the report to the Second Regular Session of the 133rd Legislature.'
22 Amend the resolve by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or
23 section number to read consecutively.
24 SUMMARY
2255 This amendment specifies that the 2-year pilot program established by the resolve to
2266 enhance youth hunting participation must take place in calendar years 2026 and 2027. The
2277 amendment also adds a date certain for when the Department of Inland Fisheries and
2288 Wildlife must submit the annual reports and proposed legislation based on the department's
2299 findings during the pilot program and gives the joint standing committee of the Legislature
3300 having jurisdiction over inland fisheries and wildlife matters the authority to report out
3311 legislation based on the report submitted in 2028.
