Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 282
An Act to Raise the Limit on the Total Number of Public Charter
Schools
Reference to the Committee on Education and Cultural Affairs suggested and ordered
printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative DRINKWATER of Milford.
Cosponsored by Senator BRADSTREET of Kennebec and
Representatives: COLLINS of Sidney, QUINT of Hodgdon.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 20-A MRSA §2405, sub-§10, as enacted by PL 2019, c. 307, §2, is amended
3 to read:
4 10. Limit on number of public charter schools. No more than 10 public charter
5 schools may operate at any time. Any time the cap is reached, the commissioner may not
6 accept further registrations from the commission or from local school boards and
7 collaboratives of local school boards cap increases by one.
8 SUMMARY
99 This bill requires that when the cap on the number of public charter schools allowed is
1100 reached, the cap increases by one.
