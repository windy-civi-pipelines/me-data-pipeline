5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 443, L.D. 1025, “An Act to Allow
10 Federal Civil Service to Count for Purposes of Maine Public Employees Retirement System
11 Benefits”
12 Amend the bill in section 1 in §17759-A in the headnote (page 1, line 3 in L.D.) by
13 striking out the following: "Federal employment service credit purchase" and inserting
14 the following: 'Nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government service'
15 Amend the bill in section 1 in §17759-A in subsection 1 in the 2nd line (page 1, line 5
16 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "an employee of the federal employment" and
17 inserting the following: 'a nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government'
18 Amend the bill in section 1 in §17759-A in subsection 1 in the 4th line (page 1, line 7
19 in L.D.) by striking out the following: "an employee of the federal employment" and
20 inserting the following: 'a nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government'
21 Amend the bill by inserting after section 1 the following:
22 'Sec. 2. 5 MRSA §18364 is enacted to read:
23 §18364. Nonmilitary public employee of the United States government service
24 1. Service before becoming member. A member who served as a nonmilitary public
25 employee of the United States Government before becoming a member is entitled under
26 this subsection to purchase service credit for the period of time that the member served as
27 a nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government by complying with the
28 applicable provisions of subsection 2. Service credit purchased under this section is limited
29 to 5 years.
30 2. Members qualified for credit at actuarial cost. A member qualified under
31 subsection 1 to purchase service credit at the cost set forth in this subsection is entitled to
32 service credit if the member pays an amount that, together with regular interest on that
33 amount, is the actuarial equivalent, at the effective date of the retirement benefit, of the
34 portion of the retirement benefit based on the additional creditable service.

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 443, L.D. 1025
1 A. Payment must be made before any retirement benefit becomes effective for that
2 member.
3 B. Payment may be made to the retirement system by a single direct payment or by
4 annual direct payments in accordance with section 18301, subsection 4.'
5 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
6 number to read consecutively.
7 SUMMARY
88 This amendment clarifies that a member of the Maine Public Employees Retirement
99 System with service as a nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government
1100 may purchase service credit. It also provides that a member of a participating local district
1111 with service as a nonmilitary public employee of the United States Government may also
1122 purchase service credit.
