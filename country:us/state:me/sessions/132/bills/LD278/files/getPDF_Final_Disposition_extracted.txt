1 L.D. 278
2 Date: (Filing No. S- )
3 TAXATION
5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 128, L.D. 278, “An Act to Eliminate the
10 Tobacco Products Tax on Certain Products That Contain Nicotine”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 36 MRSA §4401, sub-§9, as amended by PL 2023, c. 613, Pt. B, §5, is
14 further amended to read:
15 9. Tobacco products. "Tobacco products" means any products that are made from or
16 derived from tobacco, or that contain nicotine, whether natural or artificial, including, but
17 not limited to, cigars, including premium cigars; cheroots; stogies; electronic smoking
18 devices and liquids used in electronic smoking devices whether or not they contain
19 nicotine; periques, granulated, plug cut, crimp cut, ready rubbed and other smoking
20 tobacco; snuff; snuff flour; snus; cavendish; plug and twist tobacco; finecut and other
21 chewing tobaccos; shorts; refuse scraps, clippings, cuttings and sweepings of tobacco; and
22 other kinds and forms of tobacco, prepared in such manner as to be intended for human
23 consumption or as is likely to be consumed, whether smoked, heated, chewed, absorbed,
24 dissolved, inhaled or ingested by any other means. "Tobacco products" does not include
25 noncombustible oral pouches containing nicotine powder regardless of whether that
26 powder is derived from tobacco or artificially created.'
27 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
28 number to read consecutively.
29 SUMMARY
3300 This amendment replaces the bill. The amendment excludes nicotine pouches from the
3311 definition of "tobacco products" under the tobacco products tax laws.
