An Act to Allow a Municipality to Sell Tax-acquired Property in

ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative LEMELIN of Chelsea.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 36 MRSA ยง943-C, sub-ยง4-B is enacted to read:
3 4-B. Property exempt from sale process requirement. Notwithstanding the sale
