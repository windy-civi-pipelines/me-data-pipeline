5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 74, L.D. 138, “An Act to Exempt
10 Airports from Certain State Endangered and Threatened Species Habitat Protections”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act Regarding the Permitting of Projects Affecting Public-use Airports Under
13 the Natural Resources Protection Act and the Site Location of Development Laws'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 38 MRSA §480-E, sub-§19 is enacted to read:
17 19. Public-use airports. Notwithstanding any provision of this article to the contrary,
18 the department may not include in any permit issued pursuant to this article any conditions
19 that, as determined by the department, would decrease safety within the air operations area
20 at a public-use airport.
21 As used in this subsection, the following terms have the following meanings.
22 A. "Air operations area" has the same meaning as in 14 Code of Federal Regulations,
23 Section 153.3, except that, for a public-use airport that does not have an airport security
24 program, "air operations area" means those portions of the public-use airport that, as
25 determined by the department, are substantially similar to those portions of an airport
26 described in 14 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 153.3, including aircraft
27 movement areas, aircraft parking areas, loading ramps and safety areas for use by
28 aircraft and any adjacent areas not separated by adequate security systems, measures
29 or procedures.
30 B. "Airport security program" means a security program approved by the United States
31 Transportation Services Administration under 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section
32 1542.101.
33 C. "Public-use airport" has the same meaning as in 49 United States Code, Section
34 47102(22).

1 Sec. 2. 38 MRSA §485-A, sub-§1-E is enacted to read:
2 1-E. Public-use airports. Notwithstanding any provision of this article to the
3 contrary, the department may not include in any permit issued pursuant to this article any
4 conditions that, as determined by the department, would decrease safety within the air
5 operations area at a public-use airport.
6 As used in this subsection, the following terms have the following meanings.
7 A. "Air operations area" has the same meaning as in 14 Code of Federal Regulations,
8 Section 153.3, except that, for a public-use airport that does not have an airport security
9 program, "air operations area" means those portions of the public-use airport that, as
10 determined by the department, are substantially similar to those portions of an airport
11 described in 14 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 153.3, including aircraft
12 movement areas, aircraft parking areas, loading ramps and safety areas for use by
13 aircraft and any adjacent areas not separated by adequate security systems, measures
14 or procedures.
15 B. "Airport security program" means a security program approved by the United States
16 Transportation Services Administration under 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section
17 1542.101.
18 C. "Public-use airport" has the same meaning as in 49 United States Code, Section
19 47102(22).'
20 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
21 number to read consecutively.
22 SUMMARY
2233 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It amends the Natural
2244 Resources Protection Act and the site location of development laws to provide that the
2255 Department of Environmental Protection may not include in any permit issued pursuant to
2266 either article any conditions that, as determined by the department, would decrease safety
2277 within the air operations area at a public-use airport. "Public-use airport" is defined to have
2288 the same meaning as in 49 United States Code, Section 47102(22), "airport security
2299 program" means a security program approved by the United States Transportation Services
3300 Administration under 49 Code of Federal Regulations, Section 1542.101 and "air
3311 operations area" is defined to have the same meaning as in 14 Code of Federal Regulations,
3322 Section 153.3, except as otherwise specified for a public-use airport that does not have an
3333 airport security program.
