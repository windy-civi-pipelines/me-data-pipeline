Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 448
An Act Regarding the Establishment of Fees and Fee Caps for
Dental Provider Licensing and Permits
Submitted by the Department of Professional and Financial Regulation pursuant to Joint
Rule 204.
Reference to the Committee on Health Coverage, Insurance and Financial Services
suggested and ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative MASTRACCIO of Sanford.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 32 MRSA §18309 is enacted to read:
3 §18309. Fees
4 1. Fee establishment. The director may establish by rule fees for purposes authorized
5 under this chapter in amounts that are reasonable and necessary for their respective
6 purposes, except that:
7 A. The fee for any one purpose may not exceed $550 unless another fee is authorized
8 by paragraph B or C;
9 B. The fee for an initial license or a license renewal under section 18342 or a permit
10 under section 18379 may not exceed $1,000; and
11 C. The fee for an initial license or a license renewal under section 18345 may not
12 exceed $200.
13 2. Rules. Rules adopted pursuant to subsection 1 are routine technical rules pursuant
14 to Title 5, chapter 375, subchapter 2-A.
15 SUMMARY
1166 Public Law 2023, chapter 17 inadvertently repealed fee caps for dental provider
1177 licensing and permit fees and the authorizing authority to establish those fees by rule. This
1188 bill reinstates the fee caps and authority but changes the authority from the Department of
1199 Professional and Financial Regulation, Board of Dental Practice to the Director of the
2200 Office of Professional and Occupational Regulation within the department.
