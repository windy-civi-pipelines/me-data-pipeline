Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST SPECIAL SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 1385
H.P. 907 House of Representatives, April 1, 2025
in Awarding Transportation Grants
Reference to the Committee on Housing and Economic Development suggested and
ordered printed.
ROBERT B. HUNT
Clerk
Presented by Representative GERE of Kennebunkport.
Cosponsored by Senator BENNETT of Oxford and
Representatives: ANKELES of Brunswick, CRAFTS of Newcastle, DOUDERA of Camden,
Speaker FECTEAU of Biddeford, GOLEK of Harpswell, MINGO of Calais, YUSUF of
Portland, Senator: CURRY of Waldo.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 23 MRSA §76 is enacted to read:
3 §76. Awarding of discretionary grants
4 The department shall consider a municipality's past actions and future plans toward
5 meeting regional housing production goals as described in Title 5, section 13056,
6 subsection 9 when considering the award of discretionary grants.
7 SUMMARY
88 This bill requires the Department of Transportation to consider a municipality's past
99 actions and future plans toward meeting regional housing production goals when
1100 considering the award of discretionary grants.
