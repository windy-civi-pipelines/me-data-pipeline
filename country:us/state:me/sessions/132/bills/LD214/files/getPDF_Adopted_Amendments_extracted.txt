5 STATE OF MAINE
6 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 137, L.D. 214, “An Act to Create a For-
10 hire Charter Boat Operator License and to Require Individuals Recreationally Fishing for
11 Halibut in Coastal Waters to Register with the Saltwater Recreational Fishing Registry”
12 Amend the bill in section 5 in §6313 by striking out all of subsection 3 (page 2, lines 3
13 to 5 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
14 '3. Agent fees. A fee may not be charged for a for-hire charter boat operator's license
15 under this section. A person seeking a license through an agent may be charged an agent
16 fee of $2. A person seeking a license through a publicly accessible online electronic system
17 for licensing may be charged an agent fee of $1. A person seeking a license at an office of
18 the department from a department employee may be charged an agent fee of $1.'
19 Amend the bill in section 5 in §6313 in subsection 4 by striking out all of paragraph A
20 (page 2, lines 9 and 10 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
21 'A. Possesses a valid United States merchant marine document issued by the United
22 States Coast Guard for an operator of uninspected passenger vessel or master or mate
23 captain's license; or'
24 Amend the bill by inserting after section 5 the following:
25 'Sec. 6. Effective date. This Act takes effect January 1, 2026.'
26 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
27 number to read consecutively.
28 SUMMARY
2299 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill.
30 1. It removes the license fee for a for-hire charter boat operator's license from the bill
31 and replaces it with an agent fee.
32 2. It corrects language referencing the United States Coast Guard.
