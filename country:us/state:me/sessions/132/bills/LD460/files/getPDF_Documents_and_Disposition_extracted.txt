Section 1:
132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 460
S.P. 193 In Senate, February 11, 2025
Memorial Bridge
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on February 5, 2025. Referred to the Committee
on Transportation pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator LIBBY of Cumberland.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Sec. 1. Bridge in Newfield renamed. Resolved: That the Department of
2 Transportation shall designate Bridge 5313 on Bridge Street, which crosses the Little
3 Ossipee River in the Town of Newfield and is currently known as the Dam's Mills Bridge,
4 the Dam's Mills Veterans Memorial Bridge.
5 SUMMARY
66 This resolve directs the Department of Transportation to designate Bridge 5313 on
77 Bridge Street in the Town of Newfield the Dam's Mills Veterans Memorial Bridge.
