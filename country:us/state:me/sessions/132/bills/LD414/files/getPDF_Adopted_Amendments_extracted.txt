9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 268, L.D. 414, “An Act to Prohibit
10 Deceptive Pricing”
11 Amend the bill by striking out the title and substituting the following:
12 'An Act to Prohibit Deceptive Pricing in the Rental of Short-term Lodging and in the
13 Sale of Tickets'
14 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
15 following:
16 'Sec. 1. 10 MRSA c. 206-D is enacted to read:
17 CHAPTER 206-D
18 PRICE TRANSPARENCY FOR SHORT-TERM LODGING RENTALS AND
19 TICKET SALES
20 §1250-P. Price transparency for short-term lodging rentals and ticket sales
21 1. Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise indicates, the
22 following terms have the following meanings.
23 A. "Mandatory fee" means a fee or charge required as part of an advertised price,
24 including, but not limited to, a resort fee, service charge or convenience fee.
25 "Mandatory fee" does not include:
26 (1) Taxes or fees imposed by a government or quasi-governmental entity or
27 assessment fees imposed on the transaction by a government-created special
28 district or program;
29 (2) Shipping fees or charges that reasonably reflect the amount of the charges a
30 business incurs to send physical goods to consumers through the mail, including
31 private mail services; or
Page 1 - 132LR1086(02)
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 268, L.D. 414
1 (3) Amounts charged for ancillary goods or services offered to a consumer as part
2 of the same transaction.
3 B. "Short-term lodging" means an accommodation for transient occupancy in a hotel,
4 motel, vacation rental or similar establishment, typically for under 30 days.
5 C. "Ticket" has the same meaning as in Title 8, section 1301, subsection 1, paragraph
6 C.
7 2. Total price disclosure. A person advertising or offering for rent or renting a short-
8 term lodging or a person advertising or offering for sale or selling a ticket:
9 A. Shall clearly and conspicuously disclose the total price of the short-term lodging or
10 ticket, including any mandatory fee, whenever the price is advertised or displayed;
11 B. Shall display the total price of the short-term lodging or ticket more prominently
12 than other pricing information in all advertisements, offers or displays; and
13 C. May not misrepresent any fee or charge associated with a short-term lodging or
14 ticket in any advertisement, offer or display.
15 The requirements of this subsection do not apply to a provider of short-term lodging or a
16 person advertising or offering for sale or selling a ticket that complies with the provisions
17 of 16 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 464.
18 3. Enforcement. The Attorney General shall enforce this section.
19 4. Violation. A person that violates this section commits an unfair and deceptive act
20 and a violation of Title 5, chapter 10.'
21 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
22 number to read consecutively.
23 SUMMARY
2244 This amendment replaces the bill and changes the title. It prohibits a person that is
2255 renting short-term lodging or selling an entertainment event ticket from advertising,
2266 displaying or offering a price that does not include all mandatory fees or charges other than
2277 taxes or fees imposed by a government, shipping charges and charges for ancillary goods
2288 or services. The amendment provides that sellers of short-term lodging or tickets in
2299 compliance with 16 Code of Federal Regulations, Part 464, the Federal Trade Commission
3300 final rule relating to unfair or deceptive fees, are exempt from this prohibition.
