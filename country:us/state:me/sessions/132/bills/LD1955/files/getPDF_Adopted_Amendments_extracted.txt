9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 763, L.D. 1955, “An Act to Increase
10 Child Care Affordability and Early Childhood Educator Stability”
11 Amend the bill by inserting before section 1 the following:
12 'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §3731-A, sub-§3 is enacted to read:
13 3. Nonlapsing funds. Any unexpended funds appropriated or allocated by the
14 Legislature or accepted by the commissioner for the purposes of the Maine Child Care
15 Affordability Program may not lapse and must be carried forward in the same amount for
16 the next fiscal year. The commissioner may accept donations or grants for the Maine Child
17 Care Affordability Program from any source.'
18 Amend the bill in section 1 in §3731-B in the headnote (page 1, line 3 in L.D.) by
19 inserting after the following: "Program Child Care" the following: 'Employment'
20 Amend the bill in section 1 in §3731-B in the first indented paragraph in the first line
21 (page 1, line 4 in L.D.) by inserting after the following: "Program Child Care" the
22 following: 'Employment'
23 Amend the bill in section 1 in §3731-B by inserting after subsection 4 the following:
24 '5. Nonlapsing funds. Any unexpended funds appropriated or allocated by the
25 Legislature or accepted by the commissioner for the purposes of the award may not lapse
26 and must be carried forward in the same amount for the next fiscal year. The commissioner
27 may accept donations or grants for the award from any source.'
28 Amend the bill in section 3 in §3737-B by striking out all of subsection 2 (page 2, lines
29 6 to 10 in L.D.) and inserting the following:
30 '2. Report. Beginning on January 15, 2026, and annually thereafter, the department
31 shall include in its annual report pursuant to section 3740-A the number of child care
32 professionals participating in the program, expenditures under the program and a
33 description of progress in implementing the tiered system required under subsection 4.'
34 Amend the bill in section 3 in §3737-B by inserting after subsection 4 the following:

16 SUMMARY
1177 This amendment makes the following changes to the bill.
1188 1. It clarifies that funds to support the programs in the bill are nonlapsing funds.
1199 2. It changes the name of the Maine Child Care Affordability Program Child Care
2200 Award to the Maine Child Care Affordability Program Child Care Employment Award.
2211 3. It requires the report on the number of child care professionals participating in the
2222 Salary Sustainability Program for Child Care Professionals to be included in the existing
2233 annual report on child care subsidies beginning with the January 15, 2026 report, rather
2244 than a separate report.
