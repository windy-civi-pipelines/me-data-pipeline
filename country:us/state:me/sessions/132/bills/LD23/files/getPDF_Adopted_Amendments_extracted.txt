7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 37, L.D. 23, “An Act to Extend the
10 Period of Time a Dentist May Operate the Practice of a Deceased or Incapacitated Dentist”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in subsection 5 in the 5th line (page 1, line 20 in L.D.) by
12 striking out the following: "36" and inserting the following: '48'
13 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
14 number to read consecutively.
15 SUMMARY
1166 This amendment increases the amount of time allowed in the bill for a legal guardian
1177 or personal representative of a deceased or incapacitated dentist to contract with another
1188 dentist to continue the operation of the practice from 36 months to 48 months.
