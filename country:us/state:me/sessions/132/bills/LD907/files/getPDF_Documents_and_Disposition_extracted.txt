132nd MAINE LEGISLATURE
FIRST REGULAR SESSION-2025
Legislative Document No. 907
S.P. 396 In Senate, March 11, 2025
for Persons with Permanent Disabilities
Received by the Secretary of the Senate on March 5, 2025. Referred to the Committee on
Housing and Economic Development pursuant to Joint Rule 308.2 and ordered printed.
DAREK M. GRANT
Secretary of the Senate
Presented by Senator LIBBY of Cumberland.
Cosponsored by Senators: MARTIN of Oxford, NANGLE of Cumberland, Representative:
WADSWORTH of Hiram.
Printed on recycled paper
1 Be it enacted by the People of the State of Maine as follows:
2 Sec. 1. 30-A MRSA §4353, sub-§4-A, ¶B, as enacted by PL 2009, c. 342, §1, is
3 amended to read:
4 B. If authorized by the zoning ordinance establishing the board, the board may grant
5 a variance to an owner of a dwelling who resides in the dwelling and who is a person
6 with a permanent disability for the construction of a place of storage and parking for a
7 noncommercial vehicle owned by that person and no other purpose. The width and
8 length of the structure may not be larger than 2 times the width and length of the
9 noncommercial vehicle. The owner shall submit proposed plans for the structure with
10 the request for the variance pursuant to this paragraph to the board.
11 The person with the permanent disability shall prove by a preponderance of the
12 evidence that the person's disability is permanent.
13 For purposes of this paragraph, "noncommercial vehicle" means a motor vehicle as
14 defined in Title 29‑A, section 101, subsection 42 with a gross vehicle weight of no
15 more than 6,000 pounds, bearing a disability registration plate issued pursuant to Title
16 29‑A, section 521 and owned by the person with the permanent disability.
17 SUMMARY
1188 This bill amends the municipal land use regulation law governing zoning adjustments
1199 by removing restrictions on the type of vehicle a person with a permanent disability may
2200 own in order for that person to obtain a zoning ordinance variance to construct a structure
2211 for the storage and parking of the vehicle. It also removes restrictions on the size of the
2222 structure.
