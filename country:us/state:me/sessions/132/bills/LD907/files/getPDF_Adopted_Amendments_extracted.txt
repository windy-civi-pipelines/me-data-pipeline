5 STATE OF MAINE
6 SENATE
7 132ND LEGISLATURE
8 FIRST SPECIAL SESSION
9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to S.P. 396, L.D. 907, “An Act to Amend the
10 Law Governing Zoning Ordinance Variances for Persons with Permanent Disabilities”
11 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph B in the 2nd line (page 1, line 5 in L.D.) by
12 inserting after the following: "is" the following: 'responsible to transport'
13 Amend the bill in section 1 in paragraph B in the 4th line (page 1, line 7 in L.D.) by
14 striking out the following: "owned by that person and no other purpose" and inserting the
15 following: 'owned by that person and no other purpose used to transport a person with a
16 permanent disability'
17 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
18 number to read consecutively.
19 SUMMARY
2200 This amendment clarifies the scope of a zoning ordinance variance allowed by the bill
2211 to allow a variance for the construction of a place of storage and parking for a vehicle used
2222 to transport a person with a permanent disability.
