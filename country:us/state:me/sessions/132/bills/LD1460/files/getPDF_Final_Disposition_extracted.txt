9 COMMITTEE AMENDMENT “ ” to H.P. 952, L.D. 1460, “An Act to Require
10 Parents to Be Informed of Hospitals' Safe Sleep Rules”
11 Amend the bill by striking out everything after the enacting clause and inserting the
12 following:
13 'Sec. 1. 22 MRSA §1711-H is enacted to read:
14 §1711-H. Hospital safe sleep rule for infants; parental behavior
15 A hospital licensed pursuant to chapter 405 that has a safe sleep rule for infants shall
16 inform the parent or guardian of an infant who is an inpatient at the hospital of the safe
17 sleep rule for infants, and the parent or guardian shall sign a form provided by the hospital
18 that contains the text of the safe sleep rule for infants acknowledging that the parent or
19 guardian knows the safe sleep rule for infants. If the parent or guardian refuses to sign the
20 form, the hospital shall document the refusal in the medical record prior to discharge from
21 the hospital. For purposes of this section, "safe sleep rule for infants" means a set of
22 evidence-based practices a hospital requires to be employed in order to reduce the rate of
23 infants' unsafe sleep injuries and sudden unexpected infant death related to unsafe sleep,
24 including accidental suffocation and strangulation in bed and sudden infant death
25 syndrome.'
26 Amend the bill by relettering or renumbering any nonconsecutive Part letter or section
27 number to read consecutively.
28 SUMMARY
2299 This amendment, which is the minority report of the committee, makes the following
3300 changes to the bill.
31 1. It clarifies that the safe sleep rule relates to infants rather than children.
32 2. It clarifies that a guardian, in addition to a parent, may be the person responsible for
33 the infant.
